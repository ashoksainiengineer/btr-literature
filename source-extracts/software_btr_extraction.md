**Extracted Content**:

#### A. Ruling Planets — Event Timing by Movement
- Events within 24 hours: Ascendant movement among RPs determines timing
- Events within a month: Moon movement among RPs determines the day
- Events after a month: Sun determines month, Moon determines day
- Events requiring a year+: Jupiter or Dasha sequence determines timing
- Day Lord determined from sunset to sunset, NOT midnight to midnight
- Retrograde RPs considered "weak" — may not produce immediate results

#### B. Significators — 6-Level Order of Importance
1. Planets in stars of house occupants (strongest)
2. Planets occupying the house
3. Planets in stars of house owner
4. House owner (lord) itself
5. Planets associated with above significators
6. Planets aspecting the house (weakest)
- First available level is the most important

#### C. Cusp Controllers (Sign-Star-Sub Hierarchy)
- Sign Lord: Overall nature (2-hour stability)
- Star Lord: Event type definition (30-40 min stability)
- Sub Lord: Yes/No authority (5-12 min stability)
- Star Lord defines event; Sub Lord allows or denies it

#### D. Dasha Degrees Concept
- 120 Vimshottari years = 120 degrees of spatial movement
- Add age in years to planet's birth longitude → Dasha Degree position
- Star/Sub of Dasha Degree position explains current dasha expression
- Extended: Allocate proportional degrees per planet's dasha years

#### E. Guardian Lords Concept
- Each Nakshatra's Dasha falls under a Guardian Lord (Sign Lord)
- Guardian Lord stays constant for all 3 Dashas within a sign
- Sequence of Guardian Lords = sequence of Moon's sign progression
- Guardian Lord determines life theme/status during that period

#### F. Co-Owners of Cusps (SKP)
- Placidus houses create unequal spans with multiple Sign Lords
- Each cusp has 3-5 Sign-Star pairs (Co-Owners)
- Cusps 01, 03, 07, 10, 11 have 4 Co-Owners
- Cusps 02, 05, 09 have 5 Co-Owners
- Cusps 04, 06, 08, 12 have 3 Co-Owners
- Majority Co-Owner may override primary House Lord

#### G. Active Cusps for Transit Analysis
- Current cusps (Active Cusps) occupy natal houses dynamically
- Active Cusp-1 (current Ascendant) shows current mental framework
- Star-Lord of Active Cusp projects events; Sub-Lord indicates materialization
- Natal Cusp Controllers interact with Active Cusps at event dates
