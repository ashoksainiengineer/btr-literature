## 2. PP Calculation Formulas

```
## Rule: Pranapada Rasi (Sign) Calculation — Classical Method
**Source:** Astro Secrets & KP Part IV, Chunk 4 (final), page 164
**URL:** https://vedangajyotish.wordpress.com/2011/08/17/birth-time-rectification-using-pranapada-lagna/
**Rule:** Multiply birth ghatis by 4. Divide birth vighatis by 15. Add both results. Divide total by 12. The remainder indicates the Pranapada Rasi (counted from the Sun's sign). The remainder obtained in division by 15 when doubled shows the mean pranapada.

**Formula:**
```
PP_Rasi_index = ((Ghatis × 4) + (Vighatis ÷ 15)) mod 12
PP_Sign = Sign_number_of_Sun + PP_Rasi_index (wrapping at 12)
Mean_PP = (Vighatis_remainder × 2)  [in degrees/minutes]
```

**Example:** Birth at 33.88 ghatis (Vighatis = 0) → (33.88 × 4) ÷ 12 = 135.52 ÷ 12 = remainder 3 → PP in 3rd from Sun's sign.
---

## Rule: Pranapada Lagna Degree Calculation — Classical Method
**Source:** Astro Secrets & KP Part IV, Chunk 4 (final), pages 164-165
**URL:** https://vedangajyotish.wordpress.com/2011/08/17/birth-time-rectification-using-pranapada-lagna/
**Rule:** Multiply the remainder noted in the PP Rasi calculation by 2 and add the longitude of Sun in its sign (ignoring the rasi proper). This gives the actual degree of PP within its sign. Special rule: If Sun is in a Chara (movable) sign, take degrees from that sign directly. If Sun is in a Sthira (fixed) sign, take the 9th sign from Sun. If Sun is in a Dwiswabhava (dual) sign, take the 5th sign from Sun.

**Formula:**
```
PP_degrees = ((Ghatis_remainder × 2) + Sun_longitude_in_sign) mod 30
PP_final_sign = 
  if Sun_sign is Chara → Sun_sign (count directly)
  if Sun_sign is Sthira → Sun_sign + 9 (wrap at 12)
  if Sun_sign is Dwiswabhava → Sun_sign + 5 (wrap at 12)
```

## 7. Classical Pranapada Calculation (KP & BPHS)

```
## Rule: Classical Pranapada — Detailed Computation (KP System)
**Source:** Astro Secrets & KP Part IV, Chunk 4 (final)
**Rule:** 

### Step 1: Convert birth time to Ghatis and Vighatis
```
Ghatis = (Birth_IST_hrs - Sunrise_IST_hrs) × 2.5
Vighatis = fractional part × 60
```

### Step 2: Compute PP Index
```
PP_Index = (Ghatis × 4 + Vighatis ÷ 15)  [integer portion]
PP_Rasi_Remainder = PP_Index mod 12
```

### Step 3: Determine PP Sign
```
PP_Rasi_Number = Sun_Sign_Number + PP_Rasi_Remainder
if PP_Rasi_Number > 12: PP_Rasi_Number -= 12
```

### Step 4: Determine PP Degree
```
PP_Degree_Remainder = (Vighatis ÷ 15) fractional part
PP_Degree = (PP_Degree_Remainder × 2) + Sun_Longitude_in_Sign
if PP_Degree > 30: PP_Degree -= 30
```

### Step 5: Apply Chara/Sthira/Dwiswabhava Correction
- If Sun in Chara (Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn): PP sign as computed above
- If Sun in Sthira (Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius): PP sign = computed + 9 (wrap)
- If Sun in Dwiswabhava (Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces): PP sign = computed + 5 (wrap)

### Step 6: Compute PP Longitude
```
PP_Longitude = PP_Sign_Number × 30 + PP_Degree
```

**Worked Example (from KP Part IV):**
- Birth: ~12:30 PM IST, Sunrise ~6:00 AM IST
- Ghatis from sunrise = 6.5 hrs × 2.5 = 16.25 ghatis
- Ghatis × 4 = 65
- Vighatis = 15 (0.25 × 60)
- Vighatis ÷ 15 = 1
- PP_Index = 65 + 1 = 66
- PP_Rasi_Remainder = 66 mod 12 = 6
- If Sun in Cancer (4th sign): PP = 4 + 6 = 10 (Capricorn)
---

## Rule: BPHS Pranapada Calculation — 4-Step Classical Method
**Source:** Birth Time Rectification — BPHS View
**URL:** https://pdfcoffee.com/birth-time-rectification-bphs-view-pdf-free.html
**Rule:** The classical BPHS method for Pranapada calculation proceeds in four steps:

### Step 1 — Compute PP Rasi (Sign)
```
PP_Index = (Ghatis × 4) + floor(Pala ÷ 15)
PP_Rasi_Remainder = PP_Index mod 12
```
The remainder (1-12) is the PP sign number (counted from a reference point).

### Step 2 — Compute Mean PP Degree
```
PP_Mean_Deg = (Pala ÷ 15_remainder) × 2
```
This gives the degree portion of the Mean (Madhyam) PP.

### Step 3 — Compute Explicit (Spasht) PP
Apply Sun's sign-type correction:
- **Chara (Movable)** — Add Sun's longitude directly to Mean PP
- **Sthira (Fixed)** — Add 9th sign from Sun's longitude to Mean PP
- **Dwiswabhava (Dual)** — Add 5th sign from Sun's longitude to Mean PP

### Step 4 — Verification
```
If Spasht_PP_degree ≈ Ascendant_degree → birth time is correct
```

| Step | Input | Operation | Output |
|------|-------|-----------|--------|
| 1 | Ghatis, Pala | (G×4)+(P÷15) mod 12 | PP Sign |
| 2 | Pala remainder | Remainder × 2 | PP Mean Degree |
| 3 | Mean PP + Sun (corrected) | Add with sign-type rule | Explicit PP |
| 4 | Explicit PP vs Ascendant | Compare degrees | Verification |
