## 1. Philosophical Basis

**Source:** Astrogle KP BTR Article
**URL:** https://www.astrogle.com/astrology/birth-time-rectification-through-k-p-astrology.html
**Rule:** According to classical texts of astrology, the birth of a native happens due to:
- Past Karma (Purva Punya — good deeds in past life)
- The father (who is the giver of the Beeja/seed)
- The mother is only a bearer of the child (Kshetra/field)

The 9th house of a birth chart shows the quality of antecedents of a native. Since every human being is born due to their father (even modern medical fertilization requires at least one donor sperm cell), every birth must invariably be linked to the house of father — that is, the 9th cusp.

**Therefore:** If the rectified time of birth is truly correct, the Ascendant/Lagna must be linked with the 9th cusp.

## Rule: The Genetic Lock — Ascendant Connection to 9th House (Father)

**Source:** StarSutra BTR — Phase 2: Biological Filtration (The Genetic Lock)
**URL:** https://www.starsutra.in/blog/birth-time-rectification-starsutras-exact-timing

**Rule:** Your Ascendant Star-Lord and Sub-Lord must show a connection to your 9th House (which represents your father). This is a genetic signature — you inherited your moment of birth from the circumstances of your parentage. If a candidate time fails this test, it is eliminated immediately, no matter how good it looks otherwise.

**KP Application:** For every candidate birth time, check:
1. The Ascendant Star-Lord (ruler of the nakshatra the Ascendant falls in)
2. The Ascendant Sub-Lord (ruler of the sub-division the Ascendant falls in)
3. Both must have a signification connection to the 9th House (father)
4. Connection means: owning the 9th, occupying the 9th, or being in the star of a planet connected to the 9th

**Purpose:** Acts as a biological/genetic first-pass filter before biographical event testing begins. Eliminates candidate times that are astrologically impossible regardless of how well they match life events.

**Houses/Planets:** Ascendant Star-Lord, Ascendant Sub-Lord, 9th House (father), 1st House (self)

**Cascade if Failed:** Candidate time is eliminated — no further testing needed.

## Rationale

The Rule of Origin is grounded in the KP principle that the Ascendant — the most sensitive point in the chart — carries the genetic imprint of one's lineage. The 9th House represents the father, fortune, and genetic inheritance. For the Ascendant to be correct, its stellar rulers must resonate with this inheritance. A candidate time whose Ascendant rulers have no connection to the 9th House cannot be the correct birth moment because it would imply a birth disconnected from its own genetic origin.
