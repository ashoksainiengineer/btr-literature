## Overview

Prana Dasha is the 4th sub-level of the Vimshottari Dasha system (Dasha → Bhukti → Antara → Prana → ...). Prana Dasha periods can be as short as a few days, making them the most precise dasha level for timing verification. StarSutra uses Prana Dasha as a **microscope after a magnifying glass** — narrowing candidate birth times from minutes to seconds.

## Rule: Prana Dasha Fine-Tuning (Phase 4)

**Source:** StarSutra BTR — Phase 4: Prana Dasha Fine-Tuning
**URL:** https://www.starsutra.in/blog/birth-time-rectification-starsutras-exact-timing

**Rule:** Once candidates have been narrowed to a small window through Biological Filtration (Phase 2) and Biographical Validation (Phase 3), take the **most precisely known event** in the native's biography — often a parent's exact date of passing — and align it to the **Prana Dasha level**.

Prana Dasha periods can be as short as a few days. By aligning a known event date to the Prana Dasha level, the birth time can be pinpointed to within **seconds**.

**KP Application:**
1. From the narrowed candidate pool, compute Prana Dasha for each candidate
2. Identify the most precisely datable life event (preferably with exact date known)
3. Check which candidate's Prana Dasha period at that event date matches the event's nature
4. The candidate whose Prana Dasha aligns most precisely with the event is selected
5. This narrows the birth time window from minutes to seconds

**Dasha Level Hierarchy for BTR:**
| Level | Name | Duration | Precision |
|-------|------|----------|-----------|
| 1st | Dasha (Mahadasa) | Years | Coarse |
| 2nd | Bhukti (Antardasha) | Months | Medium |
| 3rd | Antara (Sub-level) | Weeks | Fine |
| **4th** | **Prana (Sub-sub-level)** | **Days** | **Ultra-fine** |

**Houses/Planets:** Depends on the event being used (e.g., father's death → 9th House, 4th/8th/12th; mother's death → 4th House, 11th/3rd/7th)

**Data Requirement:** Requires at least one event with exact date (preferably day-precision) for reliable Prana-level alignment.

## Rule: Phase 4 — Prana Dasha as Microscopic Verification

**Source:** StarSutra BTR — Phase 4: Prana Dasha Fine-Tuning
**URL:** https://www.starsutra.in/blog/birth-time-rectification-starsutras-exact-timing

**Rule:** "This is like using a microscope after a magnifying glass. Prana Dasha periods can be as short as a few days, so aligning one to a known event date lets us pinpoint your birth time to within **seconds**."

**KP Application:** Prana Dasha verification typically uses the parent's death date as the anchor event (if precisely known) because death events are often documented to the exact day. However, any event with day-level precision (marriage date, surgery date, accident date) can serve as the anchor.

**Strengths:**
- Highest temporal resolution in the Dasha system
- Eliminates candidate times that differ by seconds
- Cross-validates with Sub-Sub-Lord analysis (Phase 5)

**Limitations:**
- Requires a precisely datable event (day-level precision)
- Only applicable after coarser filters (Phases 1-3) have narrowed the candidate pool
