### House Names & Significations (Adhyaya I, Sloka 49-52)

**1st House (Lagna):** Kalpa Udaya (rising), Adya (first), Thanu (body), Janma (birth), Vilagni, Hora.

**2nd House:** Vak (speech), Artha (wealth), Bhukthi (food), Nayam (eye), Sva (property), Kutumbi (family).

**3rd House:** Duschikya, Vikrama (prowess), Sahodara (brother), Virya (heroism), Dhairya (firmness), Katna (ear).

**4th House:** Patala, Vriddhi, Hlukhi, Ksheti (land), Matri (mother), Vidya (learning), Yana (vehicle), Ambu (water), Geha (house), Sukha (happiness), Bandhu (relations), Chathushtaya.

**7th House:** Jamithra, Kama (love), Gamani (cohabitation/travel), Kalathrasampat (spouse's wealth), Dyuna, Asta, Sapthama.

**8th House:** Randhra, Ayus (life), Ashta, Rana (battle), Mrityu (death), Vinasa.

**9th House:** Dharma (virtue), Guru (father), Subha (auspicious things), Tapas (penance), Nava (ninth), Bhagya (fortune).

**10th House:** Vyapara (employment), Meshurana, Madhya (zenith), Mana (honour), Gnatia (knowledge), Rajaspadada (sovereignty), Karma (work).

**11th House:** Ekadosa (eleventh), Upanthya (penultimate), Bhaya, Aya (income), Labha (gain).

**12th House:** Ripha, Vyaya (expenditure), Dwadasaka (twelfth), Anthyabha (last house).

### House Effects for Exalted/Debilitated Planets (Adhyaya VIII, Sloka 100-114)

- **Uchcha Phalam (Sloka 100-103):** Planets in exaltation in a house produce exceptionally good effects for the matters of that house.
- **Swashetra Phalam (Sloka 105-106):** Planets in own house produce steady, consistent results.
- **Moolatrikona Phalam (Sloka 104):** Planets in Moolatrikona produce very beneficial results.
- **Mitrakshetra Phalam (Sloka 107-111):** Planets in friendly sign produce moderately good results.
- **Satrukshetra Phalam (Sloka 112-113):** Planets in inimical sign produce disturbed results.
- **Neecha Rasi Phalam (Sloka 114):** Planets in depression produce negative results unless cancelled.

## Rule: BSP No. 27 — 4th House from Saturn at Age 48

**Rule:** At the age of 48, the **4th house from Saturn** in the D60 (Shashtiamsha) chart is activated. This activation manifests as results related to the 4th house — property, home, vehicles, mother, emotional stability, real estate, or domestic affairs.

**Mechanism:**
1. Locate Saturn's position in the D60 chart
2. Count 4 houses (rasi) from Saturn's position
3. At age 48 (or cycle-equivalents: 60, 72, 84, 96, 108), the 4th house from Saturn is activated
4. Verify that the native experienced an event matching the 4th house significations at that age

**Property/Home Application:**
Since age 48 is a key age for property and domestic stability, this rule can verify:
- Purchase or sale of property/home
- Construction or renovation of residence
- Vehicle purchase or major transport change
- Events related to mother or maternal figures
- Settling into a permanent residence
- Real estate investment or inheritance

**Example Scenarios:**
- Saturn in 2nd house D60 → 4th from Saturn = 5th house → creative property investments at age 48
- Saturn in 7th house D60 → 4th from Saturn = 10th house → career-based relocation at age 48
- Saturn in 10th house D60 → 4th from Saturn = 1st house → new domestic phase at age 48

**BTR Verification:**
If the recorded birth time yields a D60 chart where the 4th-from-Saturn activation at age 48 does NOT align with the native's known property/home events at that age, the birth time may be incorrect. Adjust until BSP Rule 27 is satisfied.

The same house number means different things in different divisional charts. The area of life seen in the divisional chart colors the house meaning:

Learning is seen from D-24, pleasures and comforts from D-16, house and immovable property from D-4 and parents from D-12. So the 4th houses in D-24, D-16, D-4 and D-12 show education, vehicle, house and mother (respectively)."

## 3. Multiple House Reference Systems (Pages 82-86)

PVR defines 9 different reference points for finding houses, each serving a different analytical purpose:

### 3.1 Lagna — True Self (Default)
> "If no reference is mentioned when houses are listed, it means lagna — the default reference — was used. Lagna shows true self."
>
> "If we are trying to understand someone's status in society, lagna may not be the correct reference. Status does not relate to 'true self'. It is a part of the illusion of this world."
>
> "However, if we are trying to understand someone's intentions in doing something or someone's knowledge or someone's persistence, it relates to 'true self'. So they are seen from the houses counted from lagna."

### 3.2 Chandra Lagna (Moon Lagna) — Mind
> "Chandra lagna means Moon taken as a reference. Because Moon is the significator of mind, these houses show things from the perspective of mind."
>
> "When we judge how happy one is, how ambitious one is and how one views one's career, the role of mind is paramount."

### 3.3 Ravi Lagna (Sun Lagna) — Soul
> "Ravi lagna means Sun taken as a reference. Because Sun is the significator of soul, these houses show things from the perspective of soul. For things related to physical vitality also, Sun is an important reference."

### 3.4 Arudha Lagna (AL) — World's Perception / Status
> "Arudha lagna shows how a native is perceived in the world. It also shows the status of a native."
>
> "A planet in the 10th house from lagna may give some important developments in one's profession. A planet in the 10th house from AL may give some important developments in one's professional status."

### 3.5 Paaka Lagna (Lagna Lord) — Physical Self
> "Paaka lagna is nothing but lagna lord taken as a reference. Lagna lord represents the physical self of a native."
>
> "Lagna shows the concept of self and it deals with one's true personality. The physical existence of the person is different from this conceptual self."
>
> **Example**: "Scholarship is a property of one's true personality and conceptual self. So 5th from lagna shows scholarship. Memory is a property of one's self that physically exists. So 5th house from paaka lagna shows memory. Success in competition is related to the illusions and perceptions of the world — the most appropriate reference is arudha lagna. The 5th house from arudha lagna shows success in competition."

### 3.6 Karakamsa — Soul's Liberation
> "Atma karaka stands for the soul of the person. The rasi occupied by atma karaka in navamsa is called Karakamsa."
>
> "The 12th house from Karakamsa shows the liberation of the soul and the situation of Ketu there is conducive to moksha."

### 3.7 Ghati Lagna (GL) — Power & Authority
> "Ghati lagna shows self, from the point of view of power, authority and fame. When we analyze promotions in career or political power of politicians, this reference is very important."

### 3.8 Hora Lagna (HL) — Wealth
> "Hora lagna shows self, from the point of view of wealth. This reference is important when analyzing one's wealth."

### 3.9 Graha Lagnas (Planetary References) — Specific Matters
> "For each house, a planet works as the natural significator. To see matters signified by a house, we can take the relevant planet as the reference."

| Planet | Houses It Signifies (as reference) |
|--------|-----------------------------------|
| Sun | 9th, 10th, 11th |
| Moon | 4th, 1st, 2nd, 11th, 9th |
| Mars | 3rd |
| Mercury | 6th |
| Jupiter | 5th |
| Venus | 7th |
| Saturn | 8th, 12th |

> "When analyzing courage or persistence or younger brothers, we analyze the 3rd house in the relevant divisional chart. We see the 3rd house from lagna and the 3rd house from Mars and draw conclusions based on the strength of both. If Mars is stronger than lagna, then the 3rd house from Mars may be more important than the 3rd house from lagna."

- Marriage: 7th from lagna AND 7th from Venus
- Children: 5th from lagna AND 5th from Jupiter
- Mother: 4th from lagna AND 4th from Moon
- Father: 9th from lagna AND 9th from Sun

## 5. Controversy on House Division (Pages 89-90)

PVR takes a definitive stand: each rasi IS a house, rejecting the chalit/bhava chakra approach.

> "Each rasi is a house. The rasi containing the reference point chosen is the 1st house and the next rasi is the 2nd house."
>
> "Though there are some indirect references in BPHS suggesting that Parasara supported house divisions placing houses in 2 rasis, there are quite a few direct references making it amply clear that each house falls in one rasi. Parasara taught us to find houses by counting rasis from the reference chosen."
>
> "Readers are advised to ignore all the discussions found in other textbooks on house division methods, 'bhaava chakra' and 'chalit chakra'."

## Rule PVR-HOU-1: D-4 Narayana Dasa for Residence/Housing Timing
**Source:** PVR, pp. 256-257 (Section 18.5)
**Rule:** Use Narayana dasa of D-4 (Chaturamsa) to time changes in residence, happiness from home, and stay in foreign countries. The seed of D-4 is the 4th house (showing home, residence, and fortune).

**BTR Application:** For housing events (purchase of house, moving to a new home, construction), compute D-4 Narayana dasa. Find the 4th lord in rasi chart, see where he is in D-4, treat that rasi as lagna, and compute ND. A favorable dasa in D-4 ND supports positive housing events; an unfavorable dasa may indicate housing problems.

## Rule PVR-HOU-2: D-4 Indicators for Housing Comfort
**Source:** PVR, pp. 251, 258 (Sections 18.4, Example 71)
**Rule:** In Narayana dasa interpretation applied to D-4:
- The 4th house from the D-4 treated lagna shows home comfort and real estate
- Benefics in the 4th from dasa lagna → happiness, well-being, and comfortable home
- Malefics in the 4th from dasa lagna → discomfort and lack of happiness in home
- The 9th house in D-4 ND shows prospering in a foreign land (when analyzing foreign residence)

**BTR Application:** For housing events, interpret D-4 ND using these principles. A move to a better home should involve benefics in the 4th from dasa lagna. A home-related difficulty should involve malefics in the 4th.

## Rule PVR-HOU-3: Narayana Dasa of D-4 for Property Transactions
**Source:** PVR, p. 256 (Section 18.5)
**Rule:** Narayana dasa of D-4 is the appropriate dasa for timing all 4th house matters including property transactions, real estate purchases/sales, and housing changes. The seed of D-4 is the 4th house.
**BTR Application:** For property purchase/sale events, verify using D-4 ND. The dasa/antardasa favorable for property should involve the 4th house (from the D-4 treated lagna) or its lord, with benefic influences.

## Rule PVR-HOU-4: A4 (Vahanapada) for Vehicle/Comfort Events
**Source:** PVR, pp. 265-266 (Example 75)
**Rule:** A4 (arudha pada of the 4th house = vahanapada) shows vehicles and comforts. The 12th from A4 = losses to vehicle. When A8 (mrityupada) is in a sign aspecting A4 or in the 12th from A4, vehicle-related troubles occur.
**BTR Application:** For vehicle purchase or vehicle accident events, track A4 in the rasi chart and D-16. Vehicle purchase should involve A4 favorably placed. Vehicle accident involves the 12th from A4 afflicted by A8 or malefics.
