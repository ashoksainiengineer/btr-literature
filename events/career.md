### Rule 1: Nakshatra Verification (Birth Constellation Check)

**Source:** Hindu Predictive Astrology, Ch.11 — B.V. Raman
**URL:** https://vedastro.org/blog/Hindu-Predictive-Astrology-Chapter-11-Birth-Verification-and-Rectification.html
**Rule:** "Multiply the time of birth in vighatis by 4 and divide the product by 9. The remainder must give the ruling constellation when counted from Aswini, Makha or Moola." — B.V. Raman

**Formula:** (Vighatis × 4) ÷ 9 → remainder → count from Aswini/Makha/Moola = birth nakshatra
**Purpose:** Confirm birth time is consistent with the Moon's birth star
**Worked example:** 2033 vighatis × 4 = 8132 ÷ 9 = remainder 5 → 5th from Aswini = Mrigasira ✓

### Three Nakshatra Groups

| Remainder | From Aswini (G1) | From Makha (G2) | From Moola (G3) |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Aswini | Makha | Moola |
| 2 | Bharani | Pubba | Poorvashadha |
| 3 | Krittika | Uttara | Uttarashadha |
| 4 | Rohini | Hasta | Sravana |

### Rule 2: Lagna Verification (Ascendant Check)

**Source:** Hindu Predictive Astrology, Ch.11 — B.V. Raman
**URL:** https://vedastro.org/blog/Hindu-Predictive-Astrology-Chapter-11-Birth-Verification-and-Rectification.html
**Rule:** "Multiply the number of ghatis from birth by 6 and add the longitude of the Sun (the number of degrees passed in the sign). Divide the sum by 30. The quotient plus 1 counted from the Sun's sign will give the rising sign." — B.V. Raman

**Formula (ghatis):** (Ghatis × 6 + Sun° in sign) ÷ 30 → quotient + 1 from Sun's sign = Ascendant
**Formula (hours):** (Hours × 15 + Sun° in sign) ÷ 30 → quotient + 1 from Sun's sign = Ascendant
**Worked example:** 33.88 ghatis × 6 = 203.28 + 24° (Sun in Cancer) = 227.28 ÷ 30 = q=7 → 8 from Cancer = Aquarius ✓
**Caveat:** Approximate check to verify Ascendant **sign**, not exact degrees.

### Cascade Effect of Time Error

| Time Error | Ascendant Effect | Dasa Balance Effect | Divisional Chart Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1-2 min | Degree shifts slightly | Days change | Usually unchanged |
| 5-10 min | Degree shifts noticeably | Weeks to months change | Navamsa (D-9) may change |
| 15-30 min | May cross Navamsa boundary | Months change significantly | Multiple divisional charts shift |
| 1-2 hrs | Likely changes sign entirely | Entire Dasa may change | Nearly all charts affected |
| 4+ hrs | Completely different Lagna | Different Dasa lord | Entirely different horoscope |

> **Page 102:**

**Rule:** Career analysis requires examining BOTH:
- **10th from lagna in D-10** → true nature of work/karma
- **10th from AL in D-10** → perceived status, reputation in career

### 2. A10 in D-10 — The Workplace

> **Page 105:**
> "A10 in D-10 shows the maya associated with one's karma (action – career). We form an impression about one's career based on the place and environment where one's work takes place. So A10 in D-10 shows one's workplace."

**Planetary significations for A10 in D-10 (Page 105):**
- **Mars in A10 in D-10:** May work at dynamic places or engineering companies
- **Jupiter in A10 in D-10:** May work at a university, college, court of law, or temple

**BTR Application:** The Dasa of the planet influencing A10 in D-10 should coincide with career establishment or job changes.

### 3. Argala on Saturn — Decisive Influences on Livelihood

> **Pages 119-121:**
> "Saturn is the significator of livelihood and karma. Argalas on him denote decisive influences on livelihood and karma."

**Example interpretations (Page 119-121):**
- **Mercury's argala on Saturn** → indicates writing and scholarliness (Example 35: P.V. Narasimha Rao)

- **Venus argala on Saturn** → indicates actor, entertainer, showman (Example 36: Ronald Reagan)

- **Rahu in 11th from Saturn (malefic argala)** → pathbreaking innovation, gains through unconventional means

**BTR Application:** When verifying career events, check which planets have argala on Saturn in D-10. The Dasa of planets with argala on Saturn should coincide with career milestones in the field indicated.

---

### 4. Darapada (A7) in D-10 — Professional Associates

> **Page 104:**
> "In D-10, darapada may show professional associates."

**Rule:** A7 in D-10 reveals the nature of one's professional network and business partners.

### 5. A5 in D-10 — Awards and Power

> **Page 105:**
> "The 5th house in D-10, the chart of career, shows the following one has. The world forms an impression about one's following based on the power wielded by one. A political leader in a position of power is assumed to have a lot of following. So A5 can show the trappings of power enjoyed by one. It can also show awards."

**BTR Application:** Career awards, honors, and recognitions should correspond with Dasas of planets connected to A5 in D-10.

## 1. Career Analysis — Three Reference Points (Sudarsana Chakra)

## 2. Sudarsana Chakra Dasa — Career Decline (Indira Gandhi Example)

> **Source section**: Part 4, Chapter 31.4 — Dasa Interpretation (pages 424-426)

**Example 127 (pages 424-426):** Indira Gandhi's party lost elections in 1976-77; she was removed as Prime Minister of India.

**Analysis:**
- Finished years: 1976-1917 = 59, add 1 → 60 ÷ 12 = remainder 12 → SC dasa of 12th house
- Among lagna/Moon/Sun, Sun (with Budha-Aaditya yoga) is strongest
- 12th from Sun → Libra SC dasa
- In Tajaka annual chart viewed from Libra: Benefic Venus in 3rd (failures), Rahu afflicts 1st, Mars/Sun/Mercury poorly placed in 2nd, Saturn afflicts 10th (career troubles)
- Bad placement (especially Saturn and Rahu) — SC dasa doesn't promise a good year

**Rule:** For career decline/power loss, check the SC dasa rasi in the Tajaka annual chart. Malefics afflicting the 10th house from the SC dasa rasi confirm career setbacks.

**BTR Application:** Known career setbacks should correspond with SC dasa of a dusthana house (6th, 8th, 12th) and malefic affliction to the 10th from the dasa rasi.

## 3. Job Change Prediction — D-10 Narayana Dasa + Tajaka Monthly Charts

> **Source section**: Part 6 — Real-life Example 3 (pages 504-508)

**Case:** Native came in May 1998 asking about job change. D-10 Narayana dasa showed Cp dasa (1989-1999), Ar antardasa (Mar 1998-Jan 1999). Ar is 8th from A10 (workplace), its lord in 12th from A10 → job change/turbulence indicated.

**Multi-layer verification:**
1. **D-10 Narayana dasa:** Ar contains AL, trine from lagna — good for career/status. But Mars with Rahu in 8th from Cp dasa rasi → pressures.
2. **D-10 of Tajaka annual chart 1998-99:** AL contains Rahu, Saturn in 5th from it — good status. But Venus (7th lord and 9th from AL) debilitated → setbacks. Saturn-Sun aspect → trouble from authorities.
3. **Tajaka monthly charts:** In May 26-June 26 1998, D-10 lagna lord Venus in 12th with exalted Sun → trouble from authorities. In June 26-July 26, D-10 lagna lord Jupiter in 10th in moolatrikona — good developments.
4. **Pratyantardasa:** Scorpio PD brought pressures (8th lord, debilitated Moon). Sg PD (July) brought success — Sun is lagna lord and 5th from AL, occupies trine from AL, aspects GL (Pi) → promotion.

**Result:** Predicted deterioration with managers (June), forced job loss, and better job in July. **Came true.**

**BTR Application:** For career events, layer D-10 Narayana dasa, Tajaka annual D-10, and Tajaka monthly D-10 to converge on exact timing. Job loss is indicated by D-10 A8 involvement; new job by D-10 AL and GL activation.
