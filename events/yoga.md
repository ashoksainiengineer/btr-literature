**Effects:**
- **Asraya & Dala yogas (Sloka 174):** Produce wealth, happiness, good birth

- **Sankhya yogas (Sloka 179-180):** Results based on numerical patterns

## Laghu Parashari: Kendra-Trikona Yoga System

**Source:** Laghu Parashari (Ududaya Pradeepa), Chapter 2 (Yoga Adhyaya)
**Source URL:** https://archive.org/details/laghu-parashari
**Reference:** `/home/jovyan/ai-pandit-app/data/btr-knowledge/classical/laghu-parashari.md`

### Fundamental Rule

> केन्द्र-त्रिकोणपतयः सम्बन्धेन परस्परम् ।
> इतरैरप्रसक्ताश्चेद् विशेषफलदायकाः ॥ १॥

**Kendra lords** (4, 7, 10) and **Trikona lords** (1, 5, 9) in mutual connection → **Yoga Karakas** — provided they are NOT connected to other house lords (3, 6, 11).

### Yoga Despite Defects

> केन्द्र-त्रिकोणनेतारौ दोषयुक्तावपि स्वयम् ।
> सम्बन्धमात्राद् बलिनौ भवेतां योगकारकौ ॥ २॥

**Key Rule:** Even if the Kendra lord and Trikona lord are **defective** (debilitated, in enemy sign, fallen), their **mere mutual connection** makes them Yoga Karakas. The connection overcomes individual defects.

### Three Valid Connection Types

| Type | Description | Strength |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **1. Exchange** (Paraspara) | Kendra lord in Trikona, Trikona lord in Kendra | Strongest |
| **2. Same position** (Ekatra) | Both in same Kendra or same Trikona | Medium |
| **3. One-way placement** (Anyatara) | Either Kendra lord in Trikona OR Trikona lord in Kendra | Lowest |

### Best Yoga (Suyoga)

> त्रिकोणाधिपयोर्मध्ये सम्बन्धो येन केनचित् ।
> केन्द्रनाथस्य बलिनो भवेद् यदि सुयोगकृत् ॥ ४॥

**10th lord** (strongest kendra lord) connected with **5th lord or 9th lord** → **Suyoga (best yoga)**

**Hierarchy:**
1. 10th lord + 5th/9th lord → **Best**
2. 7th lord + 5th/9th lord → **Medium**
3. 4th lord + 5th/9th lord → **Lower**
4. 1st lord + 5th/9th lord → **Lowest** (but 1st is already trikona)

### Single Planet Rule (Utkrishta Yoga)

> केन्द्र-त्रिकोणाधिपत्यैकत्वे योगकारिता ।
> अन्यत्रिकोणपतिना सम्बन्धो यदि किं परम् ॥ ७॥

A **single planet** ruling **both a Kendra AND a Trikona** is automatically a Yoga Karaka. If it also connects with **another Trikona lord** → this is the **highest yoga possible**.

### Rahu/Ketu as Yoga Karakas

> यदि केन्द्र-त्रिकोणे वा निवसेतां तमोग्रहौ ।
> नाथेनान्यतरेणापि सम्बन्धाद् योगकारकौ ॥ ८॥

Rahu/Ketu become Yoga Karakas when:
- They are in a **Kendra** AND connected to a **Trikona lord**, OR
- They are in a **Trikona** AND connected to a **Kendra lord**

### Yoga Cancellation (Yoga Bhanga)

> धर्म-कर्माधिनेतारौ रन्ध्र-लाभाधिपौ यदि ।
> तयोः सम्बन्धमात्रेण न योगं लभते नरः ॥ ९॥

**Yoga CANCELLED** when:
- **9th lord** is ALSO the **8th lord**, OR
- **10th lord** is ALSO the **11th lord**

In these cases, mere mutual connection does NOT produce yoga. Additional strength (exaltation, etc.) is needed.

## Rule 2: Raja Yoga Bhanga (Cancellation) — CRITICAL

> **Source:** Jataka Bharanam, Raja Yoga Bhanga Adhyaya, Slokas 1-5

**Five conditions that break Raja Yogas:**

1. **All planets in enemy signs** → even Vargottama cannot give Raja Yoga
2. **Many planets in debilitation (नीच)** → destroys Raja Yoga
3. **No planet aspects Lagna or Chandra** → Raja Yoga is broken (Parashara)
4. **Natural portents at birth** (Udaka-pata, Vyatipata, Bhukampa, Ketu-udaya) → even with Raja Yoga, native becomes destitute
5. **Sun in Tula at parama-nichamsha** → all Raja Yogas destroyed

---

## Rule 3: Raja Yoga Bhanga — Parashara's Rule

> **Source:** Jataka Bharanam, Raja Yoga Bhanga Adhyaya, Sloka 2

> चन्द्रं यदि वा लग्नं ग्रहैरेकोऽपि नेक्षते ।
> तथापि राजयोगानां भङ्गमाह पराशरः ॥

**BTR RULE:** If **no planet aspects either Chandra or Lagna**, Raja Yogas break, even if other combinations exist. This is Parashara's dictum.

---

## Rule 4: Debilitation Cancellation (Neecha Bhanga)

> **Source:** Jataka Bharanam, Graha Yoga Adhyaya

The text describes conditions when a debilitated planet has its neecha (debilitation) cancelled:
- The debilitation lord is in kendra/trikona
- The debilitated planet is in an upachaya house
- Mutual exchange between debilitated planet and its lord

# Jataka Parijata Vol II — Raja Yogas & Planetary Combinations

**Source:** Jataka Parijata Vol II, Descriptive Contents & Various Adhyayas
**Author:** Vaidyanatha Dikshita (Tr. V. Subrahmanya Sastri, 1932)

## 1. Raja Yoga Principles (from descriptive contents)
Yogas take effect when the lord of the yoga is strong (in exaltation, own house, Moolatrikona). Effect becomes mixed when the planet is in a friendly sign but not otherwise strong. Transit and Dasa must activate the yoga lord.

---

## 2. Planetary Strength for Yogas
A yoga's full effect requires:
- **Bhava lord** in Kendra or Trikona from Lagna (Adhyaya XI, Sloka 5)
- Lord in **exaltation, Moolatrikona, or own house** (Sloka 3)
- Not in **6th, 8th, or 12th** (Sloka 4)
- Aspected by or associated with **benefics** (Sloka 1)
- Lord occupying **11th, 2nd, or 3rd from the bhava** advances its strength (Sloka 11)

---

## 3. Yogas from Bhava Combinations (Adhyaya XI-XV)

**First Bhava Yogas:**
- Fame yoga (Sloka 16)
- Learning yoga (Sloka 20)

## Rule PVR-YOG-1: Raja Yogas w.r.t. Dasa Lagna = Success
**Source:** PVR, p. 251 (Section 18.4)
**Rule:** In Narayana dasa interpretation, raja yogas and dhana yogas with respect to dasa lagna bring success. If dasa lagna or paaka rasi is associated with an exalted planet or a planet in own house, there will be all-round success and accumulation of wealth in that dasa.
**BTR Application:** For a period of major success, verify that the Narayana dasa lagna forms raja yogas or dhana yogas: (a) Lord of dasa lagna exalted or in own house, (b) Conjunction of lords of quadrants/trines from dasa lagna, (c) Dasa lagna associated with exalted planet.

## Rule PVR-YOG-2: AK and AmK in Dasa Determination for Power
**Source:** PVR, pp. 273-274 (Example 76)
**Rule:** Rasis containing AK (atmakaraka) give success. Signs having a strong argala from AmK (amatyakaraka) show coming under the decisive influence of good advisors, ministers and bureaucrats — especially important for political power. GL (ghati lagna) in a rasi = seat of power.
**BTR Application:** For political power or high-authority career events, verify that the running dasa rasi contains:
- AK (for personal success and fulfillment of purpose)
- Strong argala from AmK (for capable advisors/support)
- GL (for seat of power)
**Example:** Ronald Reagan's Sg dasa brought presidency — Sg had AK, GL, and unobstructed argala from AmK.

## Rule PVR-YOG-3: Vesi Yoga (Planet in 2nd from Sun) = Dasa Success
**Source:** PVR, pp. 227, 229-230 (Examples 52, 54, 55)
**Rule:** Vesi yoga occurs when a planet is in the 2nd house from Sun. Such a planet's dasa brings the results of Vesi yoga — success, fame, achievement. This is a Ravi yoga (solar combination) and its results manifest primarily in the mahadasa (per Rath's Tripod of Life).
**BTR Application:** When verifying career/fame events, check if the running mahadasa lord forms Vesi yoga (2nd from Sun). If so, the yoga's results will manifest in that mahadasa, explaining career success.

## Rule PVR-YOG-4: AK-PK Conjunction = Raja Yoga (especially in 1st/5th)
**Source:** PVR, pp. 277-278 (Section 20.3, Example 78)
**Rule:** The conjunction of AK (atmakaraka) and PK (putrakaraka) is a raja yoga, especially in the 1st and 5th houses. This yoga gives power, authority, and success. In Narayana dasa, such a yoga with respect to dasa lagna brings success.
**BTR Application:** For major power/acquisition events, check if the running Narayana dasa lagna contains or is aspected by the AK-PK conjunction. This yoga in the 1st or 5th from dasa lagna is a strong power indicator.
**Example:** Sri Vajpayee's Sc Narayana dasa — Sc (lagna) contained AK (Mercury) and PK (Saturn) conjunction = raja yoga that made him PM.

## Rule PVR-YOG-5: Vipareeta Raja Yoga (VRY) Transfer to Lagna Lord
**Source:** PVR, pp. 241-242 (Example 62)
**Rule:** Vipareeta Raja Yoga (VRY) involves lords of 3rd/8th or 6th/8th or 12th/8th. If the planets involved in VRY have a conjunction or rasi aspect with lagna lord, then VRY can give permanent results and the power is transferred to lagna lord. The lagna lord then becomes a great yoga karaka.
**BTR Application:** For long-term career success/power, check if the dasa lord involved in a VRY is connected to lagna lord. If so, the VRY's permanent results (power/success) manifest through the lagna lord's dasa.
**Example:** Sri P.V. Narasimha Rao — Mars (3rd+8th lord) and Sun (12th lord) in VRY within 17', disposited by Mercury (lagna lord). Mercury became a great yoga karaka, making his dasa (1981-1997) excellent for career.

## Rule PVR-YOG-6: Benefics in Trines and 8th from Dasa Lagna = Yoga of Happiness
**Source:** PVR, p. 251 (Section 18.4)
**Rule:** Natural benefics in trines and 8th from dasa lagna give happiness and success. Natural malefics in those houses give failures, obstructions and unhappiness. This is a key principle for evaluating the quality of a Narayana dasa period.
**BTR Application:** For evaluating whether a period should bring happiness/success or failure/obstructions, check planetary placements from dasa lagna:
- Benefics in trines (1st, 5th, 9th) and 8th → favorable period
- Malefics in trines and 8th → challenging period
- Rahu in 8th or 12th → constant fear
