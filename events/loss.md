### Rule 1: Nakshatra Verification (Birth Constellation Check)

**Source:** Hindu Predictive Astrology, Ch.11 — B.V. Raman
**URL:** https://vedastro.org/blog/Hindu-Predictive-Astrology-Chapter-11-Birth-Verification-and-Rectification.html
**Rule:** "Multiply the time of birth in vighatis by 4 and divide the product by 9. The remainder must give the ruling constellation when counted from Aswini, Makha or Moola." — B.V. Raman

**Formula:** (Vighatis × 4) ÷ 9 → remainder → count from Aswini/Makha/Moola = birth nakshatra
**Purpose:** Confirm birth time is consistent with the Moon's birth star
**Worked example:** 2033 vighatis × 4 = 8132 ÷ 9 = remainder 5 → 5th from Aswini = Mrigasira ✓

### Rule 2: Lagna Verification (Ascendant Check)

**Source:** Hindu Predictive Astrology, Ch.11 — B.V. Raman
**URL:** https://vedastro.org/blog/Hindu-Predictive-Astrology-Chapter-11-Birth-Verification-and-Rectification.html
**Rule:** "Multiply the number of ghatis from birth by 6 and add the longitude of the Sun (the number of degrees passed in the sign). Divide the sum by 30. The quotient plus 1 counted from the Sun's sign will give the rising sign." — B.V. Raman

**Formula (ghatis):** (Ghatis × 6 + Sun° in sign) ÷ 30 → quotient + 1 from Sun's sign = Ascendant
**Formula (hours):** (Hours × 15 + Sun° in sign) ÷ 30 → quotient + 1 from Sun's sign = Ascendant
**Worked example:** 33.88 ghatis × 6 = 203.28 + 24° (Sun in Cancer) = 227.28 ÷ 30 = q=7 → 8 from Cancer = Aquarius ✓
**Caveat:** Approximate check to verify Ascendant **sign**, not exact degrees.

### Rule 3: Trinal Relationship Check

**Source:** Hindu Predictive Astrology, Ch.11 — B.V. Raman
**URL:** https://vedastro.org/blog/Hindu-Predictive-Astrology-Chapter-11-Birth-Verification-and-Rectification.html
**Rule:** "The 5th or the 9th sign from the house occupied by the lord of the sign in which the Moon is placed becomes the Janma Lagna or the ascendant. The 7th from the sign occupied by the lord of the Moon's sign or the 5th or the 9th from the similar sign and in some cases the sign where the Moon is at radix itself becomes the ascendant." — B.V. Raman

**Check:** Asc should be 5th, 9th, or 7th from the Moon's sign lord's position
**Rationale:** Houses 1, 5, 9 form the Dharma Trikona — the Ascendant lord and Moon's sign lord should be harmoniously related.

## Chapter 42 — Combinations for Penury

### 1. Ascendant Lord in 12th + 12th Lord in Ascendant (Sloka 2)
> The native will be penniless if the ascendant lord is in the 12th as the 12th lord is in the ascendant along with a Maraka lord (death inflicting planet) or be in aspect to such a planet.

**Signification:** Complete reversal of fortune — the significator of self (ascendant lord) goes to loss (12th), while loss (12th lord) takes over the ascendant.

### 2. Ascendant Lord in 6th + 6th Lord in Ascendant (Sloka 3)
> The native will be penniless if the ascendant lord is in the 6th while the 6th lord is in the ascendant with conjunction or aspect of a Maraka lord.

**Signification:** Service/disease house (6th) overcomes the self, combined with Maraka influence.

### 3. Ascendant/Moon with Ketu + Ascendant Lord in 8th (Sloka 4)
> Should the ascendant or the Moon be with Ketu while the ascendant lord is in the 8th, the person concerned will be penniless.

**BTR Application:** For individuals with known financial loss events, check Ketu's presence in ascendant or with Moon.

### 4. Ascendant Lord + Malefic in 6th/8th/12th + 2nd Lord Afflicted (Sloka 5)
> If the ascendant lord along with a malefic is in the 6th, 8th or 12th while the 2nd lord is in an enemy's sign or in debilitation, even a native of royal scion will become penniless.

### 5. Ascendant Lord Conjunct 6th/8th/12th Lord or Saturn (Sloka 6)
> If the ascendant lord is conjunct an evil house lord (one of 6th/8th/12th lords) or Saturn, devoid of benefic aspect → penniless.

### 6. 5th and 9th Lords in 6th and 12th (Sloka 7)
> Should the 5th and 9th lords be respectively found in the 6th and 12th and be in aspect to Maraka planets → penniless.

**Note:** The two primary wealth-giving houses (5th = Lakshmi, 9th = Bhagya) being in evil houses (6th = disease/debt, 12th = loss) indicates severe financial distress.

### 7. Malefics in Ascendant (Except 10th/9th Lords) + Maraka (Sloka 8)
> If malefics (except lords of 10th and 9th) are in the ascendant with/aspect to Marakas → penniless.

### 8. Chain of Evil Dispositors (Sloka 9)
> If lords of 6th/8th/12th are themselves placed in evil houses with malefics → miserable and indigent.

**BTR Application:** Trace the dispositorship chain — if it leads back to 6th/8th/12th repeatedly, poverty is confirmed.

### 9. Moon's Navamsa Lord + Maraka (Sloka 10)
> The lord of the Navamsa occupied by the Moon joining a Maraka planet or occupying a Maraka house → penniless.

### 10. Ascendant Lord + Navamsa Ascendant Lord with Maraka (Sloka 11)
> Should the lord of natal ascendant and lord of Navamsa ascendant be conjunct or aspected by Maraka planets → penniless.

### 11. Reversed Benefic/Malefic Placement (Sloka 12)
> If inauspicious houses (6th/8th/12th) have benefics while auspicious houses (1st/5th/9th) have malefics → indigent, distressed even for food.

### 12. Mars-Saturn in 2nd (Slokas 16-18)
> Mars + Saturn together in 2nd house → native's wealth destroyed.
> Mercury aspect on Mars-Saturn in 2nd → great wealth (rescue).
> Sun in 2nd aspected by Saturn → penury.
> Sun in 2nd NOT aspected by Saturn → riches and fame.

### 13. Dasa Timing of Financial Harm (Sloka 13)
> A planet associated with 6th/8th/12th lord, if bereft of trinal lord's aspect, causes financial harm in its Dasa periods.

### 14. Spendthrift (Sloka 15)
> Native will be a spendthrift if:
> - 12th from Atmakaraka is aspected by Atmakaraka's depositor, OR
> - 12th from natal ascendant is aspected by ascendant lord

**Rule:** Resignation and job loss are listed as "Good" reliability events for BTR verification. They are classified separately from career advancement events because the house signature shifts from growth houses (2, 6, 10, 11) to loss/negation houses.

**Why Resignation/Job Loss is Useful for BTR:**
- Has a **clear, verifiable date** (resignation letter, termination letter, last working day)
- The house signature is **distinct from career growth** — it tests a different set of houses
- Provides contrast: advancement AND setback both fitting the same chart is a strong verification

**House Correspondence for Job Loss/Resignation:**
| Aspect | House | Significance |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| Negation of profession | 1st | 12th from 2nd (income negation) |
| Loss of service | 5th | 12th from 6th (service negation) |
| Career setback | 9th | 12th from 10th (profession negation) |
| Loss/Endings | 12th | General loss, voluntary/involuntary endings |

**CSL Verification:**
- The **10th Cusp Sub-Lord** must signify houses 1, 5, 9, or 12 for job loss/resignation
- These are the 12th-from houses of career significators: 2 (income), 6 (service), 10 (profession)
- If the 10th CSL at the candidate time signifies growth houses (2, 6, 10, 11) instead, a job loss event at that time would be anomalous
- The Dasha lord(s) at the resignation date should connect to houses 1, 5, 9, or 12

**Differentiating Voluntary vs. Involuntary:**
- **Resignation (voluntary):** Often involves 9th house (higher追求, desire for change) and 12th (liberation, new beginnings)
- **Termination (involuntary):** Often involves 8th house (sudden crisis) alongside 5th (loss of service) and 12th (endings)

**BTR Application:**
1. Job loss/resignation is most useful when combined with a subsequent career advancement event in the same chart — the same 10th CSL must explain BOTH events at their respective dates
2. If the chart shows career growth houses for a known termination date (or vice versa), the birth time is suspect
3. Use as a supporting event rather than primary; combine with marriage or childbirth for strongest verification
