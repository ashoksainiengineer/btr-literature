### Rule 1: Nakshatra Verification (Birth Constellation Check)

**Source:** Hindu Predictive Astrology, Ch.11 — B.V. Raman
**URL:** https://vedastro.org/blog/Hindu-Predictive-Astrology-Chapter-11-Birth-Verification-and-Rectification.html
**Rule:** "Multiply the time of birth in vighatis by 4 and divide the product by 9. The remainder must give the ruling constellation when counted from Aswini, Makha or Moola." — B.V. Raman

**Formula:** (Vighatis × 4) ÷ 9 → remainder → count from Aswini/Makha/Moola = birth nakshatra
**Purpose:** Confirm birth time is consistent with the Moon's birth star
**Worked example:** 2033 vighatis × 4 = 8132 ÷ 9 = remainder 5 → 5th from Aswini = Mrigasira ✓
**Rectification:** Work backwards — determine what vighati value produces the correct remainder for the known nakshatra, adjust birth time accordingly.

**Three Nakshatra Groups:**

| Remainder | From Aswini (G1) | From Makha (G2) | From Moola (G3) |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Aswini | Makha | Moola |
| 2 | Bharani | Pubba | Poorvashadha |
| 3 | Krittika | Uttara | Uttarashadha |
| 4 | Rohini | Hasta | Sravana |
| 5 | Mrigasira | Chitta | Dhanishta |
| 6 | Aridra | Swati | Satabhisha |
| 7 | Punarvasu | Visakha | Poorvabhadra |
| 8 | Pushyami | Anuradha | Uttarabhadra |
| 9 (or 0) | Aslesha | Jyeshta | Revati |

### Rule 2: Lagna Verification (Ascendant Check)

**Source:** Hindu Predictive Astrology, Ch.11 — B.V. Raman
**URL:** https://vedastro.org/blog/Hindu-Predictive-Astrology-Chapter-11-Birth-Verification-and-Rectification.html
**Rule:** "Multiply the number of ghatis from birth by 6 and add the longitude of the Sun (the number of degrees passed in the sign). Divide the sum by 30. The quotient plus 1 counted from the Sun's sign will give the rising sign." — B.V. Raman

**Formula (ghatis):** (Ghatis × 6 + Sun° in sign) ÷ 30 → quotient + 1 from Sun's sign = Ascendant
**Formula (hours):** (Hours × 15 + Sun° in sign) ÷ 30 → quotient + 1 from Sun's sign = Ascendant
**Worked example:** 33.88 ghatis × 6 = 203.28 + 24° (Sun in Cancer) = 227.28 ÷ 30 = q=7 → 8 from Cancer = Aquarius ✓
**Caveat:** Approximate check to verify Ascendant **sign**, not exact degrees.

### Rule 3: Trinal Relationship Check

**Source:** Hindu Predictive Astrology, Ch.11 — B.V. Raman
**URL:** https://vedastro.org/blog/Hindu-Predictive-Astrology-Chapter-11-Birth-Verification-and-Rectification.html
**Rule:** "The 5th or the 9th sign from the house occupied by the lord of the sign in which the Moon is placed becomes the Janma Lagna or the ascendant. The 7th from the sign occupied by the lord of the Moon's sign or the 5th or the 9th from the similar sign and in some cases the sign where the Moon is at radix itself becomes the ascendant." — B.V. Raman

**Check:** Asc should be 5th, 9th, or 7th from the Moon's sign lord's position
**Rationale:** Houses 1, 5, 9 form the Dharma Trikona — the Ascendant lord and Moon's sign lord should be harmoniously related.
**Usage:** Less mechanical than Rules 1 & 2. Allows multiple valid Ascendant signs. Best used as a filtering tool — failure strongly suggests error.

### Cascade Effect of Time Error

| Time Error | Ascendant Effect | Dasa Balance Effect | Divisional Chart Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1-2 min | Degree shifts slightly | Days change | Usually unchanged |
| 5-10 min | Degree shifts noticeably | Weeks to months change | Navamsa (D-9) may change |
| 15-30 min | May cross Navamsa boundary | Months change significantly | Multiple divisional charts shift |
| 1-2 hrs | Likely changes sign entirely | Entire Dasa may change | Nearly all charts affected |
| 4+ hrs | Completely different Lagna | Different Dasa lord | Entirely different horoscope |

### Practical BTR Workflow (Raman's Method)

1. **Rule 1** (Nakshatra check) — narrow time range if it fails
2. **Rule 2** (Lagna check) — verify Ascendant sign
3. **Rule 3** (Trinal check) — confirm harmonic relationship
4. **Check Navamsa Lagna** (±15 min window) — test stability; use known life events to determine best fit
5. **Test Dasa transitions** — major Dasa changes should correspond to known life turning points
6. **Verify with transits** — Saturn returns, Jupiter transits to angles should coincide with major events

## 1. Sthira Karaka for Maternal Grandfather (Pages 93-94)

From PVR's sthira karaka system — used specifically for timing the death of relatives:

> **Page 93-94**: "Mercury: Maternal relatives (uncles and aunts)"

**Key Rule**: Mercury represents **maternal relatives** including the maternal grandfather. For timing the death of a maternal grandfather:

1. **Mercury** is the primary sthira karaka for maternal lineage
2. **Venus** also covers "father-in-law, mother-in-law & maternal grandparents" per PVR's sthira karaka list
3. The death should occur in a dasha connected to Mercury or Venus, or when either is afflicted

## 2. House Derivation for Maternal Grandfather (Pages 82-83)

Using PVR's derivative house principle:

> "We can find houses from houses and concatenate the meanings."

**Derivation Path**:
- Mother = 4th house (primary)
- Maternal grandfather = 9th from 4th = 12th house (father of mother)
- Alternative: 9th from Moon (Moon shows mother, 9th from Moon shows her father)
- Death of maternal grandfather = 8th from 12th = 7th house

**BTR Application**:
1. The 7th house (death trigger), 8th house (death), and 12th house (loss, the house of maternal grandfather himself) should be involved
2. The dasa lord at time of death should connect to these houses
3. Check the 9th from Chandra Lagna (Moon's 9th = mother's father)

## 4. Timing Principle

When a known maternal grandfather death event exists:
1. Identify Mercury's and Venus's positions, dignities, and aspects
2. Check the running dasha — the dasa lord should be connected to Mercury, Venus, or the 4th/12th house (maternal lineage houses)
3. Verify that maraka lords (2nd/7th from relevant reference) are activated
4. Use Chandra Lagna (Moon as reference) as an additional verification — the 9th from Moon shows maternal grandfather, 8th from 9th from Moon shows his death
5. D-12 (Dwadasamsa) confirms parent/grandparent events
