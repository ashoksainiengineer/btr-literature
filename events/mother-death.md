### Rule 1: Nakshatra Verification (Birth Constellation Check)

**Source:** Hindu Predictive Astrology, Ch.11 — B.V. Raman
**URL:** https://vedastro.org/blog/Hindu-Predictive-Astrology-Chapter-11-Birth-Verification-and-Rectification.html
**Rule:** "Multiply the time of birth in vighatis by 4 and divide the product by 9. The remainder must give the ruling constellation when counted from Aswini, Makha or Moola." — B.V. Raman

**Formula:** (Vighatis × 4) ÷ 9 → remainder → count from Aswini/Makha/Moola = birth nakshatra
**Purpose:** Confirm birth time is consistent with the Moon's birth star
**Worked example:** 2033 vighatis × 4 = 8132 ÷ 9 = remainder 5 → 5th from Aswini = Mrigasira ✓
**Rectification:** Work backwards — determine what vighati value produces the correct remainder for the known nakshatra, adjust birth time accordingly.

**Three Nakshatra Groups:**

| Remainder | From Aswini (G1) | From Makha (G2) | From Moola (G3) |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Aswini | Makha | Moola |
| 2 | Bharani | Pubba | Poorvashadha |
| 3 | Krittika | Uttara | Uttarashadha |
| 4 | Rohini | Hasta | Sravana |
| 5 | Mrigasira | Chitta | Dhanishta |
| 6 | Aridra | Swati | Satabhisha |
| 7 | Punarvasu | Visakha | Poorvabhadra |
| 8 | Pushyami | Anuradha | Uttarabhadra |
| 9 (or 0) | Aslesha | Jyeshta | Revati |

### Rule 2: Lagna Verification (Ascendant Check)

**Source:** Hindu Predictive Astrology, Ch.11 — B.V. Raman
**URL:** https://vedastro.org/blog/Hindu-Predictive-Astrology-Chapter-11-Birth-Verification-and-Rectification.html
**Rule:** "Multiply the number of ghatis from birth by 6 and add the longitude of the Sun (the number of degrees passed in the sign). Divide the sum by 30. The quotient plus 1 counted from the Sun's sign will give the rising sign." — B.V. Raman

**Formula (ghatis):** (Ghatis × 6 + Sun° in sign) ÷ 30 → quotient + 1 from Sun's sign = Ascendant
**Formula (hours):** (Hours × 15 + Sun° in sign) ÷ 30 → quotient + 1 from Sun's sign = Ascendant
**Worked example:** 33.88 ghatis × 6 = 203.28 + 24° (Sun in Cancer) = 227.28 ÷ 30 = q=7 → 8 from Cancer = Aquarius ✓
**Caveat:** Approximate check to verify Ascendant **sign**, not exact degrees.

---

### Rule 3: Trinal Relationship Check

**Source:** Hindu Predictive Astrology, Ch.11 — B.V. Raman
**URL:** https://vedastro.org/blog/Hindu-Predictive-Astrology-Chapter-11-Birth-Verification-and-Rectification.html
**Rule:** "The 5th or the 9th sign from the house occupied by the lord of the sign in which the Moon is placed becomes the Janma Lagna or the ascendant. The 7th from the sign occupied by the lord of the Moon's sign or the 5th or the 9th from the similar sign and in some cases the sign where the Moon is at radix itself becomes the ascendant." — B.V. Raman

**Check:** Asc should be 5th, 9th, or 7th from the Moon's sign lord's position
**Rationale:** Houses 1, 5, 9 form the Dharma Trikona — the Ascendant lord and Moon's sign lord should be harmoniously related.
**Usage:** Less mechanical than Rules 1 & 2. Allows multiple valid Ascendant signs. Best used as a filtering tool — failure strongly suggests error.

## 1. Mother's Death — 4th Bhava Rules (Adhyaya XII)

**Sloka 70-71:** When the lords of the 4th and the 9th bhavas are in a Dusthana and the lord of Lagna is strong → death of parents.

**Sloka 72-73:** Moon in specific position with Rahu/Ketu → mother intrigues with low-born; with Saturn → Sudra; with Mercury → Vaisya; with Sun → Kshatriya; with Jupiter/Venus → Brahmin.

**Sloka 74:** Moon in unfavorable position with lord of 3rd → person sucks another woman's breast. Lords of 4th and 9th in Dusthana + strong Lagna lord → death of both parents.

**Sloka 76:** If lords of 4th and Lagna in Trikona positions + lord of one Trikona in Lagna → mother dies along with father.

**Sloka 77:** If lords of 4th, 1st, and 9th in Kendra or Trikona → during their Dasas and Antardasas, mother follows father in death.

**Sloka 78:** Sun in 9th and Moon in 4th → mother follows father in death.

**Sloka 79:** Ascertain lord and Karaka of 4th, associated planets, occupants/aspects on Mathrubhava. Find which is malefic. During the Dasa period of this malefic planet → **mother's demise**.

**Sloka 80:** Subtract Moon's figures from Sun's → find Rasi remainder. When Saturn and Jupiter transit that Rasi and Navamsa/triangular positions → mother's death. Also subtract Yamakantaka from lord of 6th from Moon → remainder indicates Rasi and Navamsa. When Saturn transits Rasi and Sun transits Navamsa → mother passes away.

## 2. Mother's Death from 5th Bhava Section (Adhyaya XIII)

**Sloka 58:** Yogas fatal to the mother, child, and maternal uncle.

**Sloka 59:** The yoga betokening the mother's death at the next conception.

**Sloka 61-62:** Clue to find time of death of mother as well as father.

When verifying mother's death against birth time:
- **4th bhava** is primary for mother
- Dasa of malefic planet connected to 4th triggers mother's death (Sloka 79)
- Transit of Saturn and Jupiter through specific positions can pinpoint timing (Sloka 80)
- If mother died after father, check Sloka 76-78 for sequence yogas
- 5th bhava rules (Sloka 58-62) provide cross-verification

## Rule PVR-MTH-1: Matri Shoola Dasa for Mother's Death Timing
**Source:** PVR, pp. 297-300 (Section 23.5)
**Rule:** Matri Shoola dasa (matri = mother) starts from the stronger of 4th and 10th houses. It shows the motion of Shiva's force for the mother. Death can take place when Shiva's force strikes trines from sthira karaka of mother or trines from the corresponding arudha pada (A4 for mother, since 4th house represents mother).

**Key principles:**
- The matri shoola dasa is computed the same way as regular Shoola dasa but with the starting house = stronger of 4th/10th
- Each dasa = 9 years; always in regular zodiacal order
- Sthira karaka of mother = planet in a fixed sign representing mother
- Trines from sthira karaka of mother indicate the death dasa
- The 4th house arudha pada can also be used

**BTR Application:** For mother's death event verification, compute Matri Shoola dasa (from stronger of 4th/10th). Identify the sthira karaka for mother (planet in fixed sign among the seven karakas). The death should occur in the dasa of a trine from that sthira karaka.

## 1. Matri Saham — Mother Death Transit Point

**Source**: Ch.28, Table 74 (#12), page 395
**Page**: 395

**Matri Saham Formula**: Moon – Venus + Lagna (day); Venus – Moon + Lagna (night)

**BTR Application**:
- Calculate Matri Saham in the natal chart
- At mother's death, verify that malefics transited over or closely aspected Matri Saham
- 8th lord or maraka planets (2nd/7th lords) transiting Matri Saham strongly support the death event
- If multiple malefics simultaneously transit Matri Saham, the event is further confirmed

## 2. Mother's Death via Kalachakra Dasa — A4 Maraka Analysis

**Source**: Ch.24, Exercise 35, page 314, answer page 322
**Pages**: 314, 322

**Rule**: Mother's death can be timed using Kalachakra dasa and the A4 (arudha pada of 4th house) maraka analysis:
- Find A4 in rasi chart
- From A4's rasi, identify maraka rasis (2nd and 7th houses from A4)
- Death occurs when Kalachakra antardasa falls in a maraka rasi from A4
- The maraka rasi's lord should be strong (exalted) and afflict the 1st lord from A4

**Example**: Mother's death in Li-Li antardasa.
- 4th house in Sg. A4 in Vi.
- From Vi, Li is the 2nd house (maraka). Venus (Li lord) is exalted in 7th from Vi.
- Venus afflicts the debilitated 1st lord from Vi.
- Li is a strong maraka rasi from A4, hence Li-Li antardasa = mother's death.

**BTR Application**: For mother's death verification:
1. Find A4 (arudha pada of 4th house)
2. From A4's rasi, identify the 2nd and 7th houses (maraka sthanas)
3. Check if Kalachakra antardasa at death falls in one of these maraka rasis
4. Check SAV strength of the maraka rasi — strong evil house confirms evil result
5. Check if the maraka rasi's lord is strong and afflicts the 1st lord from A4
