### 2. Identifying Each Child in D-7

> **Page 183:**
> "The 5th house shows children in D-7. The 7th house is the 3rd from 5th and shows one's child's younger sibling. So the 5th lord shows the first child, the 7th lord shows the second child, the 9th house shows the third child and so on."

**Rule:** Count children sequentially by progressing through houses starting from the 5th:
- **5th lord** → first child
- **7th lord** → second child
- **9th house** → third child
- Continue through further houses

**Direction of counting (Page 183):**
> "When we count houses corresponding to siblings and children in D-3 and D-7, we count in the forward or backward direction based on whether lagna is odd or even (respectively)."

**Examples (Page 183):**
- If D-7 lagna is in **Ge (odd)**, Venus (lord of Li → 5th from Ge) shows first child, Jupiter (lord of Sg → 7th from Ge) shows second child

> "After covering all the odd or even signs, we move from odd to even signs or even to odd signs, instead of coming back to where we started."

**BTR Application:** The birth of each child should occur in the Dasa of the corresponding child-indicating planet/lord in D-7.

### Rule 1: Nakshatra Verification (Birth Constellation Check)

**Source:** Hindu Predictive Astrology, Ch.11 — B.V. Raman
**URL:** https://vedastro.org/blog/Hindu-Predictive-Astrology-Chapter-11-Birth-Verification-and-Rectification.html
**Rule:** "Multiply the time of birth in vighatis by 4 and divide the product by 9. The remainder must give the ruling constellation when counted from Aswini, Makha or Moola." — B.V. Raman

**Formula:** (Vighatis × 4) ÷ 9 → remainder → count from Aswini/Makha/Moola = birth nakshatra
**Purpose:** Confirm birth time is consistent with the Moon's birth star
**Worked example:** 2033 vighatis × 4 = 8132 ÷ 9 = remainder 5 → 5th from Aswini = Mrigasira ✓

### Rule 2: Lagna Verification (Ascendant Check)

**Source:** Hindu Predictive Astrology, Ch.11 — B.V. Raman
**URL:** https://vedastro.org/blog/Hindu-Predictive-Astrology-Chapter-11-Birth-Verification-and-Rectification.html
**Rule:** "Multiply the number of ghatis from birth by 6 and add the longitude of the Sun (the number of degrees passed in the sign). Divide the sum by 30. The quotient plus 1 counted from the Sun's sign will give the rising sign." — B.V. Raman

**Formula (ghatis):** (Ghatis × 6 + Sun° in sign) ÷ 30 → quotient + 1 from Sun's sign = Ascendant
**Formula (hours):** (Hours × 15 + Sun° in sign) ÷ 30 → quotient + 1 from Sun's sign = Ascendant
**Worked example:** 33.88 ghatis × 6 = 203.28 + 24° (Sun in Cancer) = 227.28 ÷ 30 = q=7 → 8 from Cancer = Aquarius ✓
**Caveat:** Approximate check to verify Ascendant **sign**, not exact degrees.

### Loss of Children (Sloka 37)

> "If Budha, Sani and Mangala be conjoined with Lords of three Upa Padas referred to in previous Sloka, and if same three Grahas mutually occupy each other's Rasis and Amsas, or if Sani and Guru be together in a single Rasi, there will be **loss of children**."

> "10th Bhava and its Lord, if occupied or aspected by Sani, will cause native to have no children."

   - Lord of 5th house

From the general principles of the 5th house:
- Number of children can be estimated from Grahas occupying/aspecting 5th house
- Grahas between Lagna and 5th house also contribute
- Benefic influence multiplies, malefic influence reduces
