### Rule 1: Nakshatra Verification (Birth Constellation Check)

**Source:** Hindu Predictive Astrology, Ch.11 — B.V. Raman
**URL:** https://vedastro.org/blog/Hindu-Predictive-Astrology-Chapter-11-Birth-Verification-and-Rectification.html
**Rule:** "Multiply the time of birth in vighatis by 4 and divide the product by 9. The remainder must give the ruling constellation when counted from Aswini, Makha or Moola." — B.V. Raman

**Formula:** (Vighatis × 4) ÷ 9 → remainder → count from Aswini/Makha/Moola = birth nakshatra
**Purpose:** Confirm birth time is consistent with the Moon's birth star
**Worked example:** 2033 vighatis × 4 = 8132 ÷ 9 = remainder 5 → 5th from Aswini = Mrigasira ✓

### Three Nakshatra Groups

| Remainder | From Aswini (G1) | From Makha (G2) | From Moola (G3) |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Aswini | Makha | Moola |
| 2 | Bharani | Pubba | Poorvashadha |
| 3 | Krittika | Uttara | Uttarashadha |
| 4 | Rohini | Hasta | Sravana |

### Rule 2: Lagna Verification (Ascendant Check)

**Source:** Hindu Predictive Astrology, Ch.11 — B.V. Raman
**URL:** https://vedastro.org/blog/Hindu-Predictive-Astrology-Chapter-11-Birth-Verification-and-Rectification.html
**Rule:** "Multiply the number of ghatis from birth by 6 and add the longitude of the Sun (the number of degrees passed in the sign). Divide the sum by 30. The quotient plus 1 counted from the Sun's sign will give the rising sign." — B.V. Raman

**Formula (ghatis):** (Ghatis × 6 + Sun° in sign) ÷ 30 → quotient + 1 from Sun's sign = Ascendant
**Formula (hours):** (Hours × 15 + Sun° in sign) ÷ 30 → quotient + 1 from Sun's sign = Ascendant
**Worked example:** 33.88 ghatis × 6 = 203.28 + 24° (Sun in Cancer) = 227.28 ÷ 30 = q=7 → 8 from Cancer = Aquarius ✓
**Caveat:** Approximate check to verify Ascendant **sign**, not exact degrees.

---

### Rule 3: Trinal Relationship Check

**Source:** Hindu Predictive Astrology, Ch.11 — B.V. Raman
**URL:** https://vedastro.org/blog/Hindu-Predictive-Astrology-Chapter-11-Birth-Verification-and-Rectification.html
**Rule:** "The 5th or the 9th sign from the house occupied by the lord of the sign in which the Moon is placed becomes the Janma Lagna or the ascendant. The 7th from the sign occupied by the lord of the Moon's sign or the 5th or the 9th from the similar sign and in some cases the sign where the Moon is at radix itself becomes the ascendant." — B.V. Raman

**Check:** Asc should be 5th, 9th, or 7th from the Moon's sign lord's position
**Rationale:** Houses 1, 5, 9 form the Dharma Trikona — the Ascendant lord and Moon's sign lord should be harmoniously related.

### Cascade Effect of Time Error

| Time Error | Ascendant Effect | Dasa Balance Effect | Divisional Chart Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1-2 min | Degree shifts slightly | Days change | Usually unchanged |
| 5-10 min | Degree shifts noticeably | Weeks to months change | Navamsa (D-9) may change |
| 15-30 min | May cross Navamsa boundary | Months change significantly | Multiple divisional charts shift |
| 1-2 hrs | Likely changes sign entirely | Entire Dasa may change | Nearly all charts affected |
| 4+ hrs | Completely different Lagna | Different Dasa lord | Entirely different horoscope |

### Practical BTR Workflow

1. **Rule 1** (Nakshatra check) — narrow time range if it fails
2. **Rule 2** (Lagna check) — verify Ascendant sign
3. **Rule 3** (Trinal check) — confirm harmonic relationship
4. **Check Navamsa Lagna** (±15 min window) — test stability; use known life events to determine best fit
5. **Test Dasa transitions** — major Dasa changes should correspond to known life turning points
6. **Verify with transits** — Saturn returns, Jupiter transits to angles should coincide with major events

# Jataka Parijata Vol II — Siblings (3rd Bhava) Rules

**Source:** Jataka Parijata Vol II, Adhyaya XII (Effects of 3rd & 4th Bhavas)
**Author:** Vaidyanatha Dikshita (Tr. V. Subrahmanya Sastri, 1932)
**Extracted:** 2026-05-24

---

## 1. 3rd Bhava Significations (Sloka 1)
From the 3rd bhava, the following are deducible:
- Welfare of elder and younger brothers
- Enterprise and daring
- Courage and valor
- Voice and tone
- Hearing (ear)
- Select ornaments and articles of apparel
- Steadiness
- Strength
- Edible roots and fruits
- Communications

---

## 2. Where to Find Siblings (Sloka 2)
Brothers (and sisters) are to be looked for in the following bhavas:
- **3rd** (primary)
- **9th** (secondary)
- **11th** (gains)
- **7th** (also indicated)

In the Dasa period of the lord of any of these bhavas, one may have an acquisition in the form of a brother.

---

## 3. Three Sources for Brother-Increase Dasa (Sloka 3)
Find which of these three is strongest:
1. The lord of the **3rd bhava**
2. The lord owning the **Rasi occupied by the lord of the 3rd**
3. The **planet occupying the 3rd bhava**

The Dasa of the strongest of these will lead to the increase of brothers.

> **BTR Application:** For sibling birth events, the Dasa of the strongest among these three triggers the birth of a sibling.

---

## 4. Long Life & Power (Sloka 4-5)
**Sloka 4:** If Mars occupying the 3rd bhava is without strength → the person will have long life. If Mars (Karaka of 3rd) is in Lagna → the person will be powerful.

**Sloka 5:** The Karaka of the 3rd bhava (Mars) when strong and at an advantageous position at birth produces prosperity. Lord of 3rd even when strong leads to decay when in 10th from Lagna.

---

## 5. Loss of Brothers (Sloka 6-7)
**Sloka 6:** If the lord of the 3rd bhava AND Mars occupy the 8th → loss of brothers. If both are in a malefic sign or with a malefic planet → they bring brothers into existence and take them away.

**Sloka 7:** If the lord of the 3rd and Mars (Karaka) occupy their depression signs or depression Navamsas, or are in conjunction with malefic planets or malefic Shashtyamsas (Kroora and others) → their influence brings brothers into the world and removes them prematurely.

---

## 6. Younger Brothers (Sloka 28-42, from descriptive contents)
- Conditions under which younger brothers flourish
- Conditions unfavorable to younger brothers' growth
- Yoga for one younger brother and no more
- Yogas for a younger brother endowed with health, happiness, long life
- Means of ascertaining the number of brothers
- Yogas that produce brothers or sisters or both
- Four kinds of planets that affect siblings beneficially or otherwise
- Another method for finding number of younger brothers and sisters
- Planets influencing a brother's fortunes for worse or better
- Union or disunion among brothers

---

## 7. BTR Application Summary for Sibling Events

When verifying sibling-related events against birth time:
- **3rd bhava** is primary for siblings
- Dasa of strongest among (3rd lord, its dispositor, 3rd occupant) triggers sibling births
- Mars as natural Karaka must be evaluated
- Loss of siblings occurs when 3rd lord and Mars afflict 8th
- Number of siblings can be estimated from 3rd bhava strength and occupants
- Younger brother births occur in Dasa of favorable planets connected to 3rd

### 2. Identifying Each Sibling in D-3

> **Page 183:**
> "In D-3, we see siblings. The 3rd house shows younger sibling and the 11th house shows elder sibling."

**Counting method (Page 183):**
- **5th house** (3rd from 3rd) → younger brother's younger brother = second younger sibling
- **9th house** (11th from 11th) → elder brother's elder brother = second elder sibling
- **3rd lord** → lagna of first (immediate) younger sibling
- **5th lord** → second younger sibling
- **7th lord** → third younger sibling
- **11th lord** → first (immediate) elder sibling
- **9th lord** → second elder sibling
- **7th lord** → third elder sibling

**Direction of counting (Page 183):**
> "When we count houses corresponding to siblings and children in D-3 and D-7, we count in the forward or backward direction based on whether lagna is odd or even (respectively)."

**Example (Pages 188, Example 45 — Rajiv Gandhi's D-3):**
> "Lagna in D-3 is in Sg, an odd sign. So we count houses normally. The 3rd house is Aq. Saturn is its lord and we should take Li as lagna in Rajiv Gandhi's D-3 to analyze Sanjay Gandhi's fortune."

**BTR Application:** The birth of each sibling should occur in the Dasa of the corresponding sibling-indicating planet/lord in D-3.
