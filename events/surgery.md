### Rule 1: Nakshatra Verification (Birth Constellation Check)

**Source:** Hindu Predictive Astrology, Ch.11 — B.V. Raman
**URL:** https://vedastro.org/blog/Hindu-Predictive-Astrology-Chapter-11-Birth-Verification-and-Rectification.html
**Rule:** "Multiply the time of birth in vighatis by 4 and divide the product by 9. The remainder must give the ruling constellation when counted from Aswini, Makha or Moola." — B.V. Raman

**Formula:** (Vighatis × 4) ÷ 9 → remainder → count from Aswini/Makha/Moola = birth nakshatra

### Rule 2: Lagna Verification (Ascendant Check)

**Source:** Hindu Predictive Astrology, Ch.11 — B.V. Raman
**URL:** https://vedastro.org/blog/Hindu-Predictive-Astrology-Chapter-11-Birth-Verification-and-Rectification.html
**Rule:** "Multiply the number of ghatis from birth by 6 and add the longitude of the Sun (the number of degrees passed in the sign). Divide the sum by 30. The quotient plus 1 counted from the Sun's sign will give the rising sign." — B.V. Raman

**Formula (ghatis):** (Ghatis × 6 + Sun° in sign) ÷ 30 → quotient + 1 from Sun's sign = Ascendant
**Formula (hours):** (Hours × 15 + Sun° in sign) ÷ 30 → quotient + 1 from Sun's sign = Ascendant
**Worked example:** 33.88 ghatis × 6 = 203.28 + 24° (Sun in Cancer) = 227.28 ÷ 30 = q=7 → 8 from Cancer = Aquarius ✓
**Caveat:** Approximate check to verify Ascendant **sign**, not exact degrees.

## Rule: Major Operation / Surgery as BTR Verification Event

**Source:** KP Astro Learning Hub — Best Events for BTR
**URL:** https://kpastroapp.com/learn/birth-time-rectification

**Rule:** Major operation and surgery are listed as "Very Good" reliability events for BTR verification. They rank just below marriage and childbirth in evidentiary value.

**Why Surgery is a Strong BTR Event:**
- Has a **clear, verifiable date** (medical records, hospital admission/discharge records)
- Has a **strong house signature** (6th for disease, 8th for surgery/crisis, 12th for hospitalization)
- Is **highly significant** to the native — usually remembered precisely
- Medical documentation provides independent verification

**House Correspondence for Surgery Events:**
| Aspect | House | Significance |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| Disease/condition | 6th house | Illness requiring intervention |
| Surgery/crisis | 8th house | Surgical procedure, transformation |
| Hospitalization | 12th house | Hospital stay, confinement |

**CSL Verification:**
- The **6th Cusp Sub-Lord** must signify houses 6, 8, or 12 for surgery to be permitted
- The **8th Cusp Sub-Lord** will typically connect to 8th (surgery) and 12th (hospitalization)
- Dasha running at surgery date: the period lord(s) should be significators of 6, 8, or 12

**BTR Application:**
1. For any candidate birth time, check whether the 6th CSL and 8th CSL support the surgery event

3. Transit triggers (Mars, Saturn, Rahu) at the surgery date should activate the 6th/8th house axis
4. Multiple surgeries at different dates provide independent verification points

**Caveat:** Minor procedures (e.g., dental extraction, minor skin surgery) carry less weight. Use only major operations with general anesthesia or significant medical intervention.
