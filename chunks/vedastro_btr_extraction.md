## BTR-Relevant Rules Extracted

### Verification Rules (from B.V. Raman Chapter 11):
1. **Nakshatra Formula:** (Birth time in vighatis × 4) ÷ 9 = remainder gives nakshatra counted from Aswini/Makha/Moola
2. **Lagna Formula:** (Ghatis × 6 + Sun's degrees) ÷ 30 = quotient gives signs from Sun = ascendant
3. **Trinal Relationship:** Ascendant should be 5th/9th/7th from Moon sign lord's position

### Dasa Verification Rules (from B.V. Raman Chapter 12, 24):
1. **Dasa Balance at Birth:** Moon's Nakshatra progress determines remaining Dasa at birth
2. **15 General Dasa Principles:** No Dasa exclusively good/bad; sequence position matters; Bhukti modifies Dasa
3. **Dasa-Bhukti Relationship:** Mutual kendras/trines = good; 6-8/2-12 = conflict
4. **Transit Overlay:** Dasa results modified by Gochara
5. **Life Predictor Algorithms:** General + IshtaKashtaPhalaDegree + PlanetStrengthDegree + DasaAshtakvargaBindu for Dasa verification

### Transit Verification Rules (from B.V. Raman Chapter 26):
1. **Bindu Scores:** 0-8 scale determines transit quality; >5 = good, <3 = poor
2. **Sarvashtakavarga:** >30 = highly favourable; 25-30 = neutral; <25 = unfavourable
3. **Upachaya Amplification:** Houses 3,6,10,11 amplify transit effects
4. **Kakshya Lord Precision:** 3°45' slices pin-point transit timing

### Longevity/Death Verification Rules (from B.V. Raman Chapter 14, 15):
1. **Four Life Span Categories:** Classify before identifying Marakas
2. **Maraka Hierarchy:** 6-level decision tree for death-inflicting planets
3. **Saturn Transit Formula:** S+J+Su+Mo longitude sum → sensitive transit point
4. **Balarishta:** 30 combinations + 10 antidotes for infant death assessment
