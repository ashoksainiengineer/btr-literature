### BTR Rules Extracted

#### Method 1: Event-Based Rectification (Western Astrology Framing)

**Description**: Works backwards from clearly dated life events — major relationships, relocations, career shifts, illnesses, or periods of crisis — and adjusts the birth time until predictive techniques consistently activate the chart's angles and rulers at those moments.

**Key Techniques Mentioned**:
- Planetary transits
- Secondary progressions
- Solar Arc directions

**BTR Application**: Priorities observable outcomes over theory. Each major life event serves as a data point. The rectified time is the one that best explains ALL known events through transit/progression triggers.

**Uniqueness vs. Existing KB**: This method is well-covered in the existing knowledge base across multiple event files. The Angelorum article presents it from a Western/Tropical astrology perspective rather than Vedic KP. No new technical detail beyond what's already extracted.

---

#### Method 2: Symbolic and Experiential Rectification

**Description**: Focus on how the chart is lived rather than when events occurred. Examines temperament, bodily presence, voice, appearance, and the way a person instinctively meets the world.

**Key Techniques**:
- Rising signs tested against real experience: how someone initiates action, relates to others, responds under pressure
- Physical appearance cues (face shape, body type)
- Psychological/behavioral profiling

**BTR Application**: Used especially in consultation-based and psychological astrology. The astrologer tests different Ascendant/house placements against the client's lived self-perception.

**Uniqueness vs. Existing KB**: Physical appearance-based Ascendant determination IS covered in existing KB (physical-characteristics.md, general-btr.md, timing.md). However, the experiential/psychological framing (how the chart is "lived") is a different emphasis. The article explicitly notes this method can be misleading — mirroring existing warnings in general-btr.md.

#### Method 3: Astro-Tarot Intuitive Rectification Method — NEW

**Description**: An intuitive method for those torn between two rising signs, unsure of Moon sign, or comparing Tropical vs. Sidereal charts. Uses Tarot Major Arcana cards corresponding to zodiac signs to create a spatial/embodied experience of the chart.

**Procedure**:
1. Choose the Major Arcana cards corresponding with Sun, Moon, and Ascendant signs
2. Imagine or sketch a zodiac wheel and place the cards where those placements would fall
3. Sit at the center of the wheel and feel into the chart
4. Notice how different chart versions "land in the body" — where you soften/expand vs. feel strained/blocked

#### Method 4: Conception Chart Moon Degree Swap Method — NEW Perspective

**Source**: Comment by Robin F Pool on Angelorum article (Jan 23, 2026)
**Original Presenter**: Cameron Cassidy (omegaastrology.com), presented at Big Apple Astrology Meetup

**Procedure**:
1. Find a conception chart approximately 9 months before the day of birth
2. The conception chart must satisfy: Ascendant = degree of the natal Moon; Moon = sign of the natal ascendant (plus or minus some tolerance)
3. The Moon degree of the conception chart becomes the new natal Ascendant degree

**Historical Note**: Ancient astrologers developed this because they lacked accurate clocks for minute-level birth times, yet needed precise Ascendants for Lots and other mathematical techniques.

**Philosophical Note**: This method produces a "metaphysical" birth time — the moment when the child decides to separate from the mother — not necessarily the biological moment of birth.

**Application**: Useful for rectifying birth times that are rounded or approximate within a 1-2 hour window.

**Uniqueness vs. Existing KB**: The Prenatal Epoch/EPUJ method IS already in the knowledge base (prenatal-epoch.md, general-btr.md, kp-method.md). However, this conception chart swap method differs in:
- It treats the conception chart as a complete horoscope (not just a Moon-Ascendant correspondence)
- The Moon degree of conception chart directly becomes the new Ascendant degree (not just a sign match)
- It explicitly mentions "plus or minus" tolerance for sign boundaries
- The "metaphysical birth time" framing is unique
- Origin source (Camer Cassidy / Big Apple Astrology) is a modern Western re-development
