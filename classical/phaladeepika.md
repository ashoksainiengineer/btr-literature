## 1. Dasha System & Timing

### 1.1 The Nine Mahadasas (Adhyaya XIX.2)
The planets presiding over the nine stars (counted from Krittika in groups of nine) and their respective years:
| Planet | Dasa Years |
|--------|-----------|
| Sun | 6 |
| Moon | 10 |
| Mars | 7 |
| Rahu | 18 |
| Jupiter | 16 |
| Saturn | 19 |
| Mercury | 17 |
| Ketu | 7 |
| Venus | 20 |

**Source:** Adhyaya XIX, Sloka 2

### 1.2 Dasa Balance Calculation (Adhyaya XIX.3)
At birth, find Ghatikas remaining for Moon in his star. Multiply by years allotted to star lord. Divide by 60. Quotient = years remaining. Remainder → months (×12÷60) → days (×30÷60).

**Source:** Adhyaya XIX, Sloka 3

### 1.3 Bhukti Calculation (Adhyaya XXI.2)
Multiply Mahadasa years × Antardasa planet years ÷ 120 = Bhukti years. Remainder × 12 ÷ 120 = months, remainder × 30 ÷ 120 = days. Same process for sub-sub periods. Order: first Bhukti = Dasa lord's own.

**Source:** Adhyaya XXI, Sloka 2

### 1.4 Solar Year Definition (Adhyaya XIX.4)
When Sun returns to same position in zodiac, one solar year. Used for Ududasa system.

**Source:** Adhyaya XIX, Sloka 4

## 2. Planetary Dasha Results

### 2.1 Sun's Dasa (Adhyaya XIX.5-6, 18)
**Badly placed:** Quarrel, displeasure of sovereign, sickness among relatives, wandering, mental anguish, danger from fire to concealed wealth, grain, sons, wife. Money-making by cruel acts, trouble from fire/beasts, inflammation of eyes, stomach/teeth illness, separation from revered seniors/parents, destruction of servants.

**Well placed:** Wealth through cruel deeds, journeys, kings, quarrels, roaming forests/mountains, wide fame, success in business, attachment to duty, happiness.

**Source:** Adhyaya XIX, Slokas 5-6, 18

### 2.2 Moon's Dasa (Adhyaya XIX.7-8, 19)
Tranquillity of mind, success in all business, good food, wife, children, ornaments, clothes, gems, cows, lands. Honours Brahmins. Money through sacred prayers, Gods, Brahmins, royal favour. Profusion of flowers, clothes, ornaments, perfumes. Loss of money through wicked.

**Strength check:** First 10 days of lunar month = moderate; middle 10 days = full strength (very auspicious); last 10 days = waning strength.

**Source:** Adhyaya XIX, Slokas 7-8, 19

### 2.3 Mars' Dasa (Adhyaya XIX.9, 20)
Wealth through fire, king, fighting. Money by administering medicines, falsehood, cheating. Bilious complaints, impurity of blood, fever. Intrigues with low females. Quarrels with wife, children, relations. Acquisition of cattle/lands through litigation. Suffering from bile, blood, fire diseases.

**Source:** Adhyaya XIX, Slokas 9, 20

### 2.4 Mercury's Dasa (Adhyaya XIX.10, 24)
Meeting of friends, personal comforts, fame through learned, favour from elders, cleverness in speech, doing good to others, happiness to wife/children/friends, exalted position. Money through Brahmins, acquisition of lands, cows, horses. Diseases from wind, heat, phlegm.

**Source:** Adhyaya XIX, Slokas 10, 24

### 2.5 Jupiter's Dasa (Adhyaya XIX.11, 22)
Virtuous deeds, acquisition of children, honoured by sovereign, praised by great men, elephants/horses/vehicles, meeting of wife/children/friends, fulfillment of desires. New clothes, attendants/retinue, respect/dignity. Separation from elders, ear disease, phlegmatic troubles.

**Source:** Adhyaya XIX, Slokas 11, 22

### 2.6 Venus' Dasa (Adhyaya XIX.12, 26)
Materials for sport/happiness, vehicles, cows, gems, ornaments, treasure, enjoyment with young damsels, intellectual pursuits, voyage, auspicious deeds, acceptance of presents from sovereign. Augmentation of wealth by trade, agriculture, voyages. Anxiety, mental anguish, separation of parents/elders.

**Source:** Adhyaya XIX, Slokas 12, 26

### 2.7 Saturn's Dasa (Adhyaya XIX.13, 23)
Wife/children troubled by gout/rheumatism/windy diseases, loss in agriculture, evil talk, wicked women, desertion of servants, absence from residence, destruction of relations/lands/happiness/wealth. Acquisition from country trouble/fighting, servants/old women, cows/buffaloes/bulls. Windy/phlegmatic diseases, piles, burning sensation in limbs.

**Source:** Adhyaya XIX, Slokas 13, 23

### 2.8 Rahu's Dasa (Adhyaya XIX.14-16, 21)
Trouble from sovereign, thieves, poison, fire, weapons. Sickness to children, ruffled mind, loss of relations, insult from base people, scandal, ejection from office, wounds on leg, business failure.

**If associated with benefic in good houses:** Fully auspicious, raises native to affluence like a king, desires fulfilled, wealth accumulation, widely renowned.

**In Kanya/Meena/Vrischika:** Honor/happiness, lordship of lands/carriages/servants (loss at conclusion of Dasa).

**Source:** Adhyaya XIX, Slokas 14-16, 21

### 2.9 Ketu's Dasa (Adhyaya XIX.17, 25)
Troubles from enemies, thieves, sovereign, weapons, wounds, disease from excessive heat, false calumny, family stigma, fire danger, absence from country. Sorrow/confusion from females, trouble from rich, loss of wealth, improper acts, banishment. Toothache, leg pain, phlegmatic troubles.

**Source:** Adhyaya XIX, Slokas 17, 25

### 3.3 Key Principles (Adhyaya XX.21-24)
- Whatever stated in Adhyaya I (definitions), II (planets), V (profession), XIV (diseases), XVIII (conjunctions), XV-XVII (bhavas) must be assigned to planets in their respective Dasas (XX.21)
- A planet in Vargottamamsa gives favourable results; mixed if eclipsed or in depression (XX.22)
- Dasa and Apahara of planets owning/occupying 6th, 8th, 12th = unfavourable (XX.22)
- During malefic's Dasa, sub-periods of 3rd/5th/7th star lord from natal star or Janma Rasi lord / 8th lord bring trouble (XX.23)
- Fatal Dasas: 4th = Saturn, 6th = Jupiter, 5th = Mars/Rahu; also planet at end of sign or owning Dusthana (XX.24)

**Source:** Adhyaya XX, Slokas 21-24

### 3.4 Retrograde & Position Types (Adhyaya XX.56-57)
- **Avarohini (descending):** Planet fallen from exaltation
- **Madhya (middling):** Planet in friend's or exaltation house
- **Arohini (rising):** Planet proceeding from depression
- **Adhama (worst):** Planet in depression/inimical Rasi or Amsa
- **Misra (mixed):** Eclipsed/in-depression planet in auspicious sign — good in latter half

**Source:** Adhyaya XX, Slokas 56-57

## 6. General Bhava Effects (Adhyaya XVI)

### 6.1 Lagna (Adhyaya XVI.1-4)
- Native corresponds in mein to lord of rising Navamsa
- Hue = lord of Navamsa occupied by Moon
- Body proportions = rising sign
- Lord of Lagna in Kendra/Kona, uneclipsed, in exaltation/Swakshetra + 8th lord not in Kendra + Lagna occupied by benefic = long-lived, rich, honoured, good qualities, praised by kings, fortunate, fearless
- Lord of Lagna well-associated: good village/association; strong planet = patron of renowned king; exaltation = king; own house = native place; moveable = always on legs; fixed = stationary; dual = mixed
- Lord of brilliant rays: famous; well-placed = happy/prosperous; in Dusthana/malefic/enemy/depression = miserable, despicable place
- Lagna strong: happy, thrives, power/prominence; Lagna weak: calamities, sad, disease

### 6.2 2nd House (Adhyaya XVI.5-6)
- Lord of 2nd in Lagna + benefics in 2nd = best qualities, prosperous family, rich, affable, far-sighted
- Associated with Sun: utilizes knowledge/wealth for humanity
- Associated with Saturn: insignificant learning
- Associated with Jupiter: proficient in sacred scriptures
- Associated with Mercury: clever in politics
- Associated with Venus: expert in amorous topics
- Malefics in 2nd: fool, without wealth

### 6.3 3rd House — Brothers (Adhyaya XVI.7-9)
- Lord of 3rd and Lagna in mutual exchange + strong = brave, valorous, helpful to brothers, daring acts
- Lord of 3rd strong + associated with benefic + Mars (Karaka) strong in benefic house = brothers' prosperity
- Both Karaka and 3rd lord in odd Rasis + aspected by Jupiter/Sun/Mars + 3rd odd = number of brothers from Navamsa

### 6.4 4th House — Mother (Adhyaya XVI.10-14)
- Lord of 4th + Moon both in Dusthana + no benefic aspect/conjunction or hemmed between malefics = mother's death
- Both strong + associated with/aspected by benefics + benefics in 4th = mother's happiness
- Lord of Lagna in 4th or 4th lord in Lagna + Moon aspected by either = native performs mother's funeral rites
- Lord of 4th + Venus well-placed in Lagna and 11th = palanquin vehicle, lordship, kingship, gold/silk/jewels/ornaments etc.
- Lord of 4th in Dusthana or with Sun/Mars or 4th occupied by Mars/Sun = house burnt
- Rahu/Saturn in 4th with lord = house old/dilapidated
- 4th or its lord by inimical planet = cows/lands/vehicles usurped by enemies

### 6.5 5th House — Intelligence & Children (Adhyaya XVI.15-16)
- 5th in Mercury's Rasi/Amsa or occupied by Mercury = intelligent, open-hearted
- Lord of 5th well-placed in Vaiseshikamsa = same
- Malefics in 5th, lord of Lagna with/aspect by strong 5th lord, lord of Lagna in 5th, lord of 5th in Kendra with/aspect by malefics = constant intense annoyance from enemies

### 6.6 6th House — Enemies (Adhyaya XVI.17-19)
- Lord of Lagna stronger than 6th lord + in benefic Rasi/Amsa + aspected by benefic + 4th lord strong in Kendra/Kona = hale/healthy, strong constitution, all comforts
- Lord of 6th in Dusthana/depression/inimical/eclipsed + lord of Lagna stronger + Sun in 9th = destruction of enemies
- Bhavas owned by planet with 6th lord, occupied by 6th lord, and owned by planet in 6th = prove inimical to native

### 6.7 7th House — Spouse (Adhyaya XVI.20)
- 7th connected with benefic + lord strong = wife virtuously disposed, blessed with children, all good qualities

### 6.8 8th House — Longevity (Adhyaya XVI.21)
- Lord of 8th in non-Kendra + weaker than Lagna lord = long-lived, free from anxieties/obstacles/miseries

### 6.9 9th House — Father (Adhyaya XVI.22-26)
- Mars/Sun in 9th + 9th lord in Dusthana/betwixt malefics = father's demise soon after birth (or in Sun/Mars Dasa)
- Sun (day-birth) or Saturn (night-birth) well-placed + aspected by benefics + 9th lord strong = father long-lived
- Sun/Moon in trine to Saturn/Mars = child abandoned by parents; if aspected by Jupiter = long-lived and happy
- Saturn owning 9th in moveable + unaspected by benefics OR Sun in Dusthana = child lives under foster-father
- 9th/its lord in moveable + with/aspect by Saturn + 12th lord strong = child adopted

### 6.10 10th House — Career (Adhyaya XVI.27-28)
- Benefic in 10th + lord strong in Kendra/Kona in Swakshetra/exaltation OR lord of Lagna in 10th = revered by all, widely renowned, virtuous, king-like affluence, long-lived
- Sun/Mars in 10th = great/mighty, liked by people; if 10th lord well-placed = great undertakings with valour/heroism
- Benefics in 10th = beneficial acts commended by good
- Rahu/Saturn/Ketu in 10th = sinful/wicked acts

### 6.11 11th House — Gains (Adhyaya XVI.29)
- Bhava owned by planet with 11th lord + Bhava occupied by 11th lord = acquisition of connected things
- Planets occupying 11th = similar gains

### 6.12 12th House — Loss (Adhyaya XVI.30)
- Bhava owned by planet in 12th + Bhava occupied by 12th lord = loss of connected things

### 6.13 Timing of Bhava Effects (Adhyaya XVI.31-32)
Success/acquisition of a Bhava when:
1. Lord of Lagna transits Trikona to Rasi/Amsa of Bhava lord
2. Lord of Lagna transits the Bhava itself
3. Bhava lord transits triangle to lord of Lagna's Rasi/Amsa
4. Bhava lord transits Lagna itself
5. Two lords conjoin or aspect each other
6. Or when Karaka of Bhava conjoins lord of Rasi containing Lagna/Moon
7. When Jupiter transits trine to Rasi/Amsa of Bhava lord

**Source:** Adhyaya XVI, Slokas 31-32

## 7. Bhava Strength & Annihilation (Adhyaya XV)

### 7.1 Complete Good Effects (Adhyaya XV.1)
All Bhavas produce completely good results when:
- Occupied or aspected by benefics, their own lords, or planets owning benefic Bhavas
- Free from association/aspect of malefics
- Not in depression, not eclipsed, not in inimical signs

### 7.2 Strength Determination (Adhyaya XV.2)
A Bhava is strong/thoroughly beneficent when Trikona, 2nd, 4th, 7th, 10th from it are occupied by auspicious planets or its lord, unoccupied/unaspected by malefics.
- Suffers decay when positions differently occupied/aspected
- Mixed when positions occupied/aspected by benefics AND malefics

### 7.3 Total Destruction Conditions (Adhyaya XV.3-6)
A Bhava suffers total destruction when:
- Its lord in 8th, or eclipsed, or in depression, or in inimical house, with no benefic aspect/association
- Malefics in 6th/8th/12th from the Bhava
- Lord in 6th/8th/12th, or occupied by lord of 6th/8th/12th
- Bhava, its lord AND its Karaka all weak, hemmed by malefics, or with malefic aspect/association
- 4th/8th/12th or 5th/9th from it occupied by malefics

### 7.4 Destructive Planets in Dasa (Adhyaya XV.7-8)
Planets causing destruction during their Dasas:
1. Lord of 8th from Bhava
2. Lord of 22nd Drekkana from Bhava
3-5. Weak planets in 6th, 8th, or 12th from Bhava

**Success-bringing planets:** Malefics in 3rd/6th/11th, benefics in Kendra/Trikona from Bhava, planets friendly to Bhava lord — if strong.

Additional destructive planets: lords of 3rd from Lagna & Moon, planet occupying/aspecting 8th, Saturn, lord of 22nd decanate, planet owning sign of Mandi, Rahu when weak in untoward houses.

### 7.5 Lord of Lagna's Role (Adhyaya XV.9-10)
- Lord of Lagna promotes the Bhava it occupies
- Note houses owned by planet associated with/aspecting Lagna lord — those Bhavas' effects are pushed by Lagna lord
- If Bhava lord in Dusthana: if weak = immensely harmful; if strong = slight injury
- Malefic Lagna lord promotes growth of Bhava it occupies; if also owns Dusthana, Lagna ownership predominates

### 7.6 Dual-Ownership Priority (Adhyaya XV.11)
- Moolatrikona house effects predominate, own house effects half
- First half of Dasa = effects of first-in-order house
- Odd house planet = odd house effects first; even house planet = even house effects first

### 7.7 Bhavasandhi (Adhyaya XV.13-14)
- Even if exalted/friendly/6-strengths, if planet in Bhavasandhi = ineffective
- Planets at exact degrees of a Bhava produce full effect
- Planets in Bhavasandhi produce no effect
- Intermediate positions: effect by rule-of-three

### 7.8 Karakas of the Bhavas (Adhyaya XV.17)

| Bhava | Karaka |
|-------|--------|
| 1st (Lagna) | Sun |
| 2nd | Jupiter |
| 3rd | Mars |
| 4th | Moon & Mercury |
| 5th | Jupiter |
| 6th | Saturn & Mars |
| 7th | Venus |
| 8th | Saturn |
| 9th | Sun & Jupiter |
| 10th | Jupiter, Sun, Mercury & Saturn |
| 11th | Jupiter |
| 12th | Saturn |

### 7.9 Benefic/Malefic in Houses (Adhyaya XV.18-19)
- Benefics in any house generally promote prosperity
- Malefics in any house work decay
- **Reversed for 6th, 8th, 12th:** Malefics in these promote evil effects; benefics cause destruction of said Bhavas

### 7.10 Karaka-based Analysis (Adhyaya XV.20-26)
Treat the sign occupied by the Karaka as Lagna for that relative:
- **Father:** Sun's position as Lagna
- **Mother:** Moon's position as Lagna
- **Brother:** Mars' position as Lagna
- (Similarly for all relatives via their respective Karakas)

When Bhava, its lord AND its Karaka are all strong = good effects.

But: Sun in 9th, Moon in 4th, Mars in 3rd, Jupiter in 5th, Venus in 7th, Saturn in 8th cause distress to respective Bhavas.

### 7.11 Lagna Lord's Dual Effect (Adhyaya XV.27-29)
- Lagna lord produces effects of Bhava whose lord he conjoins, or the one he occupies
- Bhava/lord strong = goodwill; weak = untoward
- More benefic dots in Lagna lord's Ashtakavarga = happy effects
- Fewer dots = adverse effects
- If Dusthana lord occupies own other house = only that house's effects (not Dusthana's)
- Example: Saturn in Makara (5th) = acquisition of sons, not 6th house effects

### 7.12 Five Planetary Connections (Adhyaya XV.30)
1. Mutual exchange of signs (Parivartana)
2. Conjunction (same house)
3. Mutual aspect
4. Kendra position (90° apart)
5. Trikona position (120° apart)

## 9. Maharajayogas (Adhyaya VII)

### 9.1 Basic Rajayogas (Adhyaya VII.1-5)
- 3+ planets in exaltation/Swakshetra in Kendras = widely renowned King
- 5+ such = ruler of world even if low-born
- Royal family birth + no Duryoga + planets not eclipsed + 3+ in Kendras in own/exaltation = King
- Even single planet in depression but brilliant/retrograde/in auspicious house = King's equal
- 2-3 such = King
- 2+ planets with Digbala = King (royal family); 4-5 (excluding Saturn) = King (ordinary family)
- Vargottama Navamsa rising in Lagna OR Moon in Vargottama + Lagna aspected by 4 planets (excluding Moon) = King even if low-born

### 9.2 Specific Rajayogas (Adhyaya VII.6-25)
- Lord of Lagna in Kendra/9th in Vargottama + 9th lord in exaltation/Swakshetra in Vargottama = King in golden vehicle on elephant
- Full Moon aspected by planet in exaltation/Swakshetra = King
- Full Moon in Kendra (not Lagna) = King with elephants/horses
- Venus in Aswini in Lagna + aspected by 3+ planets = King destroying enemies
- Mars in Mesha/Simha/Dhanus Lagna + aspected by friendly planet = ruler of Earth
- Sun in centre of Dhanus + Moon there + Saturn in Lagna + Mars in exaltation = mighty King
- Full Moon in Sun's Navamsa + benefics in Kendras unassociated with malefics = King with elephants
- Jupiter/Mercury/Venus/Moon in 9th with bright rays + aspected by/associated with friendly planets = great King worshipped like deity
- Moon in water-resorting Rasi/Amsa in Lagna or own/benefic Varga = King doing good to subjects
- Single planet at highest exaltation + aspected by friendly planet = King
- 4 Rajayogas: (1) Malefics in 3rd/6th/11th from Lagna lord or Janma Rasi; (2) Mars+Mercury in 2nd; (3) Sun+Venus in 4th; (4) Mars+Saturn+Jupiter in 10th/11th/Lagna

### 9.3 Neechabhanga Rajayogas (Adhyaya VII.26-30)
Five types when planet in depression becomes a King/emperor:
1. Lord of depression sign OR planet's exaltation lord in Kendra from Lagna/Moon
2. Lord of depression sign AND exaltation lord mutually in Kendra
3. Depressed planet aspected by lord of that sign
4. Lord of depression sign OR exaltation lord in Kendra from Lagna/Moon (cf #1)
5. Depressed planet itself in Kendra from Lagna/Moon

- Saturn/Mars in Meena (7th) = same
- Saturn+Mars in Karkataka (7th) = chaste, fortunate, beautiful wife

## 12. Longevity & Death (Adhyaya XIII, XIV, XVII)

### 12.1 Longevity Determination (Adhyaya XIII.1-25)
- Longevity = first thing to ascertain
- Child under 12: life cannot be calculated (Balarishta considerations)
- Balarishta, Yogarishta defined
- Alpayus (short), Madhyamayus (medium), Purnayus (full) — three divisions
- Maximum human life = 120 years (some say 100 in Kaliyuga)
- Yogas counteracting Arishta and securing long life
- Ayus prediction after examining Dasas, Ashtakavargas, Kalachakra Dasas

### 12.2 Death Timing via Transit (Adhyaya XVII.1-28)
Multiple methods using Saturn, Jupiter, Sun, Moon transits:

**Saturn transit death indicators:**
- Transits Rasi/Navamsa of 8th lord, Gulika, Saturn, or 22nd decanate lord
- Transits triangular positions to these
- Transits Rasi/Navamsa of Mandi + Saturn difference
- Transits Rasi 7th from Gulika (day-birth) or triangular to Gulika (night-birth)
- Transits Rasi/Amsa he occupied at birth or its trines
- Transits Rasi so far removed from Mandi as Mandi is from 8th lord

**Jupiter transit death indicators:**
- Transits Rasi/Amsa of rising decanate lord, 8th lord, or 22nd decanate lord
- Transits Rasi/Navamsa from Lagna+Sun+Mandi aggregate
- Transits Rasi from Jupiter+Rahu sum

**Sun transit death indicators:**
- Transits his own Dwadasamsa Rasi
- Transits Navamsa of 8th lord or Lagna lord

**Moon transit death indicators:**
- Transits Rasi/Navamsa of 8th lord or Sun

### 12.3 Specific Relative's Death (Adhyaya XVII.6-8)
- **Brother:** Mars - Rahu difference -> Jupiter transit
- **Father:** Yamakantaka - Sun difference -> Jupiter transit; Mandi - Yamakantaka -> Saturn transit
- **Mother:** Sun - Moon difference -> Jupiter transit; Mandi - Moon difference -> Saturn transit
- **Son:** Yamakantaka - 5th star lord difference -> Jupiter transit

### 12.4 Month & Lagna of Death (Adhyaya XVII.11-12)
- Lagna+Mandi aggregate = Rasi/Navamsa for month of death (Sun's arrival)
- Moon at death = Rasi from Mandi+Moon
- Rising sign = Lagna+Mandi+Moon aggregate

### 12.5 Maraka Houses (Adhyaya XX.40)
- 2nd and 7th houses = Maraka (death-producing) houses
- If their lords or planets therein are powerful = cause death during their Dasas
- If 8th or 12th lord very weak = death during their Bhukti

### 12.6 Cause of Death (Adhyaya XIV.12-20)
Determined by planets occupying specific houses and their nature.

## 17. Timing Principles (Adhyaya XVII, XX)

### 17.1 Dasa/Timing for Bhava Effects (Adhyaya XX.44)
Find all planets related to the Dasa lord; manifest effects in Antardasas of those planets.

### 17.2 Kendra-Trikona Exchange (Adhyaya XX.42, 45-49)
- Lord of Trikona in Kendra OR lord of Kendra in Trikona = auspicious
- During their Bhuktis in each other's Dasas = helpful, auspicious
- Kendra lord + Trikona lord even if individually evil = powerful to produce Yoga and prosperity
- If Trikona lord related to strong Kendra lord = Yogakaraka
- Yogakaraka's Dasa + Karaka Bhuktis = Rajayoga begins

### 17.3 Transit Activation (Adhyaya XX.34-38)
- Dasa lord transiting own/exaltation/friendly = promotes Bhava prosperity (if strong at birth)
- Weak/eclipsed/depression/inimical at birth + transit = total Bhava destruction
- Moon in Dasa lord's exaltation/friendly/3rd/6th/10th/11th/Trikona/7th = happy effects
- Bhukti lord transiting own/exaltation/retrograde = good; depression/inimical/eclipsed = misery
- Good Bhukti effects manifest when Sun enters planet's exaltation sign

### 17.4 Death Timing (Adhyaya XX.58)
Death of relative signified by a Bhava happens in Dasa of planet in 12th from that Bhava OR its lord — whichever weaker.

### 17.5 Dasa Auspiciousness (Adhyaya XX.59)
Dasa proves auspicious when:
- Dasa lord transits Lagna, 3rd, 6th, 10th, or 11th from it
- Dasa lord in Sapthavarga places of Lagna
- Friendly planet or benefic comes to Lagna

### 17.6 Moon-Based Timing (Adhyaya XX.60-62)
Effects determined by house from Moon that Dasa lord occupies in transit.
- Moon in friend's house, exaltation, 7th, Trikona, Upachaya of Dasa lord = beneficial
- Moon in these favourable positions promotes prosperity of Bhava represented by Rasi occupied

## 20. Special Rules for BTR

### 20.1 Bhavasandhi Sensitivity (Adhyaya XV.13-14)
Planets in Bhavasandhi (junction between two bhavas) become ineffective regardless of strength. This is critical for BTR — if a key planet falls at a bhava cusp, its effects are nullified.

### 20.2 Degree-Based Bhava Effects (Adhyaya VIII.34-35)
A planet produces full effect when its distance from the start of its Rasi equals the Lagna's distance in the Lagna Rasi. Planets at exact Bhava centre (Bhavamsa) produce full effects. This is a precise timing tool for BTR.

### 20.3 Lord of Lagna's Dual Role (Adhyaya XV.9-10)
Lagna lord promotes the Bhava it occupies AND the Bhavas associated with/aspecting it. If Lagna lord is malefic owning a Dusthana, the Lagna ownership predominates.

### 20.4 Karaka Principle (Adhyaya XV.20-26)
For relatives: treat Karaka's sign as Lagna and read 12 houses from it. This allows verifying BTR by cross-checking relative events.

### 20.5 Dasa-Bhukti Verification (Adhyaya XX.29)
Bhukti effects determined by Bhava counted from Dasa lord that Bhukti lord occupies. If Bhukti lord in 6th/8th/12th from Dasa lord = unhappy; otherwise = good.

### 20.6 Planet Strength Hierarchy (Adhyaya XX.30)
Good influence: max at exaltation → ¾ at Moolatrikona → ½ at Swakshetra → ¼ at friendly → minimum at inimical → nil at depression.
For benefics/lords of benefic houses. Reverse for malefics. Combustion = depression effect.

### 20.7 Fatal Planet Identification (Adhyaya XX.31)
Among Saturn, Mandi, Rahu, 22nd decanate lord, 8th lord, and their Navamsa lords — find weakest. That planet's Dasa proves fatal. Death when Saturn transits untoward house (6th/8th/12th).

### 20.8 Dasa Lord Transit Activation (Adhyaya XX.34)
Dasa lord transiting own/exaltation/friendly house promotes the Bhava it represents (counted from Lagna) — provided the planet was strong at birth.
