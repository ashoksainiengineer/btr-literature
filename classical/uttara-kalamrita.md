## 1. Time of Birth & Nakshatra Determination

### Sloka 4 (Section I, Khanda I)
> Exact time of birth in terms of Ghatikas etc., that have elapsed since sunrise, and gnomonic shadow indicated by instruments and other appliances, should first be ascertained. Then duration of day and night on the day of birth in question. From correct position of Surya, with respect to first point of Mesha, Lagna should be calculated. Next should be ascertained through Guru's blessings exact positions of several Grahas in Zodiac at the time as revealed by Drig system of calculations. Then Bhavas with their Sandhis. All these should be determined with a clear intellect by intelligent Astrologer.

### Sloka 5 — Nakshatra (Birth Star) Determination

**Formula:**

```
Nakshatra = (Ghatikas_since_sunrise × 4) ÷ 9
```

- Multiply Ghatikas and Vighatikas that have elapsed since sunrise by **4**
- Divide the product by **9**
- The **remainder** represents the number of the asterism (birth star), reckoned from groups of nine beginning with **Aswini**, **Magha**, or **Moola** as the case may be

**Example:** Birth at 53¾ Ghatikas after sunrise on Friday, 23 July 1875.
- 53¾ × 4 = 215 → 215 ÷ 9 = 23 remainder **8**
- Remainder 8 → Nakshatra = **Pushya**
- Anujanma (2ndary star) = **Anuradha**
- Trijanma (3rdary star) = **Uttarabhadra**
- Actual Janma Nakshatra of native was Uttarabhadra

## 2. Sex Verification via Vighatikas

### Sloka 5 (alternative method) & Sloka 6

**Formula:**

```
Vighatikas_since_sunrise ÷ 225
Group remainder into: 15 - 30 - 45 - 60 - 75
```

**Step 1:** Convert Ghatikas elapsed since sunrise to Vighatikas (× 60)

**Step 2:** Divide by 225 (Tatva = 25, Aswin = 2 → 25 × 9 = 225)

**Step 3:** Group the remainder into five groups:

| Remainder Range | Sex |
|----------------|-----|
| 0 to 15 | **Male** |
| >15 to <45 | **Female** |
| 45 to <90 | **Male** |
| 90 to <150 | **Female** |
| 150 to <225 | **Male** |

**Five groups represent in order:** Male, Female, Male, Female, Male

**Example:** 53¾ Ghatikas × 60 = 215 × 15 Vighatikas
- 215 × 15 ÷ 225 = 14⅓ → falls within 1st group of 15 → **Male** ✓

## 3. Lagna Determination via Mandi & Chandra

### Sloka 6 (commentary)

**Method to deduce Lagna at birth:**

1. Find the **Rasi** (sign) and **Navamsa** occupied by **Mandi** (son of Saturn)
2. Find the **Rasi** and **Navamsa** occupied by **Chandra** (Moon)
3. **Lagna at birth** will correspond to the Rasi occupied by **Mandi or Chandra**, whichever is **stronger**
4. Alternatively, Lagna may be the **7th, 5th, or 9th** from either of the above two Rasis
5. If the **Navamsa Rasis** occupied by Mandi and Chandra are found to possess **greater strength** than their corresponding Rasis, then Lagna corresponds to any of the aforesaid Bhavas reckoned from the **stronger of the two Navamsa Rasis**

### Mantreswara's Alternative Method (from Phaladeepika III-16)

> "Note Rasi occupied by Mandi at birth and note also where Lord of that Rasi is posited. Lagna at birth will be a position **triangular** to that of aforesaid Lord or one triangular to Navamsa occupied by owner of Rasi representing Navamsa occupied by Gulika. When Chandra is strong, Lagna should be deduced similarly through Chandra alone instead of through Mandi."

The translator notes Mantreswara's view appears more correct.

## 4. Mandi & Gulika Calculation

### Sloka 7 — Mandi (Daytime)

**Formula for Mandi position during DAY:**

```
Duration_of_day_in_Ghatikas × {26, 22, 18, 14, 10, 6, 2} ÷ 30
```

These 7 multipliers correspond to the 7 weekdays from **Sunday onwards**:
- Sunday: ×26/30
- Monday: ×22/30
- Tuesday: ×18/30
- Wednesday: ×14/30
- Thursday: ×10/30
- Friday: ×6/30
- Saturday: ×2/30

The result gives the time in Ghatikas elapsed since sunrise of Mandi's exact position.

### Sloka 8 — Gulika

**Daytime Gulika:**
- Period of day divided by **8** → length of each part presided over by a Graha
- Lords of first 7 parts: counted from Lord of weekday in order: **Surya, Chandra, Mangala, Budha, Guru, Sukra, Sani**
- **8th (last) portion is lordless**
- **Sani's portion in each day is called Gulika**
- Gulika's exact position corresponds to **Lagna at the very end of Sani's Muhurta**

**Nighttime Gulika:**
- Lords of first 7 portions counted from **Lord of 5th weekday from the day chosen**
- Again, **Sani's Muhurta is Gulika**

### Mandi vs Gulika Distinction

Their exact positions on any weekday are NOT the same.

**Example (Saturday, day length = 30 Ghatikas):**
- Mandi's position = Lagna at **2 Ghatikas** after sunrise
- Gulika's position = Lagna at **3¾ Ghatikas** after sunrise

> Mandi and Gulika are declared by Sage Mandavya as "two villains" — they cause much injury in the Rasi they tenant.

## 5. Lying-in-Chamber & Birth Verification

### Sloka 9 — Lagna to Chamber Direction Mapping

| Lagna (Ascendant) | Chamber Direction |
|---|---|
| Kataka (Cancer), Mesha (Aries), Vrischika (Scorpio), Tula (Libra), Kumbha (Aquarius) | **East** |
| Simha (Leo), Makara (Capricorn) | **South** |
| Vrishabha (Taurus) | **West** |
| Kanya (Virgo), Dhanus (Sagittarius), Mithuna (Gemini), Meena (Pisces) | **North** |

Place of birth will usually correspond to **Lagna or its Navamsa**, whichever is stronger.

### Sloka 10 — Father's Presence at Birth

When Chandra does NOT aspect Lagna:
- **Surya in moveable Rasi** → father absent in another village
- **Surya in immoveable Rasi** → father in his own village
- **Surya in dual Rasi** → father on his way home
- AND Surya must be in 9th or 8th Rasi
- If Chandra is in conjunction with malefics → much distress to mother during delivery

### Sloka 11 — Number of Attendants & Details

- **Females attending** = number of Grahas between rising Rasi and Chandra
- **Those outside** = number of Grahas away from these two limits
- **Oil in lamp** = portion of Rasi yet to be traversed by Chandra
- **Wick of lamp** = portion of rising Rasi still below the horizon
- **Character of lamp** = nature of Rasi occupied by Surya (moveable/fixed/dual)
- **Door direction** = determined by strongest Graha in Kendra position

**Lords of Quarters:**
| Direction | Lord |
|-----------|------|
| East | Surya |
| Southeast | Sukra |
| South | Mangala |
| Southwest | Rahu |
| West | Sani |
| Northwest | Chandra |
| North | Budha |
| Northeast | Guru |

## 6. Shadbala — Sixfold Planetary Strength

### Sthanabala (Positional Strength) — Sloka 10½–11½

Five components of Sthanabala:
1. **Uchachabala** — Exaltation strength
2. **Saptavargajabala** — Division strength
3. **Kendra Bala** — Angular house strength
4. **Amsa Bala** — Odd/even sign & Navamsa strength
5. **Lingadi Bala** — Masculine/feminine/neuter placement

### Digbala (Directional Strength) — Sloka 11½–12½

| Planet | Strong Direction | Opposite (Weak) |
|--------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Budha (Mercury) & Guru (Jupiter) | **East** (Lagna) | West (7th) |
| Chandra (Moon) & Sukra (Venus) | **North** (4th) | South (10th) |
| Sani (Saturn) | **West** (7th) | East (Lagna) |
| Mangala (Mars) & Surya (Sun) | **South** (10th) | North (4th) |

Intermediate positions: calculated by **rule-of-three process**.

### Kalabala (Temporal Strength) — Sloka 12½–15½

**Pakshabala (Fortnight strength):**
- **Bright half (Sukla Paksha):** Benefic strength = Tithis passed; Malefic strength = Tithis remaining
- **Dark half (Krishna Paksha):** Reversed
- Chandra's Pakshabala is **doubled**

**Ahoratraja / Dinaratritribhagabala (Day-Night strength):**
| Time | Lord Getting Strength |
|------|---------------------|
| First portion of day | Budha |
| Middle portion of day | Surya |
| Last portion of day | Sani |
| First portion of night | Chandra |
| Middle portion of night | Sukra |
| Last portion of night | Mangala |
| All times | Guru |

**Natabala Formula:**
```
Natabala = (Nata_in_Ghatikas × 2) ÷ 60   [in Rupas]
```
- **Nata** = Ghatikas intervening between midday and Surya at birth
- **Unnata** = Ghatikas between Surya and midnight
- **Unnatabala** = 1 - Natabala
- Chandra, Mangala, Sani have **Natabala**
- Surya, Budha, Guru, Sukra have **Unnatabala**

**Natonnatabala Formula:**
```
Natonnatabala = Nata_or_Unnata ÷ 30   [in Rupas]
```

**Hora Lord & Strength — Sloka 15½–16½:**
```
Lord_of_Hora_number = (Ghatikas_elapsed_since_sunrise × 2) ÷ 5
```
- Quotient = number of Horas elapsed
- Lord of first Hora on any weekday = Lord of weekday itself
- Subsequent Horas: **Surya, Sukra, Budha, Chandra, Sani, Guru, Mangala** (in order)

Strength contributions:
- Lord of Year: ¼ Rupa
- Lord of Month: ½ Rupa
- Lord of Weekday: ¾ Rupa
- Lord of Hora: 1 Rupa

**Ayanabala (Course strength) — Sloka 16½–17½:**
- Chandra & Sani: strong in **Southern** course
- Budha: always strong
- Rest: strong in **Northern** course
- Retrogrades: strong via Cheshtabala

### Nisargabala (Natural Strength) — Sloka 19½–20½

| Planet | Natural Strength (Rupas) |
|--------|------------------------|
| Surya | 1.0 |
| Chandra | 6/7 ≈ 0.857 |
| Sukra | 5/7 ≈ 0.714 |
| Guru | 4/7 ≈ 0.571 |
| Budha | 3/7 ≈ 0.429 |
| Mangala | 2/7 ≈ 0.286 |
| Sani | 1/7 ≈ 0.143 |

Each successive planet decreases by exactly **1/7 of a Rupa**.

### Drigbala (Aspectual Strength) — Sloka 17½–19½

**Aspect Rules:**
| Planet | Full Aspect | Quarter/Half Aspects |
|--------|-------------|---------------------|
| Sani | 3rd, 10th | Others: ¼ glance |
| Guru | 5th, 9th | Others: ½ glance |
| Mangala | 4th, 8th | Others: ¾ glance |
| All Grahas | **7th** (full) | — |

**Grahas DO NOT aspect:** 2nd, 11th, 12th, and 6th Bhava from themselves.

**Drigbala Calculation:**
1. Subtract aspecting Graha from aspected one → extent in Rasis and degrees
2. Use **rule-of-three** with proportional difference in strength between the Rasi strength and the next Rasi's strength
3. Classify as **benefic** or **malefic** according to the aspecting Graha
4. Algebraic sum = exact Drigbala for each Graha

### Planetary War — Sloka 16½–17½
- Two Grahas in same Rasi with same longitude to degree and minute → **war**
- The one **North** of the other is **conqueror**
- Conqueror gains 1 Rupa, vanquished loses 1 Rupa

### Shadbala Total — Sloka 20½–21½

```
Shadbala = Sthanabala + Digbala + Kalabala + Nisargabala + Cheshtabala + Drigbala_rectified
```

**Strength thresholds:**
| Shadbala (Rupas) | Classification |
|------------------|---------------|
| < 5 | **Weak** — Dasa causes misery |
| 5 to 10 | **Normal** — Dasa productive of happiness |
| > 10 | **Purnabala (Full)** — Dasa exceedingly favorable |

**Plus planetary war adjustment:** +1 Rupa if victorious, −1 Rupa if vanquished.

## 7. Saptavargajabala

### Sloka 8½–9½

**Seven Vargas (divisions):**
1. **Rasi** (sign) — 30°
2. **Hora** (half-sign) — 15°
3. **Drekhana** (decanate) — 10°
4. **Saptamsa** (1/7) — ~4°17'
5. **Navamsa** (1/9) — 3°20'
6. **Dwadasamsa** (1/12) — 2°30'
7. **Trimsamsa** (1/30) — 1°

**Rasi Strength Values:**
- Exaltation: **1 Rupa**
- Moolatrikona: **¾ Rupa**
- Own Rasi: **½ Rupa**
- Dear Friend's Rasi: **⅜ Rupa**
- Friend's Rasi: **¼ Rupa**
- Neutral's Rasi: **⅛ Rupa**
- Inimical Rasi: **1/16 Rupa**
- Inveterate Enemy's Rasi: **1/32 Rupa**

**Saptavargajabala Calculation:**
> Enter against Rasi column strength in terms of Rupas already obtained for Rasis, and write out against other six Vargas, commencing from Hora, a **moiety (half)** of strength fixed in Rasi Varga for Graha owning Varga in question. The sum-total of these seven kinds of strength constitutes Saptavargajabala of Graha.

### Uchachabala (Exaltation Strength) — Sloka 9½–10½

**Formula (Aroha — approaching exaltation):**
```
Uchachabala = Degrees_from_depression_point / 1800   [in Rupas]
```
- Numerator: degrees between Graha's depression point and current position (× 60 for minutes)
- Denominator: 1800 (30° × 60)

**Formula (Avaroha — past exaltation, towards depression):**
```
Uchachabala = 1 - (Degrees_from_depression_point / 1800)
```

### Kendra Strength — Sloka 9½–10½
| Position | Strength |
|----------|---------|
| Kendra (1,4,7,10) | 1 Rupa |
| Panaphara (2,5,8,11) | ½ Rupa |
| Apoklima (3,6,9,12) | ¼ Rupa |

### Amsa Bala — Sloka 10½–11½
- Chandra and Sukra in **even** Rasi or even Navamsa: ¼ Rupa
- Surya, Mangala, Guru, Sani, Budha in **odd** Rasi or odd Navamsa: ¼ Rupa

### Lingadi Bala
- **Masculine** Grahas in initial portion of Rasi: ¼ Rupa
- **Hermaphrodite** in middle portion: ¼ Rupa
- **Feminine** in last portion: ¼ Rupa

## 8. Amsayurdaya — Longevity via Nakshatra Padas

### Sloka 1 (Section III) — Planetary Contribution

**Formula:**
```
Nakshatras_passed_by_Graha ÷ 3
→ Remainder × 4 = number of Navamsas (from Mesha) traveled by Graha
→ = Years contributed by Graha
```

**Step-by-step:**
1. Count the number of asterisms (Nakshatras) from Aswini that have been passed by the Graha
2. Divide this number by **3**, discard the quotient
3. Multiply the **remainder** by **4**
4. The product = number of Navamsas from Mesha already traveled = **years contributed**
5. For months/days: calculate from the interval between the end of the last Nakshatra Pada and the Graha's current position

### Sloka 3 — Lagna Ayurdaya

**Formula:**
```
Lagna_years = (Ghatikas_elapsed_in_Lagna_Rasi × 9) ÷ Rising_period_of_that_Rasi
```
- Remainder → months and days via proportional multiplication/division
- If Lagna is strong (Lord in/aspects Lagna, or Lord exalted) → years = Rasis counted from Mesha in Lagna figure

### Sloka 4 — Religious Merit from Decanates

1. Two decanates exist on each side of Lagna Drekhana (3 decanates total)
2. Find Saptavargajabalas (in Rupas) of the Lords of these 3 decanates
3. Take only **¼** → represents **Punya (religious merit)** of native in Rupas
4. Subtract from **4** → represents **Papa (evil)** portion

### Sloka 5 — Simplified Ayurdaya

```
Malefic_portion_of_Saptavargajabalas × 120 ÷ 7
```
- Result = years
- Remove as many 120-year cycles as possible
- Remainder (<120) = Ayus of native in the respective Janma (past, present, or future)

### Sloka 2 — Reductions

| Condition | Reduction |
|-----------|-----------|
| Malefic in 6 Bhavas backward from 12th | Whole / ½ / ⅓ / ¼ / ⅕ / ⅙ of Ayurdaya |
| Benefic in same positions | Half the malefic reduction |
| Malefic occupying Lagna | **Whole** Ayurdaya of Lagna lost |
| Graha in depression | Reduced by **half** |
| Graha in inimical Rasi | Loses **⅓** of Ayus |
| Eclipsed or defeated in war | Reduced by **half** further |
| Graha victorious in war | +1 Rupa |
| Graha vanquished in war | −1 Rupa |

### Sloka 6 — Life-span Indicators

| Condition | Longevity |
|-----------|-----------|
| Sukra, Guru, Budha in Kendra & Kona | **Long life** |
| Same in 2nd, 3rd, 11th | **Medium life (~60 years)** |
| Same in 6th, 8th, 12th | **Below medium** |
| Malefics in 8th, 12th, Kendra, Kona | Not productive of good |

### Sloka 7-8 — Short Life Yogas

**Alpayuryoga indicators:**
1. Lord of Lagna not aspected by benefics in Kendra/Kona
2. Owner of Rasi occupied by Lagna Lord not aspected by benefics
3. Lagna or Chandra Lagna aspected by **Lord of 8th Rasi**
4. Lord of Lagna has no benefic aspect
5. Lord of 9th from Lagna or Chandra Lagna aspected by **Sani or Mangala** without benefic aspect on Lagna Lord

## 9. Dasha System — Vimshottari Dasha

### Sloka 3 (Khanda II, Section VII)

**Dasa Years:**

| Planet | Dasa Years |
|--------|-----------|
| Surya (Sun) | **6** |
| Chandra (Moon) | **10** |
| Mangala (Mars) | **7** |
| Rahu | **18** |
| Guru (Jupiter) | **16** |
| Sani (Saturn) | **19** |
| Budha (Mercury) | **17** |
| Ketu | **7** |
| Sukra (Venus) | **20** |

**Starting Dasa Formula:**
```
Count_from_Krittika_to_natal_star_inclusive ÷ 9
Remainder → Lord of starting Dasa (counted from Surya onwards)
```

### Sloka 4 — Balance of Dasa at Birth

```
Balance_years = (Ghatikas_remaining_in_asterism ÷ Total_Ghatikas_of_asterism) × Dasa_years_of_Lord
Remainder × 12 = months
Further_remainder × 30 = days
```

### Sloka 5 — Bhukti (Sub-period) Calculation

```
Bhukti_months = (Dasa_years_of_Graha_A × Dasa_years_of_Graha_B)
```
- Product in 2-3 digits: first digit(s) = months, last digit × 3 = days

**Example:** Budha's Bhukti in Sani's Dasa = 17 × 19 = **323** → 32 months, 9 days

**Antara (Sub-sub period):**
```
Antara_Ghatikas = (Bhukti_days × Dasa_years_of_sub_lord) × ½
Antara_days = Antara_Ghatikas ÷ 60
```

## 10. Varshika Dasa — Solar Return Timing

### Sloka 33½–34½ (Khanda II)

**Solar Return Method:**
1. Find exact position of Surya at birth
2. In any required year, determine when Surya returns to that exact position → **solar year day**
3. Cast a horoscope for that exact time
4. Number of asterism where Chandra is at that time, reckoned from natal star ÷ 9 → order of Dasa from Surya

### Sloka 34½–36½ — Balance at Year Start

```
Balance_days = (Ghatikas_remaining_for_Chandra_in_asterism × Annual_Dasa_days) ÷ Total_asterism_Ghatikas
```

**Annual Dasa periods (for 365-day year):**
| Lord | Days |
|------|------|
| Surya | 110 |
| Chandra | 60 |
| Mangala | 32 |
| Budha | 40 |
| Guru | 48 |
| Sukra | 56 |
| Sani | 4 |
| Rahu | 5 |
| Lagna | 10 |

> "Exact time is required and a horoscope for that time should be cast accurately."

Dasa Lord order: Surya → Chandra → Mangala → Budha → Guru → Sukra → Sani → Rahu → Lagna

## 11. Lost Horoscope Reconstruction

### Sloka 4-5 (Khanda II)

**Method to reconstruct a lost horoscope using Guru's position:**

1. Number of flowers/money units handed over by querist × **basis number** ÷ 12
2. Remainder = Rasi (from Mesha) where **Guru** was posited in the nativity
3. Through Guru's position at query time, estimate the age and probable **Guru cycles** since birth
4. Months/days elapsed since last birthday = portion of Rasi traversed by Guru
5. Subtract total period from present Surya position to get **year, month, etc. of birth**

> "Month, weekday, Ghatikas etc. elapsed on the day, all will be solar. From this, Chandra's position, lunar years, month, Tithi etc., and hence whole horoscope can be obtained."

## 12. Prasna Methods for Verification

### Sloka 13 — Chandra Position at Query

```
Chandra_sukshma_position = (Ghatikas_from_sunrise_at_query × 9) ÷ 20
```

- **Sthula Chandra** = position from Ephemeris
- **Sukshma Chandra** = calculated position
- Their respective Navamsas should also be determined

### Sloka 18 — Timing of Event

Basis number divided by 5:
| Remainder | Time Unit |
|-----------|-----------|
| 1 | Ghatikas |
| 2 | Tithi |
| 3 | Weekday |
| 4 | Month |
| 5 | Year |

Add quotient to basis number, divide by 60, 30, 7, 12, 60 respectively → remainders = number of Ghatikas, Tithis, etc.

### Sloka 19 — Applying Aspects for Event Timing

- Lords of Lagna and Bhava representing query should have **applying aspect** (within 12°)
- Range = 0° → event happened at query time
- Range < 12° → event happens shortly
- Range > 12° → far future
