## Overview

Sarvartha Chintamani ("The Gem of All Desires/Thoughts") is one of the most important classical texts of predictive astrology. Unlike many works that follow the standard verse-by-verse format, this text employs a **unique methodology**: it first states the subject matter or objects pertaining to each house, then explains astrologically using:

1. **The house concerned** (Bhava)
2. **Its lord** (Bhava Lord)
3. **Its significator** (Karaka)
4. **Results** — good or bad — declared decisively based on the above

The text is famous for its exhaustive treatment of **Yoga formations** and their results, expanding greatly on Varahamihira's observations. B. Suryanarain Rao notes that Varahamihira dealt with Karmajeeva in merely four verses, whereas Venkatesa expands this subject extensively.

This extraction focuses on **Birth Time Rectification (BTR)** — rules concerning house significations, event timing, longevity determination, and dasha dynamics.

## 1. House Significations (Bhavartha) — BTR Event Verification

Sarvartha Chintamani's primary contribution to BTR is its **exhaustive house signification list**. Each house's results are stated in terms of:
- **Positive results** when the house, its lord, and karaka are strong
- **Negative results** when they are afflicted

### 1.1 Lagna (Ascendant) — Ch. 2

### 1.2 2nd House — Family & Wealth (Ch. 3)

**Subject Matter:** Wealth, family, speech, face, right eye, food, learning.

**BTR-Applicable Rules:**
- Lord of 2nd in Kendra/Kona with strength → wealthy family at birth
- Lord of 2nd in 6th/8th/12th or with malefics → financial struggles at birth
- 2nd lord in Lagna → wealth earned through self-effort
- Venus strong in 2nd → pleasant speech, artistic family

**Event Verification:** For wealth/inheritance/financial events, check that running dasha lord signifies the 2nd house.

### 1.3 3rd & 4th Houses — Siblings, Home, Mother (Ch. 4)

**3rd House — Siblings:**
- 3rd lord strong, in Kendra/Kona, with benefics → many siblings, harmonious relations
- 3rd lord in 6th/8th/12th → few siblings, conflicts
- Mars aspecting 3rd → courageous, multiple brothers
- Mercury in 3rd → skilled communicator, many siblings

**4th House — Mother, Home, Lands:**
- 4th lord strong and with benefics → happy mother, comfortable home
- Moon (karaka for mother) in 4th or well-placed → mother's well-being
- 4th lord in 6th/8th/12th → mother's ill-health, property disputes
- Jupiter in 4th → happy domestic life
- Saturn in 4th → mother's health issues, distant from birthplace

**BTR Application:** For mother-related events (death, health), verify dasha lord at event time signifies 4th house.

### 1.4 5th & 6th Houses — Children, Intelligence, Enemies (Ch. 5)

**5th House — Children, Intelligence:**
- 5th lord strong in Kendra/Kona with Jupiter → many children, high intelligence
- 5th lord in 6th/8th/12th → few or no children, obstacles in education
- Jupiter aspecting 5th → children's success
- Moon in 5th → fluctuating intellect, changeable mind
- Afflicted 5th → miscarriage, loss of children

**6th House — Enemies, Diseases:**
- 6th lord strong → ability to overcome enemies, good health
- Malefics in 6th → chronic diseases, many enemies
- 6th lord in Lagna → sickly constitution, litigations
- Sun in 6th → enemies in high places

**BTR Application:** For childbirth events, verify dasha lord at the time signifies 5th house. For health/disease events, verify 6th house connection.

### 1.5 7th House — Marriage, Partners, Travel (Ch. 6)

**Subject Matter:** Spouse, marriage, business partnerships, travel, sexual relationships, public dealings.

**Key Rules for Marriage Timing:**
- **7th lord in Kendra/Kona** with benefics → early and happy marriage
- **7th lord in 6th/8th/12th** → delayed, denied, or troubled marriage
- **Venus strong** in chart → timely marriage, good spouse
- **Jupiter aspecting 7th** → marriage through family arrangements
- **7th lord with Saturn** → delayed marriage (after 30+ years)
- **7th lord with Rahu** → unconventional marriage, inter-caste

**Travel Indications:**
- 7th lord in movable sign → frequent travels
- 7th lord in 12th → long-distance overseas travel
- Malefics in 7th → travel obstacles

**BTR Application:** Marriage timing is one of the strongest BTR anchors. The dasha lord at marriage must signify the 7th house. The rectified chart must show 7th lord strength consistent with known marriage age.

### 1.6 8th & 9th Houses — Longevity, Fortune, Overseas (Ch. 7)

**8th House — Longevity, Calamities:**
- **8th lord in Kendra/Kona with benefics** → long life (70+ years)
- **8th lord in 6th/8th/12th with malefics** → short life
- **8th lord in Lagna** → lifespan threatened in childhood
- **Saturn in 8th** → long life but with struggles
- **8th lord with Rahu** → sudden life changes, hidden enemies

**9th House — Fortune, Father, Pilgrimage:**
- 9th lord strong → fortunate, pious father
- 9th lord with malefics → father's difficulties
- Jupiter in 9th → great fortune, religious inclination
- Sun (karaka for father) well-placed → father's success

**BTR Application:** For death events, verify dasha lord signifies 8th house. For father events, verify 9th house connection.

### 1.7 10th, 11th & 12th Houses — Career, Gains, Loss (Ch. 8)

**10th House — Career, Father:**
- 10th lord in Kendra/Kona with benefics → successful career, high position
- 10th lord in 6th/8th/12th → career obstacles
- Sun in 10th → government service
- Saturn in 10th → laborious profession, late success
- Jupiter in 10th → teaching, advisory, spiritual profession

**11th House — Gains, Fulfillment:**
- 11th lord strong → substantial gains, fulfilled desires
- 11th lord with malefics → gains through struggle
- Venus in 11th → wealth through artistic pursuits

**12th House — Loss, Expenditure, Foreign Lands:**
- 12th lord strong → heavy expenditure, charity
- 12th lord with malefics → wasteful spending, legal troubles
- Jupiter in 12th → spiritual inclinations, foreign settlement

**BTR Application:** For career/promotion events, verify dasha lord signifies 10th/11th. For loss/relocation events, verify 12th house connection.

## 2. Raja Yogas (Ch. 9) — Status & Profession Verification

Sarvartha Chintamani provides the most exhaustive classification of Raja Yogas in any classical text. The key principle: **Raja Yogas form when lords of Kendra (1, 4, 7, 10) and Kona (1, 5, 9) conjoin or exchange.**

### Key Raja Yoga Rules:

| Lords Conjoining | Yoga Name | Result |
|-----------------|-----------|--------|
| 1st & 9th lords | Raja Yoga | Fame, wealth, authority |
| 4th & 10th lords | Raja Yoga | Land, vehicles, government position |
| 5th & 9th lords | Dharmakarmadhipati Yoga | Ethical leadership, fame |
| 1st & 7th lords | Raja Yoga (if both strong) | Public life, partnerships |
| 9th & 10th lords | Raja Yoga | High government position |
| 5th lord in 9th or 9th lord in 5th | Raja Yoga | Inherited status, family legacy |

**BTR Application:** Known professional status must be consistent with Raja Yogas present in the rectified chart.

## 3. Longevity Determination (Ch. 10-12) — CRITICAL for BTR

### 3.1 Three Classes of Longevity

| Type | Span | Indicator |
|------|------|-----------|
| Alpayu (Short Life) | 0-32 years | 8th lord with malefics in trika houses |
| Madhyayu (Medium Life) | 32-70 years | 8th lord average strength |
| Purnayu (Full Life) | 70-120 years | 8th lord strong in Kendra/Kona |

### 3.2 Pindayu Method (Ch. 10)
This is one of the primary longevity calculation methods described. It involves:
1. Computing the contribution of each planet based on its longitude
2. Adding the contributions to get total Ayurdaya
3. Converting to years based on prescribed formulas

### 3.3 Amsayurdaya Method
Based on the Navamsa positions of planets. Each planet's Navamsa contributes a specific number of years.

### 3.4 Balarishta (Infant Mortality) Rules (Ch. 11)

**Key combinations indicating danger to life in childhood:**
- Lagna lord in 6th/8th/12th with malefics
- Moon in 6th/8th/12th with malefics, waning
- 8th lord in Lagna with malefics
- Saturn + Mars in Lagna
- Sun + Saturn in Lagna

**Arishtabhanga (Cancellation) Rules:**
- A benefic in Lagna or aspecting Lagna
- Strong Jupiter aspecting the afflicted planet
- Lord of Lagna in own/exalted sign
- Many planets with even Navamsa Lords (indicating inner strength)

### 3.5 Medium Life (Madhyayu) Rules (Ch. 12)

When neither clear Alpayu nor Purnayu is indicated:
- 8th lord moderately placed
- Mixed Raja Yoga influences
- 8th lord aspected by both benefics and malefics

**BTR Application:** Lifespan class must be consistent with known lifespan. If the native lived to 80+, the rectified chart must show Purnayu indications.

## 4. Dasha Systems (Ch. 13-16) — Event Timing Verification

### 4.1 Vimshottari Dasha — Mahadasa Years

| Planet | Dasa Years |
|--------|-----------|
| Sun | 6 |
| Moon | 10 |
| Mars | 7 |
| Rahu | 18 |
| Jupiter | 16 |
| Saturn | 19 |
| Mercury | 17 |
| Ketu | 7 |
| Venus | 20 |

### 4.2 Dasha Balance Calculation (Ch. 13)
At birth, find the degrees remaining in Moon's Nakshatra. Multiply by the Dasa years of the Nakshatra lord. Divide by 120 (total Nakshatra Ghatis). The quotient and remainder give the balance.

### 4.3 Dasha Results by Planet

**Sun's Dasha (Ch. 13):**
- Well-placed: Government favor, authority, father's fortune, fame
- Badly placed: Fevers, eye troubles, conflict with authorities, father's illness

**Moon's Dasha:**
- Well-placed: Wealth, conveyances, mental peace, mother's fortune
- Badly placed: Mental instability, water-related troubles, mother's health issues

**Mars's Dasha (Ch. 14):**
- Well-placed: Land, property, courage, success in litigation
- Badly placed: Accidents, injuries, conflicts, surgery

**Mercury's Dasha:**
- Well-placed: Education, business success, communication gains
- Badly placed: Business losses, speech defects, worries

**Jupiter's Dasha (Ch. 15):**
- Well-placed: Wealth, children, spiritual growth, honors
- Badly placed: Financial losses, legal trouble, children's suffering

**Venus's Dasha:**
- Well-placed: Marriage, comforts, luxury, artistic success
- Badly placed: Marital problems, venereal disease, financial extravagance

**Saturn's Dasha:**
- Well-placed: Long life, authority, career success (after struggle)
- Badly placed: Chronic disease, poverty, isolation, delays

**Rahu's Dasha (Ch. 16):**
- Well-placed: Sudden rise, foreign connections, unconventional success
- Badly placed: Scandals, poisoning, imprisonment, confusion

**Ketu's Dasha:**
- Well-placed: Spiritual progress, liberation from material ties
- Badly placed: Accidents, theft, mysterious ailments, separation

### 4.4 Antardasha Principles (Ch. 16)

The order of Antardashas in each Mahadasa follows the standard Vimshottari sequence:
1. First Antardasha = Dasha lord's own
2. Subsequent Antardashas follow the Vimshottari sequence
3. Each Antardasha is further divisible into Pratyantardasha

**The dasha event principle in Sarvartha Chintamani:** Events occur when:
- The **Dasha lord** signifies the event's house(s)
- The **Bhukti lord** also signifies the event's house(s)
- Transit activates the combination

## 5. BTR-Specific Rules Summary

### 5.1 Event-to-House Mapping

| Life Event | Primary House | Secondary Houses | Significator |
|-----------|--------------|------------------|-------------|
| Birth | 1st (Lagna) | 5th | Jupiter |
| Mother's Death | 4th | 8th, 12th | Moon |
| Father's Death | 9th | 8th, 12th | Sun |
| Marriage | 7th | 2nd, 11th | Venus |
| Childbirth | 5th | 1st, 9th | Jupiter |
| Career Start | 10th | 1st, 11th | Sun, Mercury |
| Promotion | 10th | 11th, 2nd | Jupiter |
| Financial Gain | 11th | 2nd, 5th | Jupiter, Venus |
| Financial Loss | 12th | 6th, 8th | Saturn |
| Disease | 6th | 8th, 12th | Mars, Saturn |
| Surgery | 6th | 8th | Mars |
| Overseas Travel | 12th | 3rd, 7th | Ketu |
| Spiritual Event | 9th | 12th | Jupiter, Ketu |
| Accident | 8th | 6th, 12th | Mars, Saturn |

### 5.2 Timing Verification Rule

For any known life event at time T:
1. Determine the Dasha-Bhukti running at time T
2. The Dasha lord must **signify** the event's primary house
3. The Bhukti lord must **signify** either the primary or secondary house
4. The lord of the event house must be **functionally benefic** or well-placed
5. If the Dasha lord is **neutral** to the event house → the event occurs in Antardasha of a planet that DOES signify it

### 5.3 Strength Criteria for BTR

A rectified birth time is more credible when:
1. **Lagna Lord** is in a dignity consistent with native's known life trajectory
2. **House lords** for known major events are activated in the correct Dashas
3. **Karaka planets** for key relationships (Sun=father, Moon=mother, Jupiter=children, Venus=spouse) are placed consistently with known facts
4. **Longevity class** matches the native's actual lifespan

## 6. Unique Contributions of Sarvartha Chintamani to BTR

1. **Exhaustive House Significations**: More detailed than any earlier text — allows precise event-to-house mapping
2. **Yoga Classifications**: The most comprehensive Raja Yoga treatment — enables verification of social/professional status
3. **Dasha Phala (Results)**: Detailed result descriptions for each planet in Dasha — enables event-character verification (e.g., "was this Dasha character consistent with the event?")
4. **House Lord + Significator Method**: Unique dual-reference methodology that reduces ambiguity in event attribution
5. **Madhyayu Classification**: Explicit rules for medium lifespan — critical for BTR where lifespan is partially known
