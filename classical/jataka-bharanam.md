> **Jataka Bharanam** means "Ornament of Horoscopy." It is a comprehensive classical Sanskrit work on predictive astrology by Dhundiraja, covering the entire spectrum of Jyotisha from conception to death. The text is structured systematically, covering Samvatsara (60-year cycle), Ayana (solstice), Ritu (season), Masa (month), Paksha (fortnight), Tithi (lunar day), Vaara (weekday), Nakshatra (constellation), Yoga, Karana, Drekkana, Bhava Phala, Graha Phala, Yogas, Arishta, Dasha systems, Niryana (death timing), and Stri Jataka (female horoscopy).

# 1. Mangalacharana — Author's Statement on Birth Time

> कृत मया नोदकयन्त्रसाधनं कालेक्षणं चापि न शंकुसाधनम् ।
> परोपदिष्टात्समयात्र यज्न्तः शुभाशुभं जन्मफलं मयोच्यते ॥१४॥

**Translation:** For determining birth time (जन्मकाल), I have not used water-clock (yantra), nor observation of time (kāla-īkṣaṇa), nor gnomon-shadow (śaṅku-sādhana). Relying on the time told by others, I write this birth chart to describe the good and bad results of birth.

> **BTR Insight:** Dhundiraja explicitly states he relies on **reported birth time** (paropadiṣṭāt samayāt), acknowledging that BTR tools existed (water-clock, shadow measurement) but he works with available data — the same challenge modern BTR faces.

# 2. Samvatsara Phala — 60-Year Cycle Effects on Birth

The text lists all 60 Samvatsaras with their birth effects (प्रभवादि षष्ठिसंवत्सर फल). Each year gives specific physical and character traits at birth:

**Key Rule:** Birth in **Prabhava** — wealthy, many children, high intelligence, long-lived
**Key Rule:** Birth in **Vibhava** — enjoys all pleasures, beautiful, strong, learned
**Key Rule:** Birth in **Vilambī** (32nd) — lazy, extremely greedy, phlegmatic constitution, weak, argumentative
**Key Rule:** Birth in **Pramādī** — fraudulent, cruel, angry, miserable

> **BTR Application:** Cross-reference the reported birth year's Samvatsara with the native's known physical constitution and character traits.

### Moola Pada Effects (Sloka 25)
> मूलस्य पादत्रितये क्रमेण पितुर्मातुश्च धनस्य रिष्टम् ।

**BTR RULE:** Birth in **first 3 padas** of Moola → destruction of (in order) father, mother, and wealth. **4th pada** → highly auspicious. For **Ashlesha**, reverse the order.

**Critical BTR Application:** If native claims birth in Moola 1st pada, verify if father had misfortune. If Ashlesha 1st pada, it's actually auspicious.

### Abhuktamoola (Sloka 20-21)

**BTR RULE:** Last 2 ghatis of Jyeshtha + first 2 ghatis of Moola = **Abhuktamoola** (unoccupied Moola). Child born here faces death — father should not see child's face for 8 years.

### Moola Time-of-Day Effects (Sloka 27)

- **Daytime birth** → destroys father's clan

- **Night birth** → destroys cattle

# 8. Muhurta Effects at Birth (Slokas 38-41)

The text lists **5 inauspicious Muhurta lords** for birth:
> राक्षसो यातुधानश्च पितृसंज्ञो यमस्तथा । कालश्चेति पञ्चैते जन्मकालेऽशुभाः स्मृताः ॥

**BTR RULE:** If birth occurs during a Muhurta ruled by **Rakshasa, Yatudhana, Pitri, Yama, or Kala** → inauspicious.

**Combined judgment rule (Sloka 41):**
> पादे मुहूर्ते वेलायां हस्ते च पुरुषाकृतौ ।
> अनिष्टे शुभबाहुल्ये शुभबाहुल्ये शुभं फलम् ॥

**BTR RULE:** Among the four factors — (1) Nakshatra Pada, (2) Muhurta, (3) Velā/Time, (4) Purusha-akriti Chakra — if **auspicious factors dominate**, predict auspicious; if **inauspicious dominate**, predict inauspicious.

# 12. Raja Yogas & Raja Yoga Bhanga (Cancellation) — Slokas 1-5

**Raja Yoga Bhanga Rules:**

1. If **all planets are in enemy signs**, even Vargottama navamsha cannot give Raja Yoga. Many planets in **debilitation** also cancel Raja Yogas.

2. If **no planet aspects** either Lagna or Chandra, Raja Yogas are broken (Parashara's view).

3. If Sun is in own navamsha but **Chandra is aspected only by malefics** with no benefic aspect → native loses kingdom after gaining it.

4. If birth occurs during **Udaka-pata, Vyatipata, Bhukampa, or Ketu-udaya** (natural portents) → even with Raja Yoga, native becomes destitute.

5. If **Sun is in Tula at parama-nichamsha** → all Raja Yogas are destroyed.

# 16. Death Manner Determination (Niryana Adhyaya) — Slokas 25-48

## Death from Machine Pressure (Sloka 30)
> क्षीणेन्दुमन्दाक युते विलग्ने भूमीसुते सप्तमभावयाते ।
> विनाशनं यन्त्रनिपीडनेन भवेदवश्यं परिदेहि वाक्यम् ॥

**BTR RULE:** If **waning Moon** + **Saturn** + **Rahu** in Lagna, + **Mars** in 7th house → death from **machine/crushing**.

## Death in Filthy Place (Sloka 31)
> भौमे तुलायां च यमे च कर्के पापेक्षिते रविजालयस्थे ।
> विण्मूत्रिता कुलितप्रदेशेऽवश्यं विनाशः परिवेदितव्यः ॥

**BTR RULE:** Mars in Tula, Saturn in Karka (Cancer), Moon in Makara/Kumbha → death in water+filth mixed place.

## Death in Ravine/Mountain (Sloka 32)
> मेषादितश्च स्वगृहे क्रमेण क्षीणेन्दुमन्दाप्यनिषुत्रयुक्तौ ।
> दुर्गान्तराले च शिलोच्चये वा वनान्तराले भयः किल स्यात् ॥

**BTR RULE:** Waning Moon + Saturn in 4th, Mars in Lagna (or sequential) → death in ravine, mountain, or forest.

## Death from Tuberculosis/War (Sloka 33)
> बलोपपन्नावनिसूनुभौ पुनः क्षीणे विधौ रन्ध्रगतेऽतिपापयुक्ते ।
> गुद्यामयाद्वा कलिहेतुतो वा भवेदवश्यं मरणं रणाद्वा ॥

**BTR RULE:** Waning Moon + Saturn in 8th, aspected by strong Mars → death from TB (gudya amaya), warfare, or quarrels.

## Death from Birds/Wild Animals (Sloka 34)
> मित्रे कलत्रोपगते स भौमे मन्देऽष्टमस्थे च विधौ चतुर्थे ।
> विहङ्गमव्यापदहेतुना वा निर्याणमाहुरुनयः प्रराणाम् ॥

**BTR RULE:** Mars + Surya in 7th, Saturn in 8th, Moon in 4th → death from birds or wild animals (tiger, etc.)

## Death from Wall/Cliff Fall (Sloka 35)
> लग्नेऽहित्रिकोणेषु भानुभौमाकनिन्दुमिः ।
> त्रितीयो भवेन्मृत्युरित्तिपातभवोऽथवा ॥

**BTR RULE:** Surya, Mars, Saturn, Moon in Lagna, 8th, 5th, 9th respectively → death from wall/rock fall.

## Holy Death at Tirtha (Sloka 36)
> सौम्ये ग्रहे रन्ध्रगते शुभदृष्टियुक्ते धर्मेश्वरे वा शुभखेचरेन्द्रे ।
> तीर्थे मृतिः स्याद्यदि योगसम्यक् तीर्थे हि विष्णुस्मरणेन मृत्युः ॥

**BTR RULE:** Mercury in 8th with benefic aspect, OR 9th lord is benefic → death at a **Tirtha** (holy place), with remembrance of Vishnu.

## Sati (Self-immolation by widow) (Sloka 37)
> धर्मस्वामी धर्मगो धर्मसंस्थः सूर्यात्मजौ भौमतदाप्रिप्रवेशम् ।
> कुर्यात्पत्नी लग्नजामित्रनाथौ मित्रे स्यातां नान्यथा सद्भिरुक्तम् ॥

**BTR RULE:** If 9th lord, Surya, Mars are all in 9th house, AND Lagna lord and 7th lord are friendly → the widow enters fire (Sati). If not friendly, she does not.

# 17. Stri Jataka (Female Horoscopy)

## General Rule (Sloka 1)
> यज्जन्मकालादुदितं नराणां होराप्रवीणैः फलमेतदेव ।
> स्त्रीणां प्रकर्तव्यं खलु चेदयोग्यं तन्नायके तत्परिदेशितव्यम् ॥

**BTR RULE:** The same phala (results) calculated from birth time for men applies to women. Whatever is unfit for a woman should be attributed to her husband.

## Key House Significations for Women (Sloka 2)
> लग्ने शशांके च वरप्रविचिन्त्यं वपुः कलत्रे पतियोगमादौ ।
> सुतार्थभावे प्रसवोग्मयोस्तु वैधव्यमस्याः किल कालगेहे ॥

**BTR RULE:** For women's BTR: **Lagna + Chandra** → body/appearance; **7th house** → husband; **5th house** → children; **8th house** → widowhood.

## Same-sign Lagna-Chandra (Sloka 3)
> लग्ने च चन्द्रे समराशियाते कान्ता नितान्तं प्रकृतिस्थिता स्यात् ।
> सततं भूषासहिता सुखी सा सौम्येक्षिता तु यदि चारुशीला ॥

**BTR RULE:** If Lagna and Chandra are in the same sign (same Rashi) → woman is natural, always adorned, happy. If aspected by a benefic → supremely virtuous.

## Vishama Rasha Lagna-Chandra (Sloka 4)
> तयोः स्थितिश्चेद्विषमाख्यराशौ नारी नराकारपरा कुरूपा ।
> पापग्रहालोकनयोगयुक्ता चेत्कुलशीला गुणवर्जिता स्यात् ॥

**BTR RULE:** If Lagna and Chandra are in **odd (vishama) signs** → woman is masculine in form, ugly. If aspected by malefics → ill-charactered, devoid of virtues.

## Samvatsara Determination Formula (Sloka 1-2)

> शकाब्दा द्विकरैर्निघ्ना भूतन्द्राश्विभिरुच्यते ।
> शराग्निभिरिह भवेत् लब्धियुक्ता शकाब्दका ॥

**BTR INTEGRATION NOTES:**

1. **Cross-verification method:** For a reported birth time, check Samvatsara → Ayana → Ritu → Masa → Paksha → Tithi → Vaara → Nakshatra → Yoga → Karana — each gives physical/character traits that should match the native.

2. **Niryana-based verification:** The Chandra-based death timing (month, paksha, tithi, vaara, nakshatra) provides the strongest post-facto BTR verification — if death details are known, they should match the Janma Chandra's rashi predictions.

3. **Moola/Ashlesha sub-division:** The pada-level and time-of-day distinctions within these nakshatras are critical — many BTR errors arise from not accounting for these subdivisions.

4. **Abhuktamoola (2 ghatis):** A powerful BTR check — if birth is during this interval, specific life events (father avoidance for 8 years) should be observable.

5. **Dasha-based event matching:** The dasha lord's position and strength determine when specific events manifest — cross-reference with actual life events for BTR.
