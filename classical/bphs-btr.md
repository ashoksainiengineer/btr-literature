## Overview

This paper presents the **Pranapada (PP) method for Birth Time Rectification** as given in BPHS Chapter 4 (Shloka 8, Varanashi edition by Shri Ganesh Dutt Pathak). The author, Hemant Bhatt, emphasizes that BTR must be done **before reading any chart**, and that BTR **cannot be done on the basis of life incidents** — it must be based on mathematical calculations only.

**Key Philosophical Foundation:**
- **Ascendant = Soul (Atmaa)**
- **Pranapada = Breathing (Prana)**
- When both are synchronized, human birth comes into existence
- If birth time is not purified with reference to Pranapada, Gulika, or Chandra, the Ascendant is considered that of a **non-living being (sthaavar)**

## 2. Pranapada Calculation Formula (Step-by-Step)

```
## Rule: Pranapada Calculation — BPHS Method (4-Step)
**Source:** Birth Time Rectification — BPHS View
**URL:** https://pdfcoffee.com/birth-time-rectification-bphs-view-pdf-free.html
**Rule:** The PP calculation proceeds in four steps:

### Step [01] — Compute PP Rasi (Sign) Index
```
PP_Index = (Ghatis × 4) + floor(Pala ÷ 15)
PP_Rasi_Remainder = PP_Index mod 12
```
- Multiply birth ghatis by 4
- Divide pala (vighatis) by 15 — take the integer quotient
- Add both results
- Divide by 12 — the REMAINDER is the PP Rasi (count from a reference point)

### Step [02] — Compute Mean PP Degree
```
PP_Mean_Degree = (Pala_Remainder_from_Step1) × 2
```
- The REMAINDER from the pala ÷ 15 division is multiplied by 2
- This gives the degree of the **Madhyam (Mean) PP**
- Result: PP Rasi (from Step 1) + PP Mean Degree = **Mean PP**

### Step [03] — Compute Explicit (Spasht) PP
Apply the Sun correction based on sign type:
- **Chara (Movable)** — add Sun's longitude directly
- **Sthira (Fixed)** — add the 9th sign from Sun's longitude
- **Dwiswabhava (Dual)** — add the 5th sign from Sun's longitude

```
Spasht_PP = Mean_PP + Corrected_Sun_Longitude
```

### Step [04] — Verification
```
VALID if |Spasht_PP_degree - Ascendant_degree| ≈ 0 (within tolerance)
```
When the sign-degree of PP and Asc are equal, the birth time is said to be correct and valid for horoscope reading.

So at V1 level, the check is: Asc and PP in trine (1st, 5th, or 9th from each other).

## 5. Chara/Sthira/Dwiswabhava Sun Correction

```
## Rule: Sun Sign-Type Correction for Explicit PP
**Source:** Birth Time Rectification — BPHS View
**URL:** https://pdfcoffee.com/birth-time-rectification-bphs-view-pdf-free.html
**Rule:** The Sun's sign type determines how to correct the Mean PP into Explicit (Spasht) PP:

### Movable (Chara) Signs — Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn
```
Spasht_PP = Mean_PP + Sun_Longitude
```
Add the Sun's actual sign and degree directly to the Mean PP.

### Fixed (Sthira) Signs — Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius
```
Spasht_PP = Mean_PP + (Sun_Longitude + 9 signs)
```
Add the 9th sign from the Sun's position (with same degree) to the Mean PP.

### Dual (Dwiswabhava) Signs — Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces
```
Spasht_PP = Mean_PP + (Sun_Longitude + 5 signs)
```
Add the 5th sign from the Sun's position (with same degree) to the Mean PP.

### Why This Matters
The Sun represents the soul (Atma). The correction ensures Pranapada (breathing/life-force) is correctly aligned with the solar life-force based on the seasonal/qualitative nature of the Sun's placement at birth.
