# A — Samjna Tatva (Definitory Principles)

## Key Divisions Used in BTR

### The 12 Signs (A 1-5)

The parts of the body of Kalapurusha beginning with Mesha are:
1. Head, 2. Face, 3. Chest, 4. Heart, 5. Stomach, 6. Buttocks,
7. Abdomen, 8. Private parts, 9. Thighs, 10. Knees, 11. Shanks, 12. Feet.

**Lords from Mesha onwards:** Mars, Venus, Mercury, Moon, Sun, Mercury, Venus, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Saturn, Jupiter.

### Sign Classifications (A 6-9)

- **By nature:** Fierce & auspicious, male & female, from Mesha alternately
- **By movement:** Moveable (Chara), Fixed (Sthira), Dual (Dvisvabhava) — from Mesha respectively
- **By rising:** Sirshodaya (face-first): Simha, Kanya, Tula, Vrischika, Dhanus, Makara + Mithuna + Kumbha. Meena = Ubhayodaya (both). Rest = Prishtodaya (back-first)
- **Day/Night strength:** Sirshodaya (except Mithuna) strong by day; Prishtodaya strong by night

### Bhavas (Houses) (A 10)

1. Deha (Body), 2. Dhana (Wealth), 3. Sahaja (Courage/Brothers),
4. Matru (Mother), 5. Atmaja (Children), 6. Ari (Enemies),
7. Stri (Spouse), 8. Mriti (Death), 9. Dharma (Fortune),
10. Karma (Action), 11. Labha (Gains), 12. Vyaya (Losses)

### Vargas (Divisions) (A 11-20)

- **Shadvargas:** Rasi, Hora, Drekkana, Navamsa, Dwadasamsa, Trimsamsa
- **Saptavargas:** Shadvargas + Saptamamsa
- **Dasavargas:** Saptavargas + Dasamsa, Shodasamsa, Shashtyamsa

### Key House Groups (A 24-34)

| Group | Houses |
|-------|--------|
| **Kendra** | 1st, 4th, 7th, 10th |
| **Panaphara** | 2nd, 5th, 8th, 11th |
| **Apoklima** | 3rd, 6th, 9th, 12th |
| **Upachaya** | 3rd, 6th, 10th, 11th |
| **Trikona** | 5th, 9th |
| **Chaturasra** | 4th, 8th |
| **Maraka** | 2nd, 7th |
| **Trika** (evil) | 6th, 8th, 12th |
| **Dusthana** | 6th, 8th, 12th |

> **BTR Application:** The **2nd and 7th** are Maraka houses (death-inflicting). The **8th and 3rd** are Ayus houses (longevity). The **12th from these** are Maraka (death-determining). The 2nd = family/wealth; 7th = spouse — the two factors most cherished yet ultimately destructive.

### Rikshasandhis (A 33)

The **last portions (degrees)** of **Kataka, Vrischika and Meena** are known as Rikshasandhis — critical junction points.

### Karakas (Significators) of Bhavas (A 35)

| House | Karaka(s) |
|-------|-----------|
| 1st (Lagna) | Sun |
| 2nd | Jupiter |
| 3rd | Mars |

**Watery signs:** Kataka, Kumbha, Makara, Meena, Vrischika, Tula
**Dry signs:** The rest

> **BTR Application:** Watery signs in Lagna or Moon position influence birth near water, delivery conditions.

# B — Sutika Tatva (Conception & Birth) — CRITICAL for BTR

## I. Father's Position at Birth (B 1-5)

**B.1:** When the Lagna is not aspected by the Moon, the birth is out of the father's sight.

**B.2-3:** If the Sun (Pitrukaraka for day-births) occupies a fixed sign identical with the 8th, 9th, 11th or 12th, and Lagna is not aspected by Moon → father in own native place but birth out of his sight. If Sun occupies a moveable sign → birth when father is in a foreign country.

**B.4:** When Saturn rises (Lagna) or Mars sets (7th), father will be away at birth time. (Saturn=night Pitrukaraka)

**B.5:** Same effect if Moon is between Mercury and Venus.

> **BTR Application:** Use to verify father's location at birth — can help confirm Lagna and birth time.

## II. Birth Circumstances (B 6-19)

**B.6:** Malefic Rasi rising + Moon in Mars' decanate + benefics in 2nd & 11th → child born with navel cord coiled round body.

**B.7:** Sun in quadruped sign + other planets strong in dual signs/Navamsas → twins in one sheath.

**B.8:** Vrishabha, Mesha or Simha rising + Saturn or Mars occupying it → cord coiled round that body part.

**B.9:** Saturn & Mars in malefic signs (Mesha, Simha, Kumbha) occupying 5th, 9th or 7th from Sun → father in forced seclusion.

**B.10:** Full Moon in Kataka + Mercury in Lagna + Jupiter in 4th → birth in a boat.

**B.11:** Watery Lagna + full Moon in 7th → birth near water.

**B.12:** Lagna & Moon in watery signs → birth very near water.

**B.13:** Moon in watery sign or 4th house → same effect.

**B.14:** Moon in Lagna + Saturn in 12th aspected by malefic → birth in a gaol.

**B.15:** Kataka/Vrischika Lagna + Saturn occupying it aspected by Moon → delivery in a hollow/pit.

**B.16:** Saturn in watery Lagna aspected by Mercury/Sun/Moon → delivery in pleasure house/temple/sandy soil respectively.

**B.17:** Human Rasi rising + Saturn occupying it aspected by Mars → delivery in cemetery/burning place. If aspected by Venus+Moon → lovely place. Jupiter → sacred fire place. Sun → palace/temple/cow-house. Mercury → art gallery.

**B.18:** Moon in 5th/7th/9th from Mars & Saturn in same house → child abandoned by mother. If Moon aspected by Jupiter → long-lived.

**B.19:** Moon in Lagna aspected by malefic + Mars in 7th → child perishes abandoned by mother.

**B.20:** Moon in Lagna aspected by malefic + Saturn & Mars in 11th → same.

**B.21:** Delivery place determined by strength of planets representing relatives.

**B.22:** All benefics in depression → birth in exposed place (rampart, river bank, tree-filled area).

**B.23:** 3+ benefics in depression + Moon & Lagna not aspected → birth in a forest.

**B.24:** Moon in 4th, or conjoined/aspected by Saturn, or in watery sign → accouchement in darkness.

**B.25:** Sun strong + aspected by Mars → birth in defective house.

## III. Birth Details (B 28-41)

**B.28:** If Lagna is Sirshodaya → child emerges head-first. If Ubhayodaya → hands-first. If Prishtodaya → legs-first.

> **BTR Application:** The type of presentation at birth (head/feet/hands first) is determined solely by the rising sign's nature — a powerful BTR verification marker.

**B.29:** Malefics in 4th & 7th from Lagna or Moon → much maternal distress.

**B.30-31:** Delivery chamber direction & structure determined by strongest planet in Kendra.

**B.32:** Lagna-specific delivery chamber directions (by rising sign).

**B.33-36:** Bed position and leg defects indicated by 3rd, 6th, 9th, 12th houses.

**B.37-39:** Number of female attendants determined by planets between Lagna and Moon, with multipliers for retrogression/exaltation/depression.

**B.40:** Certain Lagnas produce incessantly crying child (Vrishabha, Mesha, Dhanus, Mithuna, Simha).

**B.41:** Moon in Lagna unaspected → delivery in lonely spot.

## IV. Delivery Difficulty & Balarishta (B 42-100)

**B.42:** Benefics in 10th & 4th → easy delivery. Malefics in 5th, 7th, 8th → difficult delivery.

**B.43:** Saturn + Mars + Sun in 4th or 10th → father died before birth.

**B.44:** Malefics in 1st, 6th, 7th, 8th → child & mother both die.

**B.45:** Eclipse + Moon with Saturn in Lagna + Mars in 8th → both die.

**B.51:** Sun in fixed sign + Saturn in Lagna + Mars in 8th → child dies quickly.

**B.54:** Moon in moveable sign/special Amsa without benefic aspect + malefics in 5th/9th → same.

# C-R — Prakirna Tatva (Event-Specific Verification Rules)

## C — First Viveka: General Bhava Analysis

**C.1:** A Bhava occupied/aspected by its lord or benefics → prosperity. Occupied/aspected by malefics → destruction. Both benefic & malefic → mixed effect.

**C.2:** Planet in depression/inimical Rasi destroys the Bhava.

**C.3:** Planet in own/friendly/exaltation sign promotes Bhava prosperity.

**C.4:** Malefics in 6th/8th/12th from any Bhava → that Bhava suffers.

**C.5:** Lord of any Bhava in 6th/8th/12th; or Bhava occupied by lord of 6th/8th/12th from it → Bhava totally destroyed.

**C.6:** Kendras, Konas & 2nd from any Bhava with benefics → growth. Malefics → destruction.

**C.7:** Strongest among: 1) lord of 8th from Bhava, 2) Sun, 3) Saturn, 4) lord of Gulika's sign, 5) lord of Gulika's Navamsa → in that planet's Dasa, loss occurs.

**C.8-23:** Body health determination from Lagna lord position, Vargas, aspects.

> **BTR Application:** C.7 provides a key timing rule — the strongest of 5 specific significators determines the Dasa of destruction/loss for any Bhava.

# T — Dasa Tatva (Dasa Timing System) — HIGH BTR Relevance

## Dasa Quality Classification (T 1-14)

**T.1:** A planet produces effects due to its position, Rasi, aspect, conjunction, means of living, etc. during its Dasa and Antardasa.

**T.2:** Sampurna (full) Dasa — planet very strong or in highest exaltation → increase of wealth & health.

**T.3:** Rikta (empty) Dasa — planet weak or in depression → loss.

**T.4:** Somewhat strong planet exalted → fully effective, brings wealth.

**T.5:** Anishta (unfavourable) Dasa — planet in extreme fall/depression Navamsa/inimical Navamsa → loss & ill-health.

**T.6:** Avarohini (descending) Dasa — planet proceeding toward depression → troubles. Same planet in friendly/exaltation/own Navamsa → middling.

**T.7:** Arohini (ascending) Dasa — planet proceeding toward exaltation → auspicious. Adhama (worst) if in depression/inimical Navamsa → little good.

**T.8:** Planet in exaltation/own/friend's house but depression/inimical Navamsa → first half good, latter mixed.

**T.9:** Planet in friend's/exaltation/own/Moolatrikona/Vargottama/own Varga → good results.

**T.10:** Planet eclipsed, in inimical/depression Navamsa → trouble & untoward results.

**T.11:** Avarohini + Adhama Dasa → trouble.

**T.12:** Arohini + Madhya Dasa → auspicious.

**T.13:** Planet conjoined/aspected by benefic, with more rays, in Kendra/Kona → auspicious Dasa. Otherwise → troublesome.

## Lagna Dasa Nature (T 14)

**T.14:** Lagna Dasa nature determined by decanate:
- Moveable sign: 1st decanate = Uttama, 2nd = Madhyama, 3rd = Adhama
- Dual sign: reversed
- Fixed sign: 1st = bad, 2nd = good, 3rd = mixed.

## Dasa Effects by Planet (T 15-20)

**T.15:** Retrograde planet's Dasa → fall in position, loss of honour.

**T.16:** Planet not in Dusthana → desires fulfilled.

**T.17:** Planet inimical to Lagna lord → aberration of mind, danger from sovereign.

**T.18:** Planet with Rahu → untoward effects.

## Antardasa Effects (T 38-48)

**T.38:** Quincunx planets in Dasa & Antara → great danger & fear.

**T.39:** Atmakaraka Bhukti → fall from position & loss.

**T.40:** Malefic Dasa + benefic Antara → trouble first, then good.

**T.41:** Benefic Dasa + benefic Antara → happiness.

**T.42:** Benefic Dasa + malefic Antara → happiness first, then fear.

**T.43:** At Dasa/Antardasa commencement → correctly calculate positions of planets & Lagna.
> **Note:** Extremely difficult to find exact moment of Dasa change. "Almanacs and Ephemeris will have to be depended upon and they do not give the required accuracy so as to enable one to come to the correct moment." (Translator's note, referencing Kesava Daivagna)

**T.44:** If Dasa lord is benefic or in Upachaya → Dasa best.

**T.45:** Lagna in friendly Varga of Dasanatha or benefic planet's Varga → auspicious Dasa.

**T.46:** Dasa/Antardasa beginning when Moon enters:
- Own house → honour, wealth, happiness
- Simha → above want
- Mars' house → quarrel & debt
- Mercury's house → learning & wealth
- Jupiter's house → agriculture happiness, honour, wealth
- Venus' house → bad wife, honour, fame, wealth
- Saturn's house → serving others

**T.47:** Strong Moon at Dasa entry not in Dusthana → very good Dasa.

**T.48:** Moon in Dasanatha's exaltation/friendly/own sign in Upachaya/Trikona/7th → good effects.

**T.49:** Moon's Rasi at Dasa commencement determines which Bhava the effects relate to.

**T.50:** Malefic with inimical planet + malefic Antara → calamity.

**T.51:** Strong planet in benefic Vargas aspected by benefic/friend at Dasa commencement → calamity warded off.

**T.52:** Dasa lord in transit in Upachaya/friendly/exaltation/Moolatrikona → full beneficial effects.
