# Laghu Parashari (उडुदायप्रदीपः / Ududaya Pradeepa)

**Author:** Maharshi Parashar (condensed by his disciple)
**Commentator:** Sri Sitaram Jha (Tattvartha Prakashika commentary in Sanskrit-Hindi)
**Source:** Internet Archive — https://archive.org/details/laghu-parashari

**Description:** Laghu Parashari is a condensed digest of the core Parashari principles. It focuses specifically on **Nakshatra Dasha** (Vimshottari) interpretation, yoga formation through Kendra-Trikona lord connections, and the application of temporal (tatkalika) vs. natural (naisargika) benefic/malefic classifications.

---

## Chapter 1: Samjna Adhyaya (Definitions & Fundamentals)

### 1.1 Author's Invocation (Mangalacharana)

> सिद्धान्तमोपनिषदं शुद्धान्तं परमेष्ठिनः ।  
> शोणाधरं महः किञ्चिद्वीणाधरमुपास्महे ॥ १॥

The author meditates upon Saraswati (the goddess of learning) who resides in the inner apartments of Brahma, whose lips are red like a lotus, who holds a veena.

### 1.2 Purpose of the Text

> वयं पाराशरीं होरामनुसृत्य यथामति ।  
> उडुदायप्रदीपाख्यं इमं देवविदां यदे ॥ २॥

Following the Parashari Hora according to our ability, we compose this text called "Ududaya Pradeepa" (Lamp of Nakshatra Dasha) for the delight of astrologers.

### 1.3 Core Principle: Vimshottari Dasha Only

> **Sloka 3:**  
> नक्षत्रदशाप्रकारेण विवृण्महे ।  
> विशोत्तरी चात्र ग्राह्या नाष्टोत्तरी मता ॥ ३॥

**Rule:** Results are declared according to Nakshatra Dasha. **Only Vimshottari Dasha** is to be used in this system. **Ashtottari Dasha is NOT accepted.**

### 1.4 Nakshatra Dasha Lord Sequence

> **Sloka (from commentary):**  
> कृत्तिकातः समारभ्य त्रिरावृत्य दशाधिपाः ।  
> भानु-भूमि-ज-राह्विन्दु-जीव-ज्ञ-शनि-शुक्र-भास्कराः ॥  
> दशा समाः क्रमादेषां षड् दश सप्त दशेन्दवः ।  
> नवभिः स्युर्नव चन्द्राद्यैर्नव चन्द्रा नवा नखाः ॥

Starting from **Krittika**, repeating 3 cycles of 9 nakshatras each, the dasha lords are:

| Planet | Dasha Years |
|--------|-------------|
| Sun (Ravi) | 6 |
| Moon (Chandra) | 10 |
| Mars (Mangala) | 7 |
| Rahu | 18 |
| Jupiter (Guru/Brihaspati) | 16 |
| Saturn (Shani) | 19 |
| Mercury (Budha) | 17 |
| Ketu | 7 |
| Venus (Shukra) | 20 |
| **Total** | **120** |

### 1.5 Computing Bhukta and Bhogya Dasha at Birth

> **Sloka:**  
> दशामानं भयात्तघ्नं भभोगेन हृतं फलम् ।  
> भुक्तं वर्षादिकं ज्ञेयं भोग्यं भोग्यवरात् तथा ॥

**Formula:**  
- **Bhukta (elapsed) = (Dasha years × Bhayat ghatis) ÷ Bhabhoga ghatis**  
- **Bhogya (balance) = Total Dasha years − Bhukta**  
- OR directly: **Bhogya = (Dasha years × Bhogya ghatis) ÷ Bhabhoga ghatis**

### 1.6 Antardasha Calculation

> **Sloka:**  
> दशाब्दाः स्वस्वमानेन हताः खार्कोद्धृताः फलम् ।  
> अन्तर्दशा भवेदेव प्रत्यन्तर-प्रभेदयः ॥

**Formula:**  
- **Antardasha = (Mahadasha years × Planet's dasha years) ÷ 120**  
- This yields years-months-days of each antardasha within a mahadasha.  
- The same process is repeated for pratyantardasha (sub-sub period), sookshma dasha, and prana dasha by successive division by 120.

> **Drutha (simplified) method:**  
> त्रिंशद् दशा दशामानं दिना ध्रुवकः स्मृतः ।  
> निघ्नं स्वस्वदशाब्देस्तद् भवेदन्तर्दशामितिः ॥

Multiply each planet's dasha years by 2 to get the **dhruva** (constant in days per year). Then multiply by the specific MD lord's years and divide by 30 to get months-days.

**Example given:** Sun dasha = 6 yrs. Dhruva = 6×2 = 18 days. Sun's antardasha in Sun MD = 18×6 = 108 days = 3 months 18 days.

### 1.7 Bhava Definitions (House Meanings)

The commentary provides detailed **etymological reasoning** for each house:

| House | Name | Rationale |
|-------|------|-----------|
| 1st | **Tanu** (Body) | The rising sign co-rises with the body; its rays fall on the body at birth |
| 2nd | **Dhana** (Wealth) | After birth, wealth is needed to sustain the body |
| 3rd | **Sahaja** (Courage/Siblings) | Wealth requires effort/valor; siblings assist in dividing/distributing wealth |
| 4th | **Sukha** (Happiness) | After effort, one seeks home, mother, and relatives for happiness |
| 5th | **Putra/Vidya** (Children/Learning) | For continuation/progeny and for Brahma-jnana (supreme knowledge) |
| 6th | **Ripu/Roga** (Enemies/Disease) | Before marriage one must be disease-free; enemies are like disease |
| 7th | **Jaya** (Spouse) | After health, desire for marriage arises |
| 8th | **Mrityu/Ayu** (Death/Longevity) | After marriage, concern about death and longevity arises |
| 9th | **Dharma/Bhagya** (Religion/Fortune) | Dharma practice increases longevity and removes death |
| 10th | **Karma/Rajya** (Career/Kingdom) | For dharma practice, one needs action, kingdom, or father's support |
| 11th | **Aya** (Gains) | For success in action, one needs resources/gains |
| 12th | **Vyaya** (Expenditure) | After gains, one must consider how to spend |

> **Summary verse:**  
> लग्नात् तनु धनं भ्राता सुख-पुत्र-रिपु-स्त्रियः ।  
> मृत्यु-धर्म-च कर्म-आय-व्ययो भावाः क्रमादमी ॥

### 1.8 Planetary Drishti (Special Aspects)

> **Sloka 5:**  
> पश्यन्ति सर्वे सप्तमं शनि-जीव-कुजाः पुनः ।  
> विशेषतः त्रिदश-त्रिकोण-चतुरष्टमान् ॥ ५॥

**Rule (Graha Drishti):**
- **All planets** aspect the **7th house** from themselves.
- **Saturn** additionally aspects **3rd and 10th** houses.
- **Jupiter** additionally aspects **5th and 9th** houses.
- **Mars** additionally aspects **4th and 8th** houses.
- Other planets give only the 7th house aspect (this is a key distinction from other texts).

> **Yukti (Rationale):**  
> सर्वे जायागृहं तस्माद् ग्रहाः पश्यन्ति सप्तमम् ।  
> (All planets aspect the 7th because protecting the wife protects dharma and progeny)  
>   
> तृतीयं विक्रमस्थानं दशमं राज्यमेव च ।  
> भृत्याधीनं यतस्तस्मात् शनिः पश्यति तद्द्वयम् ।  
> (Saturn as servant aspect 3rd=valor and 10th=kingdom)  
>   
> विद्यायाः पञ्चमं स्थानं धर्मस्य नवमं गृहम् ।  
> गुर्वधीनं यतस्तस्माद् गुरुः पश्यति तद्द्वयम् ।  
> (Jupiter as preceptor aspects 5th=learning and 9th=dharma)  
>   
> सुखस्थानं चतुर्थं स्यादायुःस्थानं तथाष्टमम् ।  
> सेनाध्यक्षं यतस्तस्मात् कुजः पश्यति तद्द्वयम् ।  
> (Mars as commander aspects 4th=comfort and 8th=longevity)

### 1.9 Natural Benefic/Malefic Classification

> **Sloka (from commentary):**  
> सूर्य-सौरि-कुजाः पापा गुरु-शुक्रौ शुभौ स्मृतौ ।  
> चन्द्र-बुधौ समौ, केतुस्तमः साहचर्यात् फलप्रदः ॥

| Nature | Planets |
|--------|---------|
| **Natural Malefic (Papa)** | Sun, Saturn, Mars |
| **Natural Benefic (Shubha)** | Jupiter, Venus |
| **Neutral (Sama)** | Moon, Mercury |
| **Shadow (Tamas)** | Ketu (results depend on association) |
| | Rahu (same as Ketu) |

**Note:** Moon is benefic when waxing (purṇimānta), malefic when waning (amāvasyānta). Mercury is neutral — becomes benefic with benefics, malefic with malefics.

### 1.10 Tatkalika (Temporary) Benefic/Malefic — Lordship Effects

This is a unique emphasis of Laghu Parashari. Two types of benefic/malefic exist:
1. **Svabhavika (Naisargika)** — Natural as above
2. **Tatkalika** — Temporary, based on house lordship

#### 1.10.1 Trikona Lordship (1st, 5th, 9th) — Benefic

> **Sloka 6:**  
> लग्न-त्रिकोणनेतारो ग्रहाः शुभफलप्रदाः ।  
> पतयस्त्रिषडायानां यदि पापफलप्रदाः ॥ ६॥

**Rule:**  
- Any planet ruling a **Trikona** house (1, 5, 9) becomes **benefic** in that capacity.  
  - Even natural malefics become benefic as trikona lords.  
  - Natural benefics become **exceptionally benefic** as trikona lords.  
- Any planet ruling a **Trishadaya** house (3, 6, 11) becomes **malefic** in that capacity.  
  - Even natural benefics become malefic as trishadaya lords.  
  - Natural malefics become **extremely malefic** as trishadaya lords.

> **Note:** Lagna is included in trikona (1, 5, 9) for this purpose, not in kendra (4, 7, 10).

> **Yukti (Rationale):**  
> विद्या-धर्मौ गृहे चेत् स्तस्तदा क्रूरोऽपि साधुताम् ।  
> व्रजेदतीव साधुत्वं साधुश्चेदिति दृश्यते ॥  
> (If learning and dharma are in the house, even a cruel person becomes good; a good person becomes extremely good)  
>   
> आयः पराक्रमौ वाऽपि शत्रुर्वाऽपि गृहे तदा ।  
> साधोरपि खलत्वं यादिति लोकेऽपि दृश्यते ॥  
> (If gains, valor, or enmity are in the house, even a good person develops cruelty)

#### 1.10.2 Kendra Lordship (4, 7, 10) — Neutralizing Effect

> **Sloka 7:**  
> न दिशन्ति शुभं नृणां सौम्याः केन्द्राधिपा यदि ।  
> क्रूराश्चेदशुभं दद्युः प्रबला उत्तरोत्तरम् ॥ ७॥

**Rule:**  
- **Benefics as Kendra lords** (4, 7, 10) **do NOT give benefic results** (they lose their natural beneficence).  
- **Malefics as Kendra lords** (4, 7, 10) **do NOT give malefic results** (they lose their natural maleficence).  
- All planets as kendra lords become **neutral** — they forget their nature.  
- **Strength order among Kendras:** Lagna(1) < 7th(7) < 4th(4) < 10th(10).  
  - 10th lord is strongest, then 4th, then 7th, then 1st (but 1st is already in trikona).

> **Yukti (Rationale):**  
> येषां गृहे सुखं नित्यं राज्यं वाऽपि वराङ्गना ।  
> विस्मरन्ति स्वभावं स्वं ते हि तत्र गतमानसाः ॥  
> (Those who have constant comfort, kingdom, or beautiful wife forget their nature)

**SUMMARY — Four Types of Lordship Effects:**

| Lordship | Result |
|----------|--------|
| **Trikona** (1, 5, 9) | **Benefic** (shubha-phalaprada) |
| **Trishadaya** (3, 6, 11) | **Malefic** (papa-phalaprada) |
| **Kendra** (4, 7, 10) | **Neutral** (forget nature) |
| **2, 8, 12** | **Mixed/Context-dependent** |

#### 1.10.3 Second and Twelfth Lords — Context-Dependent Results

> **Sloka 8:**  
> लग्नाद् व्यय-द्वितीयेशौ परेषां साहचर्यतः ।  
> स्थानान्तरानुगुण्येन भद्रतः फलदायकौ ॥ ८॥

**Rule:** The 2nd lord (Dhanesha) and 12th lord (Vyayesha) give results **based on:**
1. **Sahacarya** — Which planets they associate with
2. **Sthanantara** — Their other house lordship (if any)
3. Their own position

They do NOT give fixed benefic/malefic results by nature — their results are entirely contextual.

#### 1.10.4 Eighth Lord — Especially Malefic (With Exceptions)

> **Sloka 9:**  
> भाग्यव्ययाधिपत्येन रन्ध्रेशो न शुभप्रदः ।  
> स एव शुभसंधाता लग्नाधीशोऽपि चेत् स्वयम् ॥ ९॥

**Rule:**  
- The **8th lord (Randhresha)** is **malefic** because it rules the "expenditure of bhagya" (destruction of fortune).  
- **Exception:** If the 8th lord is also the **1st lord (Lagnesh)**, or also a **Trikona lord**, it becomes a **shubha-sandhata** (organizer of good results).  
- If the 8th lord rules only the 8th house (no other lordship), it is mildly malefic.

**Detailed analysis of Vyayadhipatya (expenditure lordship) effects on each bhava:**

| Bhava | Its Vyayadhipati (Expenditure Lord) | Effect |
|-------|-------------------------------------|--------|
| 1st (Body) | 2nd lord | Benefic (wealth expended for body's protection is good) |
| 2nd (Wealth) | 3rd lord | Malefic (expenditure on courage depletes wealth) |
| 3rd (Courage) | 4th lord | Malefic (expenditure on comfort reduces valor) |
| 4th (Comfort) | 5th lord | Mixed: benefic malefic does good, malefic does bad |
| 5th (Learning) | 6th lord | Benefic (spending on disease/education leads to learning) |
| 6th (Disease) | 7th lord | Malefic (spouse causes expenditure on health) |
| 7th (Spouse) | 8th lord | Malefic (death expenditure) |
| 8th (Longevity) | 9th lord | Benefic (dharma expenditure prolongs life) |
| 9th (Fortune) | 10th lord | Benefic (spending on karma/career supports fortune) |
| 10th (Career) | 11th lord | Benefic (gains from career expenditure) |
| 11th (Gains) | 12th lord | Mixed |
| 12th (Expenditure) | 1st lord | Benefic (spending with self-awareness) |

### 1.11 Avasthas (Planetary States) — from Other Texts

Though Laghu Parashari primarily relies on Nakshatra Dasha, the commentary mentions 9 avasthas recognized in other texts:

> दीप्तः स्वस्थः प्रमुदितः शान्तो दीनोऽतिदुःखितः ।  
> विकलः खलः कोपी नवधा खेचरो भवेत् ॥

| Avastha | Condition | Dasha Effect |
|---------|-----------|--------------|
| **Dipta** (Bright) | Exalted | Benefic |
| **Svastha** (Healthy) | Own or moolatrikona | Benefic |
| **Pramudita** (Delighted) | Friend's sign | Benefic |
| **Shanta** (Calm) | Neutral sign | Benefic |
| **Dina** (Poor) | Enemy's sign | Malefic |
| **Ati-duhkhita** (Distressed) | Debilitated | Malefic |
| **Vikala** (Afflicted) | Combust | Malefic |
| **Khala** (Wicked) | Malefic sign | Malefic |
| **Kopi** (Angry) | Aspected by malefics | Malefic |

The text notes that while avasthas are mentioned, **the Laghu Parashari system primarily relies on Nakshatra Dasha and lordship patterns** rather than avasthas.

### 1.12 Natural and Temporary Combined Assessment

> **Sloka (from commentary):**  
> तत्काले च निसर्गे च शुभः सोऽतिशुभप्रदः ।  
> उभयथापि पापो यः सोऽतिपापफलप्रदः ।  
> शुभश्चैकत्र चान्यत्र पापः स समतामियात् ।  
> इत्येवं तारतम्येन फलान्यूह्यानि सूरिभिः ॥

**Four-fold classification:**

| Natural (Naisargika) | Temporary (Tatkalika) | Overall Classification |
|---------------------|----------------------|-----------------------|
| Benefic | Benefic (trikona lord) | **Ati-shubha** (very benefic) |
| Malefic | Malefic (trishadaya lord) | **Ati-papa** (very malefic) |
| Mixed (one benefic, one malefic) | — | **Sama** (neutral) |
| One neutral, one malefic | — | Papa remains papa |
| One neutral, one benefic | — | Shubha remains shubha |

## Chapter 2: Yoga Adhyaya (Yoga Chapter)

### 2.1 Kendra-Trikona Yoga — Definition

> **Sloka 1:**  
> केन्द्र-त्रिकोणपतयः सम्बन्धेन परस्परम् ।  
> इतरैरप्रसक्ताश्चेद् विशेषफलदायकाः ॥ १॥

**Rule:**  
When **Kendra lords** (4, 7, 10) and **Trikona lords** (1, 5, 9) are **mutually connected** (by exchange, conjunction, or mutual aspect), AND are **not connected** to any other (non-kendra, non-trikona) house lord, they become **special benefic results-givers (yoga karakas)**.

- If also connected to other house lords (3, 6, 11 etc.), they give only **ordinary benefic results**.

### 2.2 Yoga Despite Defects

> **Sloka 2:**  
> केन्द्र-त्रिकोणनेतारौ दोषयुक्तावपि स्वयम् ।  
> सम्बन्धमात्राद् बलिनौ भवेतां योगकारकौ ॥ २॥

**Rule:**  
Even if the Kendra lord and Trikona lord are themselves **defective** (in debilitation, enemy sign, or fallen), their **mere mutual connection** makes them **Yoga Karakas** (bestowers of yoga results). They overcome their individual defects through their connection.

> **Yukti:**  
> सद्दोषोऽपि वरं विद्वान् न मूर्खो हितकारकः ।  
> दोषः सम्बन्धिवर्गेषु विदुषा गोप्यतेऽनिशम् ॥  
> (A learned person despite defects is better than a fool; a wise person hides defects among relatives)

### 2.3 Three Types of Valid Connection

> **Sloka 3:**  
> निवसेतां व्यत्ययेन तावुभौ धर्म-कर्मणोः ।  
> एकत्रान्यतरो वाऽपि वसेच्चेद् योगकारकौ ॥ ३॥

**Three valid connections that make Kendra-Trikona lords Yoga Karakas:**

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **1. Paraspara (Exchange)** | Kendra lord in Trikona house, Trikona lord in Kendra house |
| **2. Ekatra (Same position)** | Both lords in same house (Kendra or Trikona) |
| **3. Anyatara (One in other's house)** | Either Kendra lord in Trikona OR Trikona lord in Kendra |

**Effect strength:** Type 1 > Type 2 > Type 3

### 2.4 Best Yoga: 10th Lord with 5th/9th Lord

> **Sloka 4:**  
> त्रिकोणाधिपयोर्मध्ये सम्बन्धो येन केनचित् ।  
> केन्द्रनाथस्य बलिनो भवेद् यदि सुयोगकृत् ॥ ४॥

**Rule:**  
Among the kendra lords, the **10th lord (Karmesha)** is the strongest. If the 10th lord connects with either the **5th lord** or **9th lord**, it produces the **best yoga (Suyoga = Uttama Yoga)**.

**Hierarchy of yoga strength:**
1. 10th lord + 9th/5th lord = **Best (Suyoga)**
2. 7th lord + 9th/5th lord = **Medium**
3. 4th lord + 9th/5th lord = **Lower**
4. 1st lord + 9th/5th lord = **Lowest** (but 1st is already trikona)

### 2.5 When Yoga Phala Manifests

> **Sloka 5:**  
> दशास्वपि भवेद् योगः प्रायशो योगकारिणोः ।  
> दशाद्वयीमध्यगतस्तदयुक्शुभकारिणाम् ॥ ५॥

**Rule:**  
- Yoga results manifest primarily in the **dasha of the Yoga Karaka planets** themselves.  
- Even in the dasha of **benefics not connected** to the yoga karakas, if one yoga karaka's antardasha and the other's pratyantardasha occur simultaneously, yoga results manifest.  
- **Malefics, even if connected**, do not easily give yoga results in their dasha.

### 2.6 Malefics with Yoga Karaka Connection

> **Sloka 6:**  
> योगकारकसम्बन्धात् पापिनोऽपि ग्रहाः स्वतः ।  
> तत्तद्भुक्त्यनुसारेण दिशेयुर्योगजं फलम् ॥ ६॥

**Rule:**  
Even **natural malefics**, if connected to Yoga Karakas, give yoga results in their dasha periods (specifically in the antardasha of the yoga karaka).

> **Yukti:**  
> सम्बन्धे सति साधूनां खलोऽपि हितकारकः ।  
> (Even a wicked person becomes beneficial when associated with the virtuous)

### 2.7 Single Planet as Both Kendra and Trikona Lord

> **Sloka 7:**  
> केन्द्र-त्रिकोणाधिपत्यैकत्वे योगकारिता ।  
> अन्यत्रिकोणपतिना सम्बन्धो यदि किं परम् ॥ ७॥

**Rule:**  
If a **single planet** is lord of **both a Kendra and a Trikona** house, it is automatically a **Yoga Karaka**. If it additionally connects with **another Trikona lord**, this is the **highest yoga possible**.

> **Yukti:**  
> विद्वान् राज्याधिकारी चेत् प्रददाति प्रजासुखम् ।  
> धर्माधिकारिसम्बन्धो यदि तस्य विशेषतः ॥  
> (A learned person with royal authority gives happiness to subjects; if also connected to spiritual authority, it's exceptional)

### 2.8 Rahu/Ketu as Yoga Karakas

> **Sloka 8:**  
> यदि केन्द्र-त्रिकोणे वा निवसेतां तमोग्रहौ ।  
> नाथेनान्यतरेणापि सम्बन्धाद् योगकारकौ ॥ ८॥

**Rule:**  
**Rahu and Ketu** become Yoga Karakas if:
1. They are in a **Kendra** and connected to a **Trikona lord**, OR
2. They are in a **Trikona** and connected to a **Kendra lord**.

> **Yukti:**  
> अन्धा यथाऽत्र संसारे बलबुद्धियुता अपि ।  
> तादृङ्मार्गेण गच्छन्ति यादृङ्मार्गं प्रदर्शकः ॥  
> (Blind people, even if strong and intelligent, follow the path shown by their guide — similarly, Rahu/Ketu follow the results of planets they associate with)

### 2.9 Yoga Cancellation (Yoga Bhanga)

> **Sloka 9:**  
> धर्म-कर्माधिनेतारौ रन्ध्र-लाभाधिपौ यदि ।  
> तयोः सम्बन्धमात्रेण न योगं लभते नरः ॥ ९॥

**Rule:**  
If the **9th lord** is also the **8th lord** (randhra = 8th), OR the **10th lord** is also the **11th lord** (labha = 11th), then their mere mutual connection does **NOT** produce yoga results. In such cases, additional factors like exaltation or special strength are needed.

**Reasoning:** The 9th lord (dharma) also ruling the 8th (destruction), or the 10th lord (karma) also ruling the 11th (gains from expenditure of karma), cannot produce pure yoga through mere connection.

### Yoga Chapter Summary Table

| Condition | Result |
|-----------|--------|
| Kendra lord + Trikona lord connected, no other connections | **Special yoga** (Vishesha yoga) |
| Connected + also connected to other lords | **Ordinary benefic results** |
| Both defective (debilitation) but mutually connected | **Still yoga karaka** (defect overcome) |
| 10th lord + 5th/9th lord connected | **Best yoga (Suyoga)** |
| Single planet ruling both Kendra and Trikona | **Automatic yoga karaka** |
| Rahu/Ketu in Kendra/Trikona + lord connection | **Yoga karaka** |
| 9th lord = 8th lord OR 10th lord = 11th lord, mere connection | **Yoga cancelled** (bhanga) |

## Chapter 5: Shubha Dasha Phala Adhyaya (Benefic Dasha Results)

### 5.1 Dasha of Planets Joined with 5th/9th Lords

> **Sloka 8-10:**  
> पञ्चमेशस्य दशायान्तु धर्मेशान्तर्दशा यदा ।  
> अतीव शुभदा प्रोक्ता कालविद्भिर्मनीषिभिः ॥ ८॥

**Rule 8:** The dasha of the **5th lord** with the **antardasha of the 9th lord** is **extremely benefic**.

> पञ्चमेशेन युक्तस्य तपोधिपस्य दशा शुभा ।  
> मानेश्वरस्य सुखपस्य दशा तथा प्राहुरुदारचित्ताः ॥ ९॥

**Rule 9:** The dasha of a planet **joined with the 5th lord** gives **royalty and children** (if also connected to 9th lord). The dasha of the **4th lord joined with the 9th lord** is similarly benefic.

> पञ्चमेशेन युक्तस्य ग्रहस्य शुभदा दशा ।  
> तथा धर्मपयुक्तस्य दशा परमशोभना ॥ १०॥

**Rule 10:** Any planet **conjoined with the 5th lord** or **9th lord** has a benefic dasha.

### 5.2 Malefic vs. Benefic Conjunction Results

> पापयुक्तस्य खेटस्य दशा हानिकरी मता ।  
> शुभयुक्तस्य खेटस्य दशा द्रव्यप्रदा भवेत् ॥ ११॥

**Rule 11:**  
- A planet **conjoined with malefics** → **harmful dasha**  
- A planet **conjoined with benefics** → **wealth-giving dasha**

### 5.3 Lagnesh Dasha Results

> पञ्चमेश-तपोमेश-लग्नपस्य दशा राज्यप्रदायिनी ।  
> तथा धर्मयुक्तस्य लग्नपस्य दशा मता ॥ १२॥

**Rule 12:** Lagnesh conjoined with **5th lord** or **9th lord** → **royalty-bestowing dasha**.

### 5.4 Kendra-Trikona Connected Planet Dashas

> **Sloka 13-15:**  
> पञ्चमेशेन युक्तस्य तपोधिपस्य दशा भवेद् राज्यसुखार्थदात्री ।  
> तथैव मानाधिपसंयुतस्य सुतेश्वरस्यापि दशा शुभा स्यात् ॥ १३॥

**Rule 13:** Dasha of **9th lord joined with 5th lord** gives royalty and happiness.

> पञ्चमेशेन युक्तस्य मानेशस्य दशा शुभा ।  
> सुखेशसंहतस्यापि धर्मेशस्य दशा शुभा ॥ १४॥

**Rule 14:**  
- Dasha of **10th lord joined with 5th lord** → benefic.  
- Dasha of **9th lord joined with 4th lord** → benefic.

### 5.5 Specific Positional Combinations

> **Sloka 16-23:** Planetary positions producing benefic dasha:

| Position | Dasha Result |
|----------|-------------|
| 9th lord in 10th house | Benefic, gives happiness and prestige |

### 8.4 General Maraka Rules

> **Sloka 31:**  
> एतच्छास्त्रानुसारेण मारकान् निर्दिशेद् बुधः ।  
> चन्द्र-सूर्यौ विना सर्वे मारका मारकाधिपाः ॥

**Rule:** According to this system, **all planets except Moon and Sun** can act as Marakas if they are Marakesh (lord of 2nd or 7th house). A maraka planet kills in **its own dasha and its own antardasha**, not in others' antardasha. Benefic planets' antardasha does not cause death.
